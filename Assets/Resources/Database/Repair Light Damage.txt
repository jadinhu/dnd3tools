<b>Repair Light Damage</b>(Complete Arcane)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Construct touched
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
When laying your hand upon a construct that has at least 1 hit point remaining, you transmute its structure to repair the damage it has taken.
The spell repairs 1d8 points of damage +1 point per caster level (maximum +5).
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Eberron Campaign Setting  Tome and Blood: A Guidebook to Wizards and Sorcerers  Miniatures Handbook  Dragon Compendium  Spell Compendium
