<b>Sicken Evil</b>(Book of Exalted Deeds)

Necromancy [Good]
 <b>Level:</b>   Sanctified 5,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, Sacrifice
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Area:</b> 20-ft.-radius emanation
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You emanate a powerful aura that sickens evil creatures within the specified area.
Sacrifice: 1d4 points of Strength damage.
