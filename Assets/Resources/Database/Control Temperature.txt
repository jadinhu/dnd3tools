<b>Control Temperature</b>(Frostburn)

Transmutation [Cold, Fire]
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 3,  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 round
 <b>Range:</b> 20 ft./level
 <b>Area:</b> 20 cu. ft./level emanation, centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
You imbue an area with cold or fire energy, reducing or raising the temperature by one temperature band per five caster levels.
Effects of the new temperature on creatures and the environment are incurred immediately (see Cold Dangers, page 8 of this book, and Heat Dangers, page 303 of the Dungeon Master's Guide).
Arcane Material Component: A drop of mercury.
