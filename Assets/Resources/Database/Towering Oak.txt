<b>Towering Oak</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Illusion (Glamer)
 <b>Level:</b>  Ranger 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 3 rounds/level

 	
You draw on the oak's strength to improve your ability to intimidate your enemies.
You gain a competence bonus of +10 on your Intimidate checks.
