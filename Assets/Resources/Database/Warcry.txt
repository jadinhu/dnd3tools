<b>Warcry</b>(Book of Exalted Deeds)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Champion of Gwynharwyf 2,  Bard 3,  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 30 ft.
 <b>Effect:</b> Cone-shaped burst
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You unleash a tremendous battle cry that causes creatures
within a 30-foot cone to cower in fear for 1d4 rounds.
Any creature that fails its Fortitude save loses its Dexterity bonus to AC and can take no actions.
Foes gain a +2 bonus to hit cowering creatures.
