<b>Reduce Person, Greater</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 171)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 5,  Wizard 5,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 round
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One humanoid creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>The creature shrinks rapidly before your eyes.</b>
	
This spell functions like <b>reduce person</b> (PH 269), except as noted above.
