<b>Scorch</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 181)

Evocation [Fire]
 <b>Level:</b>  Wizard 2,  Sorcerer 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, AF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 30 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> 30-ft. line
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Reflex half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>A jet of roaring flame bursts from your outstretched hand, scorching any creature in its path.</b>
	
Scorch deals 1d8 points of damage per two caster levels, to a maximum of 5d8 points of damage, to each target it hits.
	
Focus: A red dragon's scale.
