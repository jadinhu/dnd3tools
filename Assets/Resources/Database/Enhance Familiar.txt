<b>Enhance Familiar</b>(Complete Arcane)

Universal
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Familiar touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
You infuse your familiar with vigor, granting it a +2 competence bonus on saves, attack rolls, and melee damage rolls, as well as a +2 dodge bonus to Armor Class.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium  Tome and Blood: A Guidebook to Wizards and Sorcerers
