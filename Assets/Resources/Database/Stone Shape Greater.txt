<b>Stone Shape, Greater</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 208)

Transmutation [Earth]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 5,  Druid 5,  Sorcerer 7,  Wizard 7,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Stone or stone objects touched, up to 10 cu. ft. + 10 cu. ft./ level
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
<b>You mutter the ancient words, and your hands glow yellow where they touch the stone. Where you touch, the stone seems as soft as clay.</b>
	
This spell functions like <b>stone shape</b> (PH 284), except that it affects a much larger quantity of stone.
