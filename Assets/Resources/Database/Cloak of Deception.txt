<b>Cloak of Deception</b>(Tome of Battle: The Book of Nine Swords, p. 76)

Shadow Hand (Boost)
 <b>Level:</b>  Swordsage 2,    
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> End of turn

 	
<b>The shadows around you seem to surge forward and engulf you. For a brief moment, they render you invisible.</b>
	
When you initiate this maneuver, you turn invisible, as the <b>greater invisibility</b> spell (PH 245). You remain invisible until the end of your current turn.
	
This maneuver is a supernatural ability.
