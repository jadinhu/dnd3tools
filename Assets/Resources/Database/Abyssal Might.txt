<b>Abyssal Might</b>(Book of Vile Darkness)

Conjuration (Summoning) [Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Blackguard 3,  Demonologist 3,  Cleric 4,  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, Demon
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> Caster
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes/level

 	
The caster summons evil energy from the Abyss and imbues himself with its might.
The caster gains a +2 enhancement bonus to Strength, Constitution, and Dexterity.
The caster's existing spell resistance improves by +2.
Material Component: The heart of a dwarf child.
