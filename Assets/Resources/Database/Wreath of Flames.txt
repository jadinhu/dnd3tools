<b>Wreath of Flames</b>(Dragon Magic)

Evocation [Fire]
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 3,  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,  Wu Jen 3 (fire),    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level

 	
A burst of white-hot fire springs into existence around you, swirling and twisting dangerously.
This spell surrounds you with white-hot flames.
At the end of each of your turns, any enemy adjacent to you or sharing your space takes 1d6 points of fire damage.
Your melee attacks deal an extra 1d6 points of fire damage.
Special: A red dragon capable of casting 5th-level sorcerer spells can cast this spell as a swift action.
