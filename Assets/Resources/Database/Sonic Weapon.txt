<b>Sonic Weapon</b>(Complete Adventurer)

Transmutation [Sonic]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 2,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,  Vigilante 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Weapon touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level (D)

 	
This spell temporarily sheathes a weapon in sonic energy.
While the spell is in effect, the affected weapon deals an extra 1d6 points of sonic damage with each successful attack.
The sonic energy does not harm the weapon's wielder.
Bows, crossbows, and slings that are affected by this spell bestow the sonic energy upon their ammunition.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
