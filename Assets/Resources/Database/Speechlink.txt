<b>Speechlink</b>(Complete Adventurer)

Divination
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 3,  Vigilante 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> You and one creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
You and a willing target can communicate verbally no matter how much distance you put between yourselves on the same plane.
Either participant can end the spell at any time.
Speechlink allows each to hear the other's vocalizations, whatever their volume.
It does not transfer other sounds from either participant's location.
This spell works on any creatures, including animals, but does not convey any special language comprehension abilities.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Magic of Faerun  Spell Compendium
