<b>Tetsubo of Earth</b>(Oriental Adventures)

Evocation [Earth]
 <b>Level:</b>  Shugenja 2 (Earth),    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Effect:</b> Tetsubolike beam
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
A 6-foot-long tetsubo formed of earth springs forth from your hands.
You wield this weapon with proficiency as if it were a greatclub.
Attacks with the tetsubo of earth are regular melee attacks.
The tetsubo deals 1d10 points of damage +1 point per two caster levels (maximum +10).
The tetsubo is considered a +1 weapon for purposes of overcoming damage reduction.
