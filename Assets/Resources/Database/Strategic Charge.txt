<b>Strategic Charge</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Blackguard 1,  Paladin 1 (Red Knight),    
<b>Components:</b>V, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level

 	
You gain the benefits of the Mobility feat, even if you do not meet the prerequisites.
You do not have to be charging to gain this benefit.
