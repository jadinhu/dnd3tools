<b>Cloak of Bravery</b>(Draconomicon)

Abjuration [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Paladin 2,  Cleric 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 60 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> 60-ft.-radius emanation centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
All allies within the area (including you) gain a morale bonus on saving throws against fear effects equal to your caster level (max +10).
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Complete Warrior
