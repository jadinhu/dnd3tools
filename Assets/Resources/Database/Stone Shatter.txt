<b>Stone Shatter</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Evocation [Sonic]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./level)
 <b>Area:</b> One stone object or creature
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (object) or Fortitude half (see text)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (object) or Yes

 	
You aim a single note of perfect pitch toward a stone object or creature, causing it to shatter.
The target object cannot weigh more than 2 pounds per caster level.
Anyone within 5 feet of an exploding object suffers 1 point of damage per caster level (maximum 15 points).
No saving throw applies to this effect.
Targeted against a stone creature (of any weight), stone shatter deals 1d6 points of damage per caster level (maximum 15d6), with a Fortitude save for half damage.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
