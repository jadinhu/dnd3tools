<b>Daze Monster</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 217)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 2,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,  Fatemaker 2,  Beguiler 2,  Sha'ir 2,  Death Master 2,  Jester 2,    
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> One living creature of 6 HD or less

 	
This spell functions like daze, but daze monster can affect any one living creature of any type.
Creatures of 7 or more HD are not affected.
