<b>Freedom</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 233)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 9,  Wizard 9,  Wu Jen 9,  Sha'ir 9,  Urban Druid 9,   Arborea 9,  Renewal 9,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) or see text
 <b>Target:</b> One creature
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
The subject is freed from spells and effects that restrict its movement, including binding, entangle, grappling, imprisonment, maze, paralysis, petrification, pinning, sleep, slow, stunning, temporal stasis, and web.
To free a creature from imprisonment or maze, you must know its name and background, and youmust cast this spell at the spot where it was entombed or banished into the maze.
