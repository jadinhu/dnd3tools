<b>Dirge</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Evocation [Sonic]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 6,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 full round
 <b>Range:</b> 50 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> All enemies within a 50-ft.-radius burst centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
Your song draws the energies of death and destruction down on your enemies.
Each round, any enemy in the area suffers 2 points of temporary Strength and Dexterity damage.
A successful saving throw only negates that round's affect; a new saving throw is required each round to avoid that round's damage.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
