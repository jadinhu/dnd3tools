<b>Kiss of the Toad</b>(Complete Arcane)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Wu Jen 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, AF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Living creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous; see text
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates; see text
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
Your touch becomes toxic, poisoning a creature you hit with a successful melee touch attack.
The poison deals 1d6 points of Constitution damage immediately and another 1d6 points of Constitution damage 1 minute later.
Each instance of damage can be negated with a successful Fortitude save.
Focus: A tattoo of a toad on your skin.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Oriental Adventures
