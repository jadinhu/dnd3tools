<b>Insignia of Blessing</b>(Races of Destiny)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 3,  Paladin 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, AF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 min./level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
This spell functions just like insignia of alarm except that the insignia wearers are also filled with courage.
Each insignia wearer gains a +1 morale bonus on attack rolls and on saving throws against fear effects.
