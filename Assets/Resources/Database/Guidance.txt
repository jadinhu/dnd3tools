<b>Guidance</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 238)

Divination
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 0,  Druid 0,  Adept 0,  Urban Druid 0,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute or until discharged
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
This spell imbues the subject with a touch of divine guidance.
The creature gets a +1 competence bonus on a single attack roll, saving throw, or skill check.
It must choose to use the bonus before making the roll to which it applies.
