<b>Guiding Light</b>(Miniatures Handbook)

Evocation [Light]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 1,  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> Creatures in a 5-ft.-radius burst
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 min./level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
Light shines over the affected area, illuminating all targets within it.
The light grants a +1 circumstance bonus on ranged attack rolls against any target in the area.
Spellcasters in battle sometimes use this spell to designate targets for archers.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
