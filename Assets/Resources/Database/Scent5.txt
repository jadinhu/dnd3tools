<b>Scent</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 180)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Ranger 1,  Druid 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
<b>At your touch, the creature inhales deeply, experiencing a new world of sensory input.</b>
	
You give the creature touched the scent ability (MM 314).
	
Material Component: A sprinkle of mustard and pepper.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Savage Species  Serpent Kingdoms  Complete Divine  Magic of Faerun
