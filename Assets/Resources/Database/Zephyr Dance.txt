<b>Zephyr Dance</b>(Tome of Battle: The Book of Nine Swords, p. 57)

Desert Wind (Counter)
 <b>Level:</b>  Swordsage 3 (One Desert Wind maneuver),    
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 immediate action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous; see text

 	
<b>You spin gracefully away from a foe?s attack, whirling like the desert zephyr racing across the sands. Your enemy?s blade barely touches your cloak as you nimbly dodge aside.</b>
	
You gain a +4 dodge bonus to AC against a single attack. You can choose to use this maneuver after an opponent resolves his attack but before he determines damage.</
