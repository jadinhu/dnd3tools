<b>Fins to feet</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 92)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 2,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Willing creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
<b>The naga's lower extremities thrash about and slowly transform into humanoid limbs.</b>
	
This spell transform tails, tentacles, or finned extremities into humanoid legs and feet. Creatures so affected lose any natural swim speed they possess, but gain a land speed instead. Transmuted Medium creatures have a base land speed of 30 feet, Small and smaller creatures have a base land speed of 20 feet, and Large or larger creatures have a base land speed of 40 feet.
	
The subject loses any natural attacks based on its tail or tentacles.
