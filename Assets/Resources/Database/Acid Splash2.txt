<b>Acid Splash</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 196)

Conjuration (Creation) [Acid]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 0,  Wizard 0,  Warmage 0,  Duskblade 0,  Sha'ir 0,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> One missile of acid
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
You fire a small orb of acid at the target.
	
You must succeed on a ranged touch attack to hit your target. The orb deals 1d3 points of acid damage.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Magic of Faerun
