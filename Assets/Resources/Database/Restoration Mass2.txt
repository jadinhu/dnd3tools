<b>Restoration, Mass</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 174)

Conjuration (Healing)
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 7,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 round
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature/level, no two of which are more than 30 ft. apart
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
<b>Hurling diamond dust into the air, you call out to your deity, and a sparkling aura briefly surrounds those you wish to heal.</b>
	
This spell functions like <b>restoration</b> (PH 272), except as noted above.
