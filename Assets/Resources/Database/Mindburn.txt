<b>Mindburn</b>(Magic of Eberron, p. 99)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/3 levels
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates; see text
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
This spell attacks a creature's mind, causing the target to lose its highest-level remaining spell, spell slot, or infusion slot each round. The target gets a save to avoid this effect each round. On its first successful save, the spell ends.
	
<b>Material Component:</b> An empty crystal vial.
