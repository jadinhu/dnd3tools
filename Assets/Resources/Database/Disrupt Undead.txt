<b>Disrupt Undead</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 223)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 0,  Wizard 0,  Warmage 0,  Duskblade 0,  Wu Jen 0,  Sha'ir 0,  Death Master 0,   Glory 1,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> Ray
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You direct a ray of positive energy.
You must make a ranged touch attack to hit, and if the ray hits an undead creature, it deals 1d6 points of damage to it.
