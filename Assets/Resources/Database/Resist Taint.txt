<b>Resist Taint</b>(Heroes of Horror)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 3,  Druid 3,  Paladin 3,  Ranger 3,  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 min./level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
This spell grants a +4 resistance bonus on saving throws made to resist acquiring taint.
