<b>Curse of Ill Fortune</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 56)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Blackguard 2,  Cleric 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> One living creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>Letting loose a stream of foul incantations, you curse the subject.</b>
	
You place a temporary curse upon the subject, giving it a -3 penalty on attack rolls, ability checks, and skill checks. <b>Curse of ill fortune</b> is negated by any spell that removes a <b>bestow curse</b> spell.
