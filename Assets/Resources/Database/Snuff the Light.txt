<b>Snuff the Light</b>(Drow of the Underdark)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Assassin 1,  Bard 1,  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One nonmagical light source
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous or 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
With a gesture, the light goes out.
Snuff the light extinguishes a single non-magical light source, such as a candle, torch, or lantern.
Against alchemical light sources, such as sunrods, you
suppress the light for 1 round per caster level.
