<b>Seek Eternal Rest</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 182)

Conjuration (Healing)
 <b>Level:</b>  Paladin 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level

 	
<b>You invoke the greater powers and are infused with a great, golden glow, empowering you with holy glory.</b>
	
You improve your ability to turn undead. For the purpose of turning or destroying undead, you are treated as a cleric of your paladin level.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Magic of Faerun
