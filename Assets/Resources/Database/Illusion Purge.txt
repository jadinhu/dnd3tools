<b>Illusion Purge</b>(Races of Eberron, p. 187)

Evocation
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 8,  Sorcerer 8,  Wizard 8,   Truth 7,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 5 ft./level
 <b>Area:</b> 5-ft.-radius/level emanation, centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level (D)

 	
You surround yourself with a powerful sphere of magical clarity that negates all illusions of 7th level or lower. Anything concealed or altered by illusion appears in its natural state while in the area.
