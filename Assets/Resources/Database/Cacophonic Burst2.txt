<b>Cacophonic Burst</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 41)

Evocation [Sonic]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 5,  Sorcerer 5,  Wizard 5,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level)
 <b>Area:</b> 20-ft.-radius burst
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Reflex half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>You complete the spell with a scream, and echoes of your cry travel outward and burst into ear-shattering sounds.</b>
	
You cause a burst of low, discordant noise to erupt at the chosen location. It deals 1d6 points of sonic damage per caster level (maximum 15d6) to all creatures within the area.
	
<b>Cacophonic burst</b> cannot penetrate the area of a <b>silence</b> spell.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Savage Species
