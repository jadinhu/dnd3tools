<b>Eradicate Earth</b>(Underdark)

Abjuration [Earth]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 40 feet
 <b>Area:</b> 40-ft.-radius burst, centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
A burst of force radiates from you, seismically damaging all earth creatures nearby.
Any creature with the earth subtype that is within the area of the spell takes 1d8 points of damage per caster level (maximum 10d8).
Creatures without the earth subtype are unaffected.
Material Component: A small stone.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
