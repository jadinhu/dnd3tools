<b>Sword of Conscience</b>(Book of Exalted Deeds)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Good, Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Champion of Gwynharwyf 4,  Cleric 4,  Exalted Arcanist 4,  Paladin 4,  Slayer of Domiel 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One evil creature
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous; see text
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
The target creature, which must be evil, is struck by pangs of conscience and remorse.
The creature instantaneously takes Wisdom and Charisma damage according to the magnitude of its evil.
The creature regains lost abilities normally; they do not automatically return when the spell's duration expires.
This effect is not language-dependent.
