<b>Teleport, Mass</b>(Tome and Blood: A Guidebook to Wizards and Sorcerers)

Transmutation [Teleportation]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 7,  Wizard 7,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 full round
 <b>Target:</b> Touched objects or touched willing creatures weighing up to 100 lb./level

 	
As teleport, except as noted above.
You do not have to teleport yourself when you cast a mass teleport spell.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Magic of Faerun
