<b>Bladeweave</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 31)

Illusion (Pattern)
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 2,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 Swift Action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> See text
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> See text

 	
<b>With a shout you unleash the power of the spell, causing every movement you make with your weapon to seem beautiful and full of flourish.</b>
	
Once per round, choose one target that you successfully attacked with a melee weapon. That creature must succeed on a Will save or be dazed for 1 round. Spell resistance applies to this effect.
