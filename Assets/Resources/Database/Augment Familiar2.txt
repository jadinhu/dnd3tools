<b>Augment Familiar</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 17)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels
 <b>Target:</b> Your familiar
 <b>Duration:</b> Concentration + 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
<b>Reaching out with magic, you empower your familiar, making it quicker and stronger.</b>
	
This spell grants your familiar a +4 enhancement bonus to Strength, Dexterity, and Constitution, damage reduction 5/magic, and a +2 resistance bonus on saving throws.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Complete Warrior
