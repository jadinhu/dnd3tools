<b>Blood Snow</b>(Frostburn)

Necromancy [Cold]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 2,  Druid 2,  Disciple of Thrym 2,  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Area:</b> 20-ft. square of snow/level
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You must cast this spell on a snow field.
You corrupt an area of fallen snow, imbuing it with negative energy.
Each round, a creature in contact with blood snow must succeed on a Fortitude save or take 1d2 points of Constitution drain.
In addition, anyone failing a saving throw is nauseated for the duration of the spell.
