<b>Skunk Scent</b>(The Forge of War, p. 116)

Conjuration (Creation)
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 1,  Ranger 1,    
<b>Components:</b>S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 30 ft.
 <b>Target:</b> One living creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude partial; see text
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
To employ this spell, you must make a successful ranged touch attack. If you hit, the target is sickened for the duration and cannot make use of the scent ability (if it possesses that ability). A successful Fortitude save reduces the duration to 1 round.
