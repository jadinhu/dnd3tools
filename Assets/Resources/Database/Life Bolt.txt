<b>Life Bolt</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Effect:</b> One ray/2 levels
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You draw forth some of your own life force to create a beam of positive energy that harms undead.
You must make a ranged touch attack to hit, and if it hits an undead creature, it deals 2d4 points of damage to it.
Creating the beam deals you 1 hit point of damage.
For every two levels of experience past 1st, you can create an additional ray, up to a maximum of five rays at 9th level.
If you shoot multiple rays, you can have them strike a single creature or several creatures.
You must designate targets before you roll spell resistance or damage.
All rays must be aimed at enemies that are all within 30 feet of each other.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
