<b>Fire Breath</b>(Oriental Adventures)

Evocation [Fire]
 <b>Level:</b>  Shugenja 5 (Fire),  Wu Jen 5 (Fire),   Flame 5,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level

 	
You gain the ability to breathe a gout of flame as a standard action, once per round for the duration of the spell.
The flame targets one creature within 15 feet of you, and you must succeed at a ranged touch attack to affect the target.
If you succeed, the target takes 1d8 points of fire damage per two caster levels (maximum 10d8).
