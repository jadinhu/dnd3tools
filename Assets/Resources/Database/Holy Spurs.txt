<b>Holy Spurs</b>(Miniatures Handbook)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Paladin 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> Your special mount
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
This spell increases your special mount's base land speed by 40 feet.
This adjustment is treated as an enhancement bonus.
