<b>Lower Spell Resistance</b>(Draconomicon)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 4,  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 round
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates (see text)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
This spell reduces the subject's spell resistance by 1 per caster level (maximum reduction 15).
This reduction can't lower a target's spell resistance below 0.
The target of the spell takes a penalty on its saving throw equal to your caster level.
