<b>Incite</b>(Miniatures Handbook)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 1,  Cleric 1,  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 min./level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
This spell incites the subject into acting.
The subject is not allowed to delay or to ready an action.
If the subject is currently delaying, it acts as soon as the spell is cast.
If the subject currently has an action readied, it may act as normal but can't later ready another action while the spell remains in effect.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
