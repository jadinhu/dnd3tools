<b>Boneblade</b>(Book of Vile Darkness)

Necromancy [Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Blackguard 2,  Cleric 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, AF, Undead
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Effect:</b> One bone that becomes a blade
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes/level

 	
The caster changes a bone at least 6 inches long into a longsword, short sword, or greatsword (caster's choice).
This weapon has a +1 enhancement bonus on attacks and damage for every five caster levels (at least +1, maximum +4).
Furthermore, this blade deals an extra +1d6 points of damage to living targets and an additional +1d6 points of damage to good-aligned targets.
This spell confers no proficiency with the blade, but the caster doesn't need to be the one wielding the blade for it to be effective.
Focus: A 6-inch-long bone.
