<b>Naturewatch</b>(Complete Divine)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 0,  Hathran 0,  Ranger 1,    
<b>Components:</b>S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> Quarter circle emanating from you to the extreme of the range
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 min./level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
This spell is identical to deathwatch, but only functions on animals and plants.
In addition, it also allows you to determine a variety of other mundane information about the animals and plants (whether or not the plants are dehydrated, the animals are malnourished, and so forth).
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Magic of Faerun  Spell Compendium
