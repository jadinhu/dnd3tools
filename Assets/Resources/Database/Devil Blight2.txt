<b>Devil Blight</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 64)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 3,  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> Living creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 1d6 rounds
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None or Fortitude partial; see text
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>You cry out the ancient words and make the prescribed motions, and your foe lets out a shriek of agony as it stumbles under the force of the spell.</b>
	
This spell deals 2d6 points of damage per round for the duration of the spell to creatures that have both the lawful and evil subtypes (such as a barghest or a devil). No saving throw is allowed against this damage.
	
If the target creature also possesses the baatezu subtype (<b>MM</b> 306), it must succeed on a Fortitude save or be stunned for the duration of the spell.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Planar Handbook
