<b>Sanctuary, Mass</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 179)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 5,   Balance 5,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature/level, no two of which are more than 30 ft. apart
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
<b>You cast this spell and you are infused witha clear, silvery aura. You touch each of your companions in turn, and they are infused with the aura as well.</b>
	
This spell functions like <b>sanctuary</b> (PH 274), except that it affects multiple creatures.
