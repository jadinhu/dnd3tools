<b>Waves of Exhaustion</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 301)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Dread Necromancer 6,  Sorcerer 7,  Wizard 7,  Warmage 7,  Sha'ir 7,  Death Master 7,  Blighter 8,   Hades 8,  Sloth 8,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 60 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> Cone-shaped burst
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> No
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
Waves of negative energy cause all living creatures in the spell's area to become exhausted.
This spell has no effect on a creature that is already exhausted.
