<b>Air Breathing</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 8)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 3,  Druid 3,  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>S, M, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Living creatures touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 2 hours/level; see text
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
<b>Tiny bubbles form on your hands as you complete the spell. As you touch each subject, its chest heaves and shudders, then begins to rhythmically expand and contract.</b>
	
The transmuted creatures can breathe air freely. Divide the duration evenly among all the creatures you touch. This spell does not make creatures unable to breathe water.
	
<b>Arcane Material Component</b>: A short reed or piece of straw.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Stormwrack  Champions of Ruin  Savage Species
