<b>Daggerspell Stance</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 57)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 2,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, AF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level

 	
<b>The daggers in your hands glow silver as you complete the spell. They feel as if they have become a part of your body.</b>
	
While this spell is in effect, if you make a full attack while holding a dagger in each hand, you gain a +2 insight bonus on attack rolls and damage rolls made with daggers in that round.
	
The magical energy that permeates your daggers while this spell is active allows you to deflect the magical energy of spells. When wielding two daggers and fighting defensively, you gain spell resistance equal to 5 + your caster level.
	
The spell focuses your concentration so that whn you devote all of your attention to defense, you can turn the force of most blows away from your body with your daggers. When wielding two daggers and using the total defense action, you gan both the spell resistances benefit described above and damage reduction 5/magic.
	
<b>Focus:</b> A pair of daggers.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Complete Adventurer
