<b>Ectoplasmic Decay</b>(Ghostwalk)

Necromancy [Ectomancy]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Area:</b> 20-ft.-radius spread
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You create a barely visible spread of energy that is harmful to ectoplasm but harmless to everything else.
All ectoplasmic creatures and objects in the area take 1d6 points of damage per caster level.
Material Component: A flat, sharpened disk made of resin.
