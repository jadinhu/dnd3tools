<b>Doom of the Seas</b>(Stormwrack)

Conjuration (Summoning) [Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 9,   Blackwater 9,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, <abbr title="Experience">XP, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 full round
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Effect:</b> One summoned creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
It is said that in the deepest recesses of blackwater caverns dwell krakens of immense power, horrific creatures touched by malevolent extraplanar powers.
Though this might certainly be true, it is likely that most experiences with such creatures comes as a result of this spell.
With this spell, you summon a fiendish kraken from the depths.

The doom of the seas obeys you for the duration of the spell.
