<b>Mountain Hammer</b>(Tome of Battle: The Book of Nine Swords, p. 84)

Stone Dragon (Strike)
 <b>Level:</b>  Crusader 2,  Swordsage 2,  Warblade 2,    
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Melee attack
 <b>Target:</b> One creature or unattended object

 	
<b>Like a falling avalanche, you strike with the weight and fury of the mountain.</b>
	
As part of this maneuver, you make a single melee attack. This attack deals an extra 2d6 points of damage and automatically overcomes damage reduction and hardness.
