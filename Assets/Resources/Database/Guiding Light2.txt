<b>Guiding Light</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 108)

Evocation [Light]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 1,  Cleric 1,  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> Creatures in a 5-ft.-radius burst
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>Across the murky plain, a beam of radiance shoots down, highlighting your foes.</b>
	
Bright lights shine at the targets. The lights grant a +2 circumstance bonus on ranged attack rolls against any highlighted target. If a creature gains total cover or total concealment relative to you, or if it leaves the area, the spell ends for that target.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Miniatures Handbook
