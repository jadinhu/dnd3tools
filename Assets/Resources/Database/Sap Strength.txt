<b>Sap Strength</b>(Book of Vile Darkness)

Enchantment [Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 2,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> One living creature
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
The caster drains the personal wellbeing from the subject, who becomes exhausted.
After 1 hour of complete rest, characters become fatigued rather than exhausted.
A fatigued character becomes exhausted again if she does something else that would normally cause fatigue.
After 8 hours of complete rest, fatigued characters are no longer fatigued.
Material Component: A long needle and a tiny glass bottle.
