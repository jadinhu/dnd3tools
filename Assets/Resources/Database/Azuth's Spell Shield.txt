<b>Azuth's Spell Shield</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 7 (Azuth),    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> Up to one creature/level, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level (see text)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
Each targeted creature gains spell resistance equal to 12 + caster level.
Divide the duration evenly among all the creatures you target.
In order to affect a creature who has spell resistance with a spell, a spellcaster must roll the creature's spell resistance or higher on 1d20 + caster level.
A creature with spell resistance may voluntarily lower it in order to accept a spell.
