<b>Hatchling's Flame</b>(Tome of Battle: The Book of Nine Swords, p. 54)

Desert Wind (Strike) [Fire]
 <b>Level:</b>  Swordsage 2 (One Desert Wind maneuver),    
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 30 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> Cone
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Reflex half

 	
<b>You focus a burning aura of energy at the end of your blade. It forms a seething sphere that, after a moment, bursts into a torrent of energy.</b>
	
The Desert Wind discipline teaches its students how to unleash their ki as burning energy. This maneuver allows you to create a wave of fire that sweeps over your enemies. When you initiate this maneuver, you blast forth a cone of flame that deals 2d6 points of fire damage.
This maneuver is a supernatural ability.
