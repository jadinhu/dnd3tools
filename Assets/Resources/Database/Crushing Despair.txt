<b>Crushing Despair</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 215)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 3,  Jester 3,  Dread Necromancer 3,  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,  Beguiler 4,  Wu Jen 4,  Sha'ir 4,  Death Master 4,   Hades 5,  Envy 5,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 30 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> Cone-shaped burst
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 min./level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
An invisible cone of despair causes great sadness in the subjects.
Each affected creature takes a -2 penalty on attack rolls, saving throws, ability checks, skill checks, and weapon damage rolls.
Crushing despair counters and dispels good hope.
Material Component: A vial of tears.
