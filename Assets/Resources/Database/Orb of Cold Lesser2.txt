<b>Orb of Cold, Lesser</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 151)

Conjuration (Creation) [Cold]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> One orb of cold
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
<b>Your hand takes on a blue tint and your fingers turn numb and unresponsive as you complete the spell. From your chilled palm flies an orb composed of blue ice.</b>
	
This spell functions like <b>lesser orb of acid</b>, except that it deals cold damage.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Complete Arcane
