<b>Regenerate Serious Wounds</b>(Masters of the Wild: A Guidebook to Barbarians, Druids, and Rangers)

Conjuration (Healing)
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 4,  Cleric 5,    
<b>Components:</b>

 	
This spell is the same as regenerate light wounds, except that it grants fast healing at the rate of 3 hit points per round.
