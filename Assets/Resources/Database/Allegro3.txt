<b>Allegro</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 9)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> 20 ft
 <b>Area:</b> 20-ft.-radius burst centered on you.
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
<b>With a quick wiggle of your fingers and a few arcane words, you release the feather in your hand to complete the spell. Suddenly, translucent blue motes burst outward from you and collect on yourself and your nearby allies before fading away.</b>
	
Each creature within the spell's area gains a 30-foot enhancement bonus to its land speed, up to a maximum of double the creature's land speed. Affected creatures retain these effects for the duration of the spell, even if they leave the original area.
	
Material Component: A tail feather from a bird of prey.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Complete Adventurer  Song and Silence: A Guidebook to Bards and Rogues
