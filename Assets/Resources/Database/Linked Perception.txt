<b>Linked Perception</b>(Player's Handbook II)

Divination
 <b>Level:</b>  Ranger 1,  Druid 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 20 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> 20-ft.-radius emanation centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
Your senses are muddied for a moment, but when they clear, your sight and hearing are improved.
This spell imparts to all allies in its area a shared awareness of their surroundings.
Each ally in the area (including yourself ) gains a +2 bonus on Spot and Listen checks per each ally in the area.
For example, if you and three allies are in the area, each of you gains a +6 bonus.
