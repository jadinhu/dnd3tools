<b>Anarchic Water</b>(Planar Handbook)

Transmutation [Chaotic]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 minute
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Flask of water touched
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (object)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (object)

 	
This transmutation imbues a flask (1 pint) of water with the energy of chaos, turning it into anarchic water (see page 75).
Material Component: 5 pounds of powdered iron and silver (worth 25 gp).
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
