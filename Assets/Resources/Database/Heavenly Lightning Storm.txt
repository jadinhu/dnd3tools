<b>Heavenly Lightning Storm</b>(Book of Exalted Deeds)

Evocation [Good]
 <b>Level:</b>  Exalted Arcanist 7,   Celestial 7,   
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Area:</b> 10-ft. wide to close range (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> Up to one creature per caster level

 	
As heavenly lightning, except it has a greater range, can strike more targets, and deals 5d6 points of special celestial electricity damage.
