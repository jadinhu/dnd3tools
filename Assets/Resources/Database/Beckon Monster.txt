<b>Beckon Monster</b>(Tome of Magic)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 3,  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, <abbr title="True Name">TN, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round; see text (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
This spell functions like beckon person, except that the effect is not restricted by creature type or size.
