<b>Repair Critical Damage</b>(Eberron Campaign Setting, p. 114)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,  Artificer 4,   Warforged 4,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 Standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Construct touched
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
As <b>repair light damage</b> , except <b>repair critical damage</b> repairs 4d8 points of damage + 1 point per caster level (maximum +20).
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Complete Arcane  Tome and Blood: A Guidebook to Wizards and Sorcerers  Miniatures Handbook  Dragon Compendium  Spell Compendium
