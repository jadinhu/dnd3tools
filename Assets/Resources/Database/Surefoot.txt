<b>Surefoot</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Ranger 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes/level

 	
Your steps are sure and true, even on the narrowest ledges.
You gain a +10 competence bonus on Balance checks.
