<b>Arcane Staff, Lesser</b>(Tower of High Sorcery)

Transmutation 
 <b>Level:</b> Anagakok 3, Beholder Mage 3, Chameleon 3, Deathwalker 3, Fleshcrafter 3, Sha-ir 3, Sorcerer 3, Wizard 3, Soul Reaper 3, Spellthief 3, Suel Arcanamach 3  
<b>Components:</b>V, S, F
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 10 minutes
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Non-magic quarterstaff touched
 <b>Duration:</b> Permanent until discharged (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Permanent until discharged (D)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (object)
 	
As arcane staff, except that you can store only up to your ability modifier (Intelligence if a wizard, Charisma if a sorcerer) in total spell levels within the quarterstaff, and the spells can be only 1st-level or 2nd-level.
