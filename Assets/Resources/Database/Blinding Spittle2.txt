You spit caustic saliva into your target's eyes with a successful ranged touch attack.
A -4 penalty applies to the attack roll.
The spittle blinds the target until it can wash its eyes with water or some other rinsing fluid.
This spell has no effect on creatures without eyes or creatures that don't depend on eyes for vision.
