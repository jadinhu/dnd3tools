<b>Warning Shout</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 236)

Transmutation [Sonic]
 <b>Level:</b>  Paladin 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 immediate action
 <b>Range:</b> 30 ft.
 <b>Target:</b> Allies within 30 feet
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
<b>You shout a few words imbued with power by your faith, and your next words ring out clear and far despite other noise or intervening barriers.</b>
	
All allies within range are no longer considered flat-footed, even if they have not yet acted in the current combat.
	
Anyone sleeping naturally (as opposed to magically induced sleep, such as from a sleep spell) within the area is woken by a warning shout.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Magic of Faerun
