<b>One Mind</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 149)

Divination
 <b>Level:</b>  Paladin 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level

 	
This spell functions like <b>lesser one mind</b>, except as noted here, and in addition it allows you and your special mount to predict each other's movements, giving you a significant edge in combat. You and your mount both gain a +2 bonus on attack rolls as long as you are mounted.
