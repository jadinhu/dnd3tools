<b>Bolt of Glory</b>(Defenders of the Faith: A Guidebook to Clerics and Paladins)

Evocation [Good]
 <b>Level:</b>   Glory 6,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> Ray
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
By casting this spell, you project a bolt of energy from the Positive Energy Plane against one creature.
You must make a successful ranged touch attack to strike your target.
