<b>Electric Jolt</b>(Magic of Faerun, p. 91)

Evocation [Electricity]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 0,  Wizard 0,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature or object
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You release a small stroke of electrical energy. You must make a ranged touch attack to deal damage to a target. The spell deals 1d3 points of electricity damage.
