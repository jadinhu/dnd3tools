<b>Fiendish Clarity</b>(Book of Vile Darkness)

Divination [Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 7,  Sorcerer 7,  Wizard 7,   Demonic 7,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> Caster
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes/level

 	
The caster develops the senses of a powerful fiend.
He has darkvision to a range of 60 feet.
The caster can see in magical darkness as if it were normal darkness.
He can see invisible creatures and objects as if he had a see invisibility spell cast on him.
The caster can detect good at will.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Fiendish Codex I: Hordes of the Abyss
