<b>Spikes</b>(Complete Divine)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 3,  Druid 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Wooden weapon touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
As brambles, except that the affected weapon gains a +2 enhancement bonus on its attacks, and its threat range is doubled.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium  Defenders of the Faith: A Guidebook to Clerics and Paladins
