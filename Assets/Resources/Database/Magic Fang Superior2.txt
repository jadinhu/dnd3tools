<b>Magic Fang, Superior</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 136)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 4,  Ranger 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level

 	
<b>You speak the old words of the incantation, and your hands glow with a yellow aura that flashes at the tips of your digits.</b>
	
Superior magic fang gives every natural weapon you possess an enhancement bonus on attack rolls and damage rolls equal to +1 per four caster levels (maximum +5 at 20th level).
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Draconomicon
