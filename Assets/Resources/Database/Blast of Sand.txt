<b>Blast of Sand</b>(Sandstorm)

Conjuration (Creation) [Earth]
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 4,  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,   Sand 4,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 30 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> Cone-shaped burst
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Reflex half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
Sand sprays from your outstretched palm with enough force to strip flesh from bone and scour surfaces.
The spell deals 1d6 points of damage per caster level (maximum 10d6).
Do not divide damage from sand scour by four when applying it to objects.
Material Component: A pinch of sand.
