<b>Sound Burst</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 281)

Evocation [Sonic]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 2,  Cleric 2,  Knight of the Chalice 2,  Jester 2,   Ocean 2,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, AF, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Area:</b> 10-ft.-radius spread
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude partial
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You blast an area with a tremendous cacophony.
	
Every creature in the area takes 1d8 points of sonic damage and must succeed on a Fortitude save to avoid being stunned for 1 round. Creatures that cannot hear are not stunned but are still damaged.
	
Arcane Focus: A musical instrument.
