<b>One With the Land</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 149)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 2,  Ranger 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level

 	
<b>You cast the spell and are bathed in a golden-green light from above. As it fades, you feel in touch with your natural surroundings.</b>
	
You forge a strong link with nature that gives you greater insight into your environment. You gain a +2 competence bonus on Handle Animal, Hide, Move Silently, Search, Survival, and wild empathy checks.
