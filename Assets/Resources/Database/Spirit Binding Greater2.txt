<b>Spirit Binding, Greater</b>(Oriental Adventures)

Conjuration (Calling) [see text]
 <b>Level:</b>  Wu Jen 8,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Target:</b> Up to 24 HD worth of spirits, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart when they appear

 	
As lesser spirit binding, except you may call a single spirit of up to 24 HD or a number of spirits of the same type whose HD total no more than 24.
Each spirit gets a saving throw, makes independent attempts to escape, and must be persuaded to aid you individually.
