<b>Trait Removal</b>(Serpent Kingdoms)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 5,  Wizard 5,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 hour
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> One touched living creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You temporarily remove one extraordinary or supernatural ability of your choice from the target creature.
This spell does not work on constructs, oozes, or undead.
Material Component: A pinch of ash.
