<b>Steel Wind</b>(Tome of Battle: The Book of Nine Swords, p. 69)

Iron Heart (Strike)
 <b>Level:</b>  Warblade 1,    
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Melee attack
 <b>Target:</b> Two creatures

 	
<b>You swing your weapon in a broad, deadly arc, striking two foes with a single, mighty blow.</b>
	
Through a combination of sheer power and unmatched talent, you make an attack that injures multiple opponents. As you initiate this strike, you make two melee attacks, each against a different foe that you threaten. Resolve each attack separately.
