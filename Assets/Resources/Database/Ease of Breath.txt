<b>Ease of Breath</b>(Frostburn)

Necromancy [Cold]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 1,  Druid 1,  Ranger 1,  Disciple of Thrym 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
You grant the target the ability to survive in thin air, conferring a +20 inherent bonus on Fortitude saves to resist altitude sickness as well as saving throws to resist becoming fatigued due to altitude or thin air.
