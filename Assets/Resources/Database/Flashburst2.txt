<b>Flashburst</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 95)

Evocation [Fire]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level)
 <b>Area:</b> 20-ft.-radius burst
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous; see text
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will partial; see text
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>A blinding flash of light follows the casting of this spell.</b>
	
<b>Flashburst</b> creates a blinding, dazzling flash of light. Sighted creatures within the area are blinded for 2d8 rounds. A successful Will save negates the blindness. Creatures in the area are dazzled for 1 round even if their save was successful. Creatures outside the area, but within 120 feet of the burst, can be blinded for 2d8 rounds if they have line of sight to the burst (Will negates). Creatures outside the burst area are not dazzled.
	
<b>Arcane Material Component:</b> A pinch of sulfur or phosphorus.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Forgotten Realms Campaign Setting
