<b>Speed of the Wind</b>(Masters of the Wild: A Guidebook to Barbarians, Druids, and Rangers)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Living creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
With this spell, you can grant the ephemeral quickness of a sudden breeze.
The subject gains a +4 enhancement bonus to Dexterity and
a -2 enhancement penalty to Constitution.
