<b>Surefoot</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 216)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Ranger 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes/level

 	
<b>You speak a few words and motion toward your feet, which begin to glow with a green hue.</b>
	
Your steps are sure and true, even on the narrowest ledges. You gain a +10 competence bonus on Balance, Climb, Jump, and Tumble checks. In addition, you do not lose your Dexterity bonus to AC when balancing or climbing.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Magic of Faerun
