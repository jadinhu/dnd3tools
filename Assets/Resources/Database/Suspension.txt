<b>Suspension</b>(Shining South)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Any nonliving object up to 1,000 lb./level
 <b>Duration:</b> 1d4 days + 1 day/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
This spell functions like levitate, except as noted above and as follows.
You can mentally direct the object to move up or down as much as 45 feet each round for the duration of the spell; doing so is a move action.
When you cease concentration, the object remains in place for the duration of the spell.
Material Component: A small leather loop suspended from a bent wire of gold.
