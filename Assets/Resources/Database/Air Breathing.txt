<b>Air Breathing</b>(Champions of Ruin)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 3,  Druid 3,  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>S, M, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Duration:</b> 2 hours/level; see text
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
The transmuted creatures can breathe air freely.
Divide the duration evenly among all the creatures you touch.
The spell does not make creatures unable to breathe water.
Air breathing dispels and counters water breathing.
Arcane Material Component: A short reed or piece of straw.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Stormwrack  Savage Species  Spell Compendium
