<b>Contagion, Mass</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 51)

Necromancy [Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 5,  Druid 5,  Sorcerer 6,  Wizard 6,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Area:</b> 20-ft.-radius spread
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>Forcing a hacking cough, you complete the spell. In the area you've designated, you see swirling red and black mist rise up, accompanied by the distant sound of your echoing cough.</b>
	
This spell functions like <b>contagion</b> (<b>PH</b> 213), but all creatures within the area are infected.
