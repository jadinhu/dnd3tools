<b>Summon Monster VIII</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 287)

Conjuration (Summoning) [see text for summon monster I]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 8,  Sorcerer 8,  Wizard 8,  Nar Demonbinder 8,  Wu Jen 8,  Sha'ir 8,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, AF, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 round
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> One or more summoned creatures, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
This spell functions like <b>summon monster I</b>, except that you can summon one creature from the 8th-level list, 1d3 creatures of the same kind fromthe 7th-level list, or 1d4+1 creatures of the same kind from a lower-level list.
	
Arcane Focus: A tiny bag and a small (not necessarily lit) candle.
