<b>Instant Locksmith</b>(Complete Adventurer)

Divination
 <b>Level:</b>  Assassin 1,  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round

 	
You can make one Disable Device check or one Open Lock check in this round as a free action.
You gain a +2 insight bonus on the check.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
