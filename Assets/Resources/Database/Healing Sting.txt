<b>Healing Sting</b>(Magic of Faerun, p. 100)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> You and one living creature
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You inflict 1d6 points of damage +1 point/level damage to a living creature and gain an equal amount of hit points. The <b>healing sting</b> cannot give you more hit points than your normal total. Excess hit points are lost. 
	
Material Component: Five dried wasp bodies.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
