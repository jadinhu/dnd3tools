<b>Aura of Tyranny</b>(Tome of Battle: The Book of Nine Swords, p. 57)

Devoted Spirit (Stance) [Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Crusader 6 (Two Devoted Spirit maneuvers),    
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> Stance

 	
<b>A sickly gray radiance surrounds you, sapping the strength of your allies and funneling it to you.</b>
	
While you are in this stance, you drain hit points from your allies. At the end of your turn, you can choose to deal 2 points of damage to each willing ally within 10 feet. For each ally who takes this damage, you heal 1 point of damage.
