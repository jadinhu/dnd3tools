<b>Withering Palm</b>(Eberron Campaign Setting, p. 117)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>   Death 7,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Living creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
A touch from your hand causes the subject's body to weaken and wither. 
	
With a successful melee touch attack, you deal 1 point of Strength damage and 1 point of Constitution damage per two caster levels to the target. If you score a critical hit, the damage from the hit is ability drain instead of ability damage.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Complete Arcane  Oriental Adventures  Spell Compendium
