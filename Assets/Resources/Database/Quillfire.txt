<b>Quillfire</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round

 	
Your hand sprouts poisonous quills that you can use in melee or as a ranged attack.
The quills inflict 1d8 points of damage +1 point/2 levels (maximum +5).
They may be thrown as a group using a single ranged attack roll (range increment 10 feet).
The quills are poisonous (injury DC 18, 1d6 Str/1d6 Str).
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
