<b>Mantle of Chaos</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 137)

Abjuration [Chaos]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 3,   Limbo 3,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>You invoke the powers of chaos, and a flickering yellow field of anarchic energy surrounds you.</b>
	
The power of this spell grants you spell resistance equal to 12 + your caster level against spells with the lawful descriptor.
