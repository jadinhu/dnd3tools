<b>Dragonmark Shield</b>(Dragonmarked, p. 150)

Abjuration [Force]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, Dragonmark
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift or immediate action; see text
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round; see text

 	
<b>A translucent shield of turquoise energy shaped like your dragonmark springs into existence to protect you.</b>
	
The <b>dragonmark shield</b> hovers in front of you and moves with you. It negates <b>magic missile</b> attacks directed at you. In addition, it provides a shield bonus to AC and a bonus on Reflex saves based on the strength of your dragonmark.

The shield bonus to AC applies against incorporeal touch attacks, since the <b>dragonmark shield</b> is a force effect. The <b>dragonmark shield</b> has no armor check penalty or arcane spell failure chance.
	
If you use an immediate action to invoke the <b>dragonmark shield</b>, it disappears at the end of your next turn.
	
<b>Dragonmark:</b> Any true dragonmark.
