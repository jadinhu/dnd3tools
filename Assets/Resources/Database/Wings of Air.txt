<b>Wings of Air</b>(Draconomicon)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Winged creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No (harmless)
