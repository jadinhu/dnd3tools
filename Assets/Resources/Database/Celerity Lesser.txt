<b>Celerity, Lesser</b>(Player's Handbook II)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 2,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 immediate action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous

 	
You borrow a slice of time from the future, pulling it into the present so that you can act.
Upon casting this spell, you can immediately take a move action.
After performing this action, you are dazed until the end of your next turn.
