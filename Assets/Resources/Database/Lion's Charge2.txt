<b>Lion's Charge</b>(Savage Species)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 1,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
You provide the subject with the powerful charging ability of the lion.
When the subject charges, he can make a full attack in the same round.
Material Component: A hair from a lion's mane.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium  Miniatures Handbook
