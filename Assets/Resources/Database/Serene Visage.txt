<b>Serene Visage</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 182)

Illusion (Glamer)
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 1,  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level

 	
<b>By casting this spell, you imbue yourself with an ephemeral quality that induces others to trust what you say.</b>
	
Simple illusions aid your attempts to be persuasive. You gain an insight bonus equal to one-half your caster level (maximum +10) on Bluff checks.
