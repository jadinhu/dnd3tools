<b>Ectoplasmic Feedback</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 78)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> See text

 	
<b>A shimmering sheath of energy surrounds you, promising harm to incorporeal creatures that touch you.</b>
	
Any creature that hits you with an incorporeal touch attack takes 1d6 points of force damage +1 point per caster level (maximum +10). If the attacker has spell resistance, it applies to this effect.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Libris Mortis: The Book of the Dead
