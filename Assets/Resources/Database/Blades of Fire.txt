<b>Blades of Fire</b>(Complete Arcane)

Conjuration (Creation) [Fire]
 <b>Level:</b>  Ranger 2,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,  Warmage 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Up to two melee weapons you are wielding
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
Flames sheathe your melee weapons, harming neither you nor the weapons but possibly burning your opponents.
Your melee weapons each deal an extra 1d6 points of fire damage.
This damage stacks with any energy damage your weapons already deal.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium  Miniatures Handbook
