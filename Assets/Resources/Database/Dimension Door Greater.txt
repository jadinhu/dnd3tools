<b>Dimension Door, Greater</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 64)

Conjuration [Teleportation]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 5,  Wizard 5,    
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> You and touched objects or other touched willing creatures
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/2 levels
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None and Will negates (object)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No and Yes (object)

 	
<b>Your flesh gives an involuntary shudder as you will yourself elsewhere. You vanish, reappearing some distance away.</b>
	
This spell functions like <b>dimension door</b> (<b>PH</b> 221), except as noted above and that you can transfer the targets once per round, up to a distance of 25 feet + 5 feet per two levels, as a move action that does not provoke attacks of opportunity.
