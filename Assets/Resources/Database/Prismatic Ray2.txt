<b>Prismatic Ray</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 162)

Evocation
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 5,  Wizard 5,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 Standard Action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Effect:</b> Ray
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> See text
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>You feel within you, as the spell nears completion, various energies locked in a battle within your body. As nausea threatens to overcome you, you croak out the final syllables of arcane power and point your hand. A single beam of brilliantly colored light then shoots from your outstretched hand.</b>
	
You must succeed on a ranged touch attack with the ray to strike a target. On a successful attack, a creature with 6 Hit Dice or fewer is blinded for 2d4 rounds by the prismatic ray in addition to suffering a randomly determined effect:
