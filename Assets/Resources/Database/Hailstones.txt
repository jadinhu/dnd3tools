<b>Hailstones</b>(Savage Species)

Evocation [Cold]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Effect:</b> One frigid globe/five levels
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> See text
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You create frigid globes that strike your enemies.
You must succeed on a ranged touch attack roll to hit.
The hailstone deals 5d6 points of cold damage (Reflex save half ).
If you create extra frigid globes (at 10th level or higher, to a maximum of four globes at 20th level), all globes must be aimed at enemies that are all within 30 feet of each other.
Material Component: A handful of crystal globes.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
