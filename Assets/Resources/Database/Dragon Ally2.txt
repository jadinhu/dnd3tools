<b>Dragon Ally</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 72)

Conjuration (Calling)
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 7,  Wizard 7,   Dragon 7,   
<b>Components:</b>V, <abbr title="Experience">XP, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 10 minutes
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> One called dragon of 18 HD or less
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
This spell functions like <b>lesser dragon ally</b>, except you can call a single dragon of up to 18 HD.
	
<b>XP Cost:</b> 250 XP
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Draconomicon
