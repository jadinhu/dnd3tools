<b>Waste Strider</b>(Sandstorm)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 1,  Ranger 1,   Sand 1,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> One touched creature/level
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
The subject can move through desert environments without hindrance, ignoring movement penalties and penalties on skill checks for all types of desert and waste terrain.
This spell does not grant a subject immunity to other ill effects of waste environments, such as heat and sun.
