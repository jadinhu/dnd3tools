<b>Fortify Cold Creatures</b>(Frostburn)

Transmutation [Cold]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Area:</b> One creature/level, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
Creatures with the cold subtype gain a +1 sacred bonus on all attack rolls and on saving throws against fire effects.
