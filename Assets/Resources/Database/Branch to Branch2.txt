<b>Branch to Branch</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Ranger 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level

 	
You gain a +10 competence bonus on Climb checks made in trees.
You can brachiate (swing via branches and vines) at your normal speed.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Complete Adventurer
