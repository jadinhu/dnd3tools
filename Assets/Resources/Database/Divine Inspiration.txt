<b>Divine Inspiration</b>(Book of Exalted Deeds)

Divination
 <b>Level:</b>   Sanctified 1,   
<b>Components:</b>Sacrifice
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> One creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1d4 rounds
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
This spell helps to tip the momentum of combat in the favor of good, granting limited precognitive ability that enables the spell's recipient to circumvent the defenses of evil opponents.
The target of the spell gains a +3 sacred bonus on all attack rolls made against evil creatures.
This bonus does not apply to attacks made against nonevil creatures.
Sacrifice: 1d2 points of Strength damage.
