<b>Summon Nature's Ally IX</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 289)

Conjuration (Summoning) [see text]
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 9,   Fey 9,  Gnome 9,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 round
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> One or more creatures, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
This spell functions like <b>summon nature's ally I</b>, except that you can summon one 9th-level creature, 1d3 8th-level creatures of the same kind, or 1d4+1 lower-level creatures of the same kind.
	
When you use a summoning spell to summon an air, chaotic, earth, evil, fire, good, lawful, or water creature, it is a spell of that type.
	
<b>Special: When casting <b>summon nature's ally IX</b> as a Gnome Domain spell, only Earth elementals or animals may be summoned.</b>
