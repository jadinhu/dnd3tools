<b>Mystic Aegis</b>(Player's Handbook II)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 immediate action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous

 	
A mantle of scintillating, multicolored light appears around you to block the spell, then fades away.
You cast mystic aegis immediately when you are targeted by a hostile spell.
You gain spell resistance equal to 12 + your caster level against that spell.
