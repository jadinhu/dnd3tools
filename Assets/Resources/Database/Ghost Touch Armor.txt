<b>Ghost Touch Armor</b>(Ghostwalk)

Transmutation [Ectomancy]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bone Collector 2,  Cleric 2,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Armor of creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
The subject's armor gains the ghost touch property.
Material Component: A shield made of resin.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium  Libris Mortis: The Book of the Dead
