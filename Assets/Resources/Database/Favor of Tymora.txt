<b>Favor of Tymora</b>(Champions of Valor)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>   Initiate of tymora 2,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, AF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 immediate action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/3 levels
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
This spell creates a glistening aura of golden magical energy around the target creature.
The creature gains a +2 luck bonus to AC.
Focus: A gold piece.
