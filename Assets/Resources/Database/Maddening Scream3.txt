<b>Maddening Scream</b>(Deities and Demigods)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 8,  Wizard 8,   Madness 8,   
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> One action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Living creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1d4+1 rounds
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
The subject cannot keep himself from screaming, gibbering, and leaping about as though completely mad.
This spell makes it impossible for the victim to do anything other than race about caterwauling.
The effect worsens the Armor Class of the creature by 4, makes Reflex saving throws impossible except on a roll of 20, and makes it impossible to use a shield.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Eberron Campaign Setting  Spell Compendium  Complete Divine  Defenders of the Faith: A Guidebook to Clerics and Paladins  Lords of Madness
