<b>Bolts of Bedevilment</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 37)

Enchantment [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 5,   Madness 5,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 Standard Action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./ level)
 <b>Effect:</b> Ray
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>With a strangled peal of laughter to complete the spell, you point your hand at your enemy and fire a black beam from your finger.</b>
	
This spell grants you the ability to make one ray attack per round. You must succeed on a ranged touch attack with the ray to strike a target. A living creature struck by the ray is dazed for 1d3 rounds if it fails its save.
