<b>Twilight Luck</b>(Book of Exalted Deeds)

Abjuration [Good]
 <b>Level:</b>   Sanctified 1,   
<b>Components:</b>V, Abstinence
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> One nonevil creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
By means of this spell, the caster can impart the luck of the fey to one nonevil being.
The target gains a +1 luck bonus on all saving throws for the duration of the spell.
Abstinence: The caster must refrain from imbibing alcohol for 3 days prior to casting this spell.
