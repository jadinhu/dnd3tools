<b>Demon Wings</b>(Fiendish Codex I: Hordes of the Abyss)

Transmutation [Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Blackguard 3,   Demonic 3,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes/level (D)

 	
You raise your arms dramatically, whisper the name &quot;Pazuzu&quot;
twice, and watch as demonic wings sprout from your back.
Wings sprout from your back, allowing you to fly at your normal land speed with average maneuverability.
You can carry weight while flying according to your normal carrying capacity, and greater burdens affect your fly speed as they would your land speed.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Book of Vile Darkness
