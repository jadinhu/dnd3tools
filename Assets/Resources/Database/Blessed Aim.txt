<b>Blessed Aim</b>(Complete Divine)

Divination
 <b>Level:</b>  Blackguard 2,  Paladin 2,  Cleric 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 60 ft.
 <b>Effect:</b> 60-ft. spread, centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> Concentration
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
This spell grants a +2 morale bonus on all ranged attacks for your allies within the spread.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium  Defenders of the Faith: A Guidebook to Clerics and Paladins
