<b>Cobra's Breath</b>(Oriental Adventures)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Wu Jen 1 (Water),   Maho 1,   
<b>Components:</b>S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous

 	
Your saliva changes into a virulent poison that you then spit forth in a 10-foot cone.
Creatures within the cone must make a successful Fortitude saving throw or take 1d3 points of temporary Constitution damage.
(There is no secondary damage).
The poison does not affect you.
Material Component: A cobra's fang.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Complete Arcane
