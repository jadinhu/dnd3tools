<b>Entangling Dragonmark</b>(Dragonmarked, p. 151)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Artificer 1,  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, Dragonmark
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> One dragonmarked creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>With a simple gesture and utterance, you entangle the target in strands of energy that erupt from its own dragonmark.</b>
	
A target that fails its saving throw is entangled for the duration of the spell.
	
<b>Dragonmark:</b> Any true or aberrant dragonmark.
