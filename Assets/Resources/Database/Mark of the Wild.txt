<b>Mark of the Wild</b>(Dragonmarked, p. 152)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Ranger 4,  Druid 5,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, Dragonmark
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> See text

 	
<b>Your dragonmark grows warm. Dangerous animals nearby no longer regard you as a threat.</b>
	
For the duration of the spell, no animal threatens or attacks you unless you attack it first. The spell's duration depends on the strength of your dragonmark.
	<table>		<tr>			<th>Dragonmark </th>			<th>Duration </th>		</tr>		<tr>			<td> Least </td>			<td> 1 min./level </td>		</tr>		<tr>			<td> Lesser </td>			<td> 10 min./level </td>		</tr>		<tr>			<td> Greater or Siberys </td>			<td> 1 hour/level </td>		</tr>	</table>	
If you attack any animal, the spell ends immediately.
	
<b>Dragonmark:</b> Any true or aberrant dragonmark.
