<b>Incorporeal Enhancement</b>(Libris Mortis: The Book of the Dead)

Necromancy [Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 full round
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One incorporeal undead/level
 <b>Duration:</b> 24 hours
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
The undead affected by this spell gain a +1 deflection bonus to Armor Class, +1d8 bonus hit points, a +1 enhancement bonus on attack rolls, and a +2 bonus to turn resistance.
Each of these enhancements doubles for every five caster levels, so that a 20th-level caster grants undead +4 to AC, +4d8 bonus hit points, +4 on attack rolls and +8 to turn resistance.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
