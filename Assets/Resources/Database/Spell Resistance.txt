<b>Spell Resistance</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 282)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 5,  Runescarred Berserker 5,  Knight of the Weave 5,  Nar Demonbinder 5,   Magic 5,  Protection 5,  Baator 5,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 min./level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
The creature gains spell resistance equal to 12 + your caster level.
