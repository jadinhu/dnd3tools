<b>Iron Bones</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,  Cleric 4,    
<b>Components:</b>

 	
As stone bones, except the target creature's skeleton changes to iron.
The creature gains a +5 natural armor bonus to AC.
Focus: A miniature skull made out of iron or steel.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium  Ghostwalk  Tome of Battle: The Book of Nine Swords
