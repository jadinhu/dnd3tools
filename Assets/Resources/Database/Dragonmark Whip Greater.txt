<b>Dragonmark Whip, Greater</b>(Dragonmarked, p. 151)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 6,  Wizard 6,    
<b>Components:</b>S, Dragonmark
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Effect:</b> One 10-foot-long whip
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
As a <b>dragonmark whip</b>, except that the whip has a reach of 20 feet, deals up to 1d8+20 points of damage, and can be used to deliver touch spells of 7th level or lower.
	
<b>Dragonmark:</b> Any greater true or aberrant dragonmark, or any Siberys dragonmark.
