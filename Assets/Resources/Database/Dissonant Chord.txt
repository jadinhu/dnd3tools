<b>Dissonant Chord</b>(Complete Adventurer)

Evocation [Sonic]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 10 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> 10-ft.-radius burst centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You emit a terrible, piercing note.
Creatures (other than you) in the affected area take 1d8 points of sonic damage per two caster levels (maximum 5d8).
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
