<b>Aid</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 196)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 2,  Adept 2,  Knight of the Chalice 2,   Good 2,  Luck 2,  Arborea 2,  Courage 2,  Revered Ancestor 2,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Living creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 min./level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
Aid grants the target a +1 morale bonus on attack rolls and saves against fear effects, plus temporary hit points equal to 1d8 + caster level (to a maximum of 1d8+10 temporary hit points at caster level 10th).
