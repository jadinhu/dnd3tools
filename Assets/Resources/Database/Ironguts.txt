<b>Ironguts</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You make a creature more able to fight the effects of poison.
The target gains a +4 circumstance bonus on Fortitude saves against all kinds of poisons, whether injury, contact, ingestion, or inhalation is the source of the poison.
Material Component: A vial containing the diluted poison of four different creatures.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
