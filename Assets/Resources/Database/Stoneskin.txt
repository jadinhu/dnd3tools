<b>Stoneskin</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 284)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Trapsmith 2,  Fatemaker 4,  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,  Death Master 4,  Adept 4,  Sha'ir 4,  Runescarred Berserker 4,  Wu Jen 4 (Earth),  Druid 5,  Disciple of Thrym 5,  Urban Druid 5,   Endurance 5,  Earth 6,  Strength 6,  Dragon 6,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 min./level or until discharged
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
The warded creature gains resistance to blows, cuts, stabs, and slashes.
The subject gains damage reduction 10/adamantine.
(It ignores the first 10 points of damage each time it takes damage from a weapon, though an adamantine weapon bypasses the reduction).
Once the spell has prevented a total of 10 points of damage per caster level (maximum 150 points), it is discharged.
Material Component: Granite and 250 gp worth of diamond dust sprinkled on the target's skin.
