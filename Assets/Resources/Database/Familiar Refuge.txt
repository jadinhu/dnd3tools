<b>Familiar Refuge</b>(Complete Mage)

Conjuration (Teleportation)
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 6,  Wizard 6,    
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> Unlimited
 <b>Target:</b> You or your familiar
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
You feel the bond between yourself and your familiar strengthen, and then the animal stands before you.
You instantly transport yourself to your familiar, or your familiar to you, as greater teleport.
You can't bring any other living creatures with you.
