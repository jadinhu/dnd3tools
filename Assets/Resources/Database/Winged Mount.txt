<b>Winged Mount</b>(Book of Exalted Deeds)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Paladin 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Special mount touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
This spell causes your mount to sprout giant, feathery white wings.
The wings grant the mount a fly speed of 60 feet, with good maneuverability.
The mount is slowed as normal for weight carried, barding worn, and environmental factors.
