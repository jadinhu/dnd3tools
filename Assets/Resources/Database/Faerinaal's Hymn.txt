<b>Faerinaal's Hymn</b>(Book of Exalted Deeds)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Good, Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> Up to one evil creature/level
 <b>Duration:</b> Concentration
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
This gentle hymn clouds the minds of evil creatures, causing targets of the spell to forgo attacks of opportunity for the spell's duration.
A target must be able to hear the hymn to be affected by the spell.
