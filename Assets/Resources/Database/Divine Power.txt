<b>Divine Power</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 224)

Evocation
 <b>Level:</b>  Runescarred Berserker 3,  Cleric 4,  Vassal of Bahamut 4,  Savant 4 (Divine),   War 4,  Celestia 4,  Competition 4,  Orc 4,  Pride 4,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level

 	
Calling upon the divine power of your patron, you imbue yourself with strength and skill in combat. Your base attack bonus becomes equal to your character level (which may give you additional attacks), you gain a +6 enhancement bonus to Strength, and you gain 1 temporary hit point per caster level.
