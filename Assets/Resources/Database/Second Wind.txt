<b>Second Wind</b>(Book of Exalted Deeds)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Paladin 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
For the duration of the spell, the target can perform long-term strenuous activity as though it had the Endurance feat.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
