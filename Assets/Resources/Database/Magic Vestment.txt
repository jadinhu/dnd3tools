<b>Magic Vestment</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 251)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 3,  Knight of the Chalice 3,  Savant 3 (Divine),   Strength 3,  War 3,  Celestia 3,  Halfling 3,  Nobility 3,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Armor or shield touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless, object)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless, object)

 	
You imbue a suit of armor or a shield with an enhancement bonus of +1 per four caster levels (maximum +5 at 20th level).
	
An outfit of regular clothing counts as armor that grants no AC bonus for the purpose of this spell.
