<b>Demonhide</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 63)

Abjuration [Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Blackguard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Evil creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
<b>You touch your minion with your holy symbols and invoke the horrid words of the lower planes. A fiery glow spreads across your servant's skin, leaving it with a deep red luster.</b>
	
The subject gains damage reduction 5/cold iron or good.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Miniatures Handbook
