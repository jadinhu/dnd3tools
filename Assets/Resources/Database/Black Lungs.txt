<b>Black Lungs</b>(Ghostwalk)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Living creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You cause the target to succumb to an infection and corruption of the lungs.
An affected creature's speed is reduced by half, it loses any Dexterity bonus to Armor Class, and it takes a -2 penalty on attack rolls.
Remove disease may end the effects of this spell early.
This spell was created by the clerics of Khostren.
