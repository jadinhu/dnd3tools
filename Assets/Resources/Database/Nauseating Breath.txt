<b>Nauseating Breath</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 146)

Conjuration (Creation)
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 3,  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 30 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> Cone-shaped burst
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
<b>You windmill your arm to complete the spell and nearly gag before exhaling a great gout of putrid-smelling green gas.</b>
	
You breathe out a cone of nauseating vapors. Any creature in the area must succeed on a Fortitude save or be nauseated for 1d6 rounds.
	
Material Component: A piece of fish at least one day old.
