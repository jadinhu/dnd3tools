<b>Heaven's Trumpet</b>(Book of Exalted Deeds)

Evocation [Good, Sonic]
 <b>Level:</b>  Emissary of Barachiel 4,  Bard 6,  Cleric 7,    
<b>Components:</b>S, AF, Archon
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 120 ft.
 <b>Target:</b> Foes in a 120-ft.-radius burst centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You produce a blast of music of utter clarity, piercing beauty, and paralyzing awe.
All foes within 120 feet of you must succeed on a Fortitude save or be paralyzed for 1d4 rounds.
Focus: A masterwork trumpet, which you must blow.
