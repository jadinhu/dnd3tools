<b>Saltray</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 179)

Evocation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> Ray
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude partial
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>A thin shaft of whirling salt crystals lances out from your crooked finger to strike and shred your foe.</b>
	
You must succeed on a ranged touch attack with the ray to strike a target. A creature struck by a saltray takes 1d6 points of damage per two caster levels (maximum 5d6) and must make a Fortitude save or be stunned for 1 round.
