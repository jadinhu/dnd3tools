<b>Inflict Critical Wounds, Mass</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 244)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 8,  Death Master 8,  Dread Necromancer 8,    
<b>Components:</b>

 	
This spell functions like mass inflict light wounds, except that it deals 4d8 points of damage +1 point per caster level (maximum +40).
