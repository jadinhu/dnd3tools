<b>Enlarge Person, Greater</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 82)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 5,  Wizard 5,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 round
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> One humanoid creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>Your intended subject begins to grow rapidly as you complete the spell.</b>
	
This spell functions like <b>enlarge person</b> (<b>PH</b> 226), except as noted above.
