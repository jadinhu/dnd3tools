<b>Suspended Silence</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 216)

Illusion (Glamer)
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> One object
 <b>Duration:</b> 24 hours or until discharged, then 6 rounds; see text
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None (object)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No (object)

 	
<b>You sprinkle the object with a handful of gem dust, and the last words of your spell fade like they are being dragged into a hole. Then the normal sounds of the world reestablish themselves, but the spell within is set to be unleashed later.</b>
	
When you touch the object and give the command word you designate, the object becomes the subject of a <b>silence</b> spell. The effect of the silence spell moves with the object and lasts for 6 rounds.
	
Material Component: A feather and a handful of gem dust worth 50 gp.
