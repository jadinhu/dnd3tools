<b>Aura of Cold, Greater</b>(Frostburn)

Transmutation [Cold]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 7,  Druid 7,    
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Range:</b> 10 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> 10-ft.-radius spherical emanation, centered on you

 	
This spell functions exactly like lesser aura of cold, except it deals 2d6 points of cold damage to all creatures within 10 feet.
