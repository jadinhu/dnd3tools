<b>Weighed In the Balance</b>(Underdark)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>   Balance 9,   
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 30 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> Creatures in a 30-ft.-radius spread centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
This spell harms or heals creatures within its area based on their alignments and life status, as given in the table below.
The effect does the most harm to those creatures most extreme in alignment and most out of balance with the natural world.
