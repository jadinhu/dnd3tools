<b>Trobriand's Glassee</b>(City of Splendors: Waterdeep)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 6,  Wizard 6,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One nonmagical stone or metal object up to 1 cu. ft. per level
 <b>Duration:</b> Permanent
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
This spell gives the target object the transparency of glass.
Material Component: Glass sliver.
