<b>Repel Ectoplasm</b>(Ghostwalk)

Abjuration [Ectomancy]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 6,  Wizard 6,   Ghost 7,   
<b>Components:</b>

 	
As repel wood, but affecting only ectoplasm, whether inert or part of an ectoplasmic creature.
Large manifestations of ectoplasm (such as a wall of ectoplasm spell) are not considered &quot;fixed firmly&quot; for the purpose of this spell and are pushed back by the waves of energy.
