<b>Blast of Flame</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 31)

Conjuration (Creation) [Fire]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 Standard Action
 <b>Range:</b> 60 ft
 <b>Area:</b> Cone-shaped burst
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Reflex half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
<b>As you cast the spell, your hand becomes sheathed in barely perceptible yellow flames. With a roar, the flames burst from your hand in the shape of a cone, leaving your hand trailing wisps of smoke.</b>
	
Flames fill the area, dealing 1d6 points of fire damage per caster level (maximum 10d6) to any creature in the area that fails its saving throw.
	
<b>Material Component:</b> A bit of wick soaked in oil.
