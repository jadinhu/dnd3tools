<b>Skyline Runner</b>(Races of Destiny)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>   City 5,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level

 	
You can walk normally on any city surface as if it were level ground.
This includes perfectly vertical walls, glass windows, and even clothes-drying lines.
Your movement speed remains unchanged, even when walking straight up a wall.
