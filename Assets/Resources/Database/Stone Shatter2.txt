<b>Stone Shatter</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 208)

Evocation [Sonic]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One stone object or creature
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None or Fortitude half; see text
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
<b>You call up energies from deep within you and utter a perfect note. The stone rocks as it is struck, and cracks appear in its form.</b>
	
You aim a single note of perfect pitch toward an unattended stone object weighing no more than 2 pounds per caster level, or toward a stone creature, causing it to shatter. A stone object is destroyed, whereas a stone creature (of any weight) takes 1d6 points of damage per caster level (maximum 15d6), with a Fortitude save for half damage.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Magic of Faerun
