<b>Increase Virulence</b>(Player's Handbook II)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Assassin 2,  Bard 2,  Blackguard 2,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 minute
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Vial of poison or creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
Ribbons of green and black energy engulf your hand and pass into the vial or creature you touch.
You make the poison in a vial or a creature more lethal.
The DCs for all saving throws against the poison increase by 2.
Material Component: Licorice root.
