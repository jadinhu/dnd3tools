<b>Circle of Flame</b>(Oriental Adventures)

Evocation [Fire]
 <b>Level:</b>  Shugenja 5 (Fire),    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> 20 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> All creatures within a 20-ft.-radius burst centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Reflex half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
Fire bursts in all directions from the point of origin, dealing 1d8 points of damage +1 point per caster level (maximum +20) to nearby creatures.
