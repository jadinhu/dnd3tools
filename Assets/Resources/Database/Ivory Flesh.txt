<b>Ivory Flesh</b>(Frostburn)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 1,  Druid 1,  Ranger 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
The subject's flesh and all his equipment turn white, granting him a +5 circumstance bonus on Hide checks in heavy snow or ice areas.
In any nonwhite-hued area (including ebony ice), ivory flesh instead incurs a -5 penalty on Hide checks.
