<b>Sonic Orb, Lesser</b>(Tome and Blood: A Guidebook to Wizards and Sorcerers, p. 93)

Evocation [Sonic]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> Up to five creatures or objects, no two of which can be more than 15 ft. apart
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
As <b>lesser acid orb</b>, except <b>lesser sonic orb</b> deals sonic damage.
