<b>Shadow Tentacle, Greater</b>(Lords of Darkness)

Abjuration (Shadow)
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 5,  Wizard 5,    
<b>Components:</b>S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> One shadow
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level or concentration
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
As lesser shadow tentacle, except the spell creates a shadow tentacle that extends up to 10 feet per caster level (maximum 100 feet) from its anchoring point.
The tentacle created by this spell has 40 hit points, AC 18, and can be burst with a Strength check (DC 25).
A character can also free himself from the tentacle with an Escape Artist check (DC 22).
