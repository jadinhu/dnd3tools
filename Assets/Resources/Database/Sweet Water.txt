<b>Sweet Water</b>(Defenders of the Faith: A Guidebook to Clerics and Paladins)

Divination
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 2,  Cleric 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level)
 <b>Effect:</b> A well shaft, 10 ft. diameter, up to 100 ft. deep.
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
This spell locates a source of fresh water within 100 feet of the surface.
If a water source is in range, it excavates a well shaft down to that water.
Otherwise, the spell fails.
Material Component: A shovel or spade.
