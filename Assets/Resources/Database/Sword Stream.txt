<b>Sword Stream</b>(Defenders of the Faith: A Guidebook to Clerics and Paladins)

Evocation
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> 5 ft. wide out to limit of range
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Reflex half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
Sword stream causes moisture in the air to sparkle, coalesce, then shoot away from your fingertips in a high-pressure stream.
Each creature in the effect takes 1d8 points of damage, plus 1 additional point per caster level to a maximum of +10.
