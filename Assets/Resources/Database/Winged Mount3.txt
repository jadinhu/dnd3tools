<b>Winged Mount</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 240)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Blackguard 4,  Paladin 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Your touched special mount
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
<b>You touch your special mount and it sprouts giant, feathery white wings.</b>
	
This spell grants your special mount a fly speed of 60 feet (good). The mount is slowed as normal because of weight carried, barding worn, and environmental factors.
