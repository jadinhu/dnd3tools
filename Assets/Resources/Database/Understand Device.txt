<b>Understand Device</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Divination
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 3 (Gond),    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level

 	
You gain an intuitive insight into the weaknesses and flaws of mechanical objects.
You can make untrained Disable Device checks as if you had a half rank in that skill.
If you already have the Disable Device skill, this spell gives you a +4 competence bonus on Disable Device checks.
