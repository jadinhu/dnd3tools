<b>Mental Strength</b>(Oriental Adventures)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Shaman 3,  Sohei 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, AF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Living creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
You imbue the recipient with magical energy that fortifies her will, granting her a +8 resistance bonus on all Will saves.
Focus: The tail of a white fox.
