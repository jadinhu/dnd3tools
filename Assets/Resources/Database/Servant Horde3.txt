<b>Servant Horde</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 182)

Conjuration (Creation)
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> Invisible, mindless, shapeless servants
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
<b>As you complete this spell, you sense a number of invisible entities form around you.</b>
	
This spell creates a number of <b>unseen servants</b> (PH 297) equal to 2d6 +1 per level (maximum +15).
	
Material Component: A small stick to which many lengths of knotted thread are attached.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Complete Arcane  Oriental Adventures
