<b>Nightshield</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 148)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 1,  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level (D)

 	
<b>With a whisper-quiet whoosh, a field of shadowy energy cloaks your body.</b>
	
This spell provides a +1 resistance bonus on saving throws; this resistance bonus increases to +2 at caster level 6th and +3 at caster level 9th. In addition, the spell negates magic missile attacks directed at you.
