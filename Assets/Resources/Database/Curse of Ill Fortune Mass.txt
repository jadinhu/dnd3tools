<b>Curse of Ill Fortune, Mass</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 56)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 5,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> Enemies in a 20-ft.-radius burst
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
This spell functions like <b>curse of ill fortune</b>, except that it affects multiple enemies.
