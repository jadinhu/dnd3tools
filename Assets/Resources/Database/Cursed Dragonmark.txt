<b>Cursed Dragonmark</b>(Dragonmarked, p. 149)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 3,  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, Dragonmark
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 Standard Action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25ft. + 5ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One dragonmarked creature
 <b>Duration:</b> Permanent
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will Negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
Every time the target creature attempts to use or expend one of its dragonmark powers, there is a 50% chance that the attempt fails, wasting that use of the mark. A <b>cursed dragonmark</b> can be restored to normal with a break enchantment , limited wish, miracle, remove curse, or wish spell.
	
<b>Dragonmark.</b> Any true or aberrant dragonmark.
