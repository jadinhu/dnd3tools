<b>Dream Walk</b>(Heroes of Horror)

Conjuration (Teleportation)
 <b>Level:</b>   Oneiromancy 4,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched, or up to eight willing creatures joining hands
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You move yourself or some other creature into a dreamscape.
You must either be able to see the dreamer whose dreams you wish to enter or be within 30 feet of her.
This spell otherwise functions exactly as plane shift.
