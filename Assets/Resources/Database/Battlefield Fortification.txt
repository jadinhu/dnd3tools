<b>Battlefield Fortification</b>(Heroes of Battle)

Transmutation [Earth]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 round
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Area:</b> See text
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
You shift earth to create a shallow trench or a berm, as described on page 35.
The trench or berm created is a straight line up to 5 feet long per two caster levels.
A berm created by this spell is 2 squares wide.
This spell has no effect when cast in an area of stone such as an unworked cavern floor, a flagstone floor, or a cobblestone street.
