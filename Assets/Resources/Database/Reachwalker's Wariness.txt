<b>Reachwalker's Wariness</b>(Races of Eberron, p. 189)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 2,  Ranger 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 30 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> 30-ft.-radius spherical emanation, centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes/level

 	
When this spell is in effect, you are instantly aware of the exact position of any aberration within the spell's area, as if you had blindsense with respect to aberrations only. See page 306 of the Monster Manual for details on the blindsense ability.
	
<b>Special:</b> Shifter spellcasters cast this spell at +1 caster level.
