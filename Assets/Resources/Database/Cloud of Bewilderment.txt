<b>Cloud of Bewilderment</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Evocation
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 2,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> 10 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> Cone
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You breathe out an invisible cloud of noxious air.
Anyone in the area is stunned and blinded for 1d6 rounds.
A moderate wind (11+ mph) disperses the cloud in 4 rounds; a strong wind (21+ mph) disperses the fog in 1 round.
