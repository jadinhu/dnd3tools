<b>Iron Construct</b>(Eberron Campaign Setting, p. 113)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Artificer 4,   Warforged 6,   
<b>Components:</b>S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 round
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Construct touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
You transform an existing construct into a powerhouse of hardened iron, similar to the effect of the <b>iron body</b> spell. The construct gains damage reduction 15/adamantine. It takes half damage from acid and fire attacks of all kinds. Even if it is not a golem, however, it becomes vulnerable to all special attacks that affect iron golems.
