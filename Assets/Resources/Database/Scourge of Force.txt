<b>Scourge of Force</b>(Champions of Ruin)

Evocation [Force]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> One tendril of force plus one additional tendril per three caster levels (maximum four)
 <b>Target:</b> One or more targets, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
One or more whiplike tendrils of force spring from your hand and lash targets within range.
Make a melee touch attack for each tendril using your highest attack bonus.
If a tendril hits, it deals 1d8 points of force damage plus 1 point of force damage per two caster levels (maximum 1d8+5).
You can lash a target with multiple tendrils.
