<b>Darkvision, Mass</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 59)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 10 ft
 <b>Target:</b> Allies in a 10-ft. radius burst centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
<b>As you conclude the spell's casting, you are aware of being able to see without light. A glance at your allies show that they too perceive more than before.</b>
	
This spell functions like <b>darkvision</b> (PH 216), except that all target creatures receive the spell's benefits. Unlike with <b>darkvision</b>, recipients of this spell cannot have the ability made permanent with a permanency spell.
	
<b>Material Component:</b> A dried carrot or three small agates.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Complete Arcane  Tome and Blood: A Guidebook to Wizards and Sorcerers  Underdark
