<b>Stone Metamorphosis</b>(Underdark)

Transmutation [Earth]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 4,  Druid 4,  Sorcerer 6,  Wizard 6,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Stone object touched, up to 10 cu. ft. + 1 cu. ft./level
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
You can change an existing piece of rock to another type of rock.
For instance, you can make crumblestone into granite, substantially strengthening it.
(See Chapter 7 for more information on types of stone).
Precious and semiprecious gems cannot be manufactured with this spell, and its effect does not change the monetary value of the affected stone object.
Arcane Material Component: A grain of talc and a chip of obsidian.
