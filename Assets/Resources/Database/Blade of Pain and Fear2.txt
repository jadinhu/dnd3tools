<b>Blade of Pain and Fear</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 30)

Evocation
 <b>Level:</b>  Assassin 2,  Blackguard 2,  Cleric 3,  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,   Deathbound 2,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 Standard Action
 <b>Range:</b> 0 ft
 <b>Effect:</b> Swordlike column of gnashing teeth
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will partial
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>A three-foot-long column of disembodied gnashing teeth springs forth from your hand.</b>
	
For the duration of the spell, you can make melee touch attacks that deal 1d6 points of damage +1 point per two caster levels (maximum +10). Your Strength modifier does not apply to the damage. A creature that you successfully deal damage to must also make a Will saving throw or become frightened for 1d4 rounds.
