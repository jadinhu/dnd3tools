<b>Touch of Madness</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 221)

Enchantment [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>   Madness 2,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>Your hand glows with roiling purple light as you reach out to deliver lunacy with your touch.</b>
	
You can cause one living creature to become dazed by making a successful touch attack. If the target creature does not make a successful Will save, its mind is clouded and it takes no actions for 1 round per caster level.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Eberron Campaign Setting
