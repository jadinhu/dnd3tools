<b>Summon Nature's Ally II</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 288)

Conjuration (Summoning)
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 2,  Ranger 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 round
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> One or more creatures, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
This spell functions like <b>summon nature's ally I</b>, except that you can summon one 2nd-level creature or 1d3 1st-level creatures of the same kind.
