<b>Blindsight</b>(Savage Species)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 2,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,  Cleric 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
This spell grants the subject the blindsight ability out to a range of 30 feet.
