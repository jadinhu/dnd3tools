<b>Ghost Touch Armor</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 102)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 2,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Armor of creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute / level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
<b>Confronted with incorporeal enemies, you give the armor you touch the power to defend against such foes.</b>
	
The subject's armor gains the ghost touch special ability (<span class="caps">DMG</span> 219).
<b>Material Component</b>: A tiny shield made of resin.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Ghostwalk  Libris Mortis: The Book of the Dead
