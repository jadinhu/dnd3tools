<b>Dispelling Screen, Greater</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 67)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 7,  Wizard 7,   Drow 6,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> Energy wall whose area is up to one 10-ft. square/level, or a sphere or hemisphere with a radius of up to 1 ft./level
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
This spell functions like <b>dispelling screen</b>, except that the maximum caster level bonus on the dispel check is +20.
