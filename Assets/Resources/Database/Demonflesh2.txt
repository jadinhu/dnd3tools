<b>Demonflesh</b>(Fiendish Codex I: Hordes of the Abyss)

Transmutation [Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Blackguard 1,   Demonic 1,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level

 	
Your flesh becomes thick and leathery like a demon's.
This spell grants you a +1 enhancement bonus to your natural armor for every five caster levels you possess (minimum +1, maximum +4).
A creature without natural armor has an effective natural armor bonus of +0.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Book of Vile Darkness
