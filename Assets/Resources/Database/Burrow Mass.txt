<b>Burrow, Mass</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 41)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Ranger 3,  Druid 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, AF, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> One creature/level, no two of which are more than 30 ft. apart
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
<b>You call upon the power of the spell and, in turn, each of your allies' hands gain a yellow nimbus, forming into the hands of large moles.</b>
	
This spell functions like <b>burrow</b>, except that it affects multiple creatures.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Underdark
