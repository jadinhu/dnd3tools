<b>Hunters of Hades</b>(Planar Handbook)

Conjuration (Summoning) [Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 9,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 minute
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Effect:</b> Two summoned pack fiends, which can be no more than 30 ft. apart
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 min./level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
This spell summons a pair of pack fiends (see page 128) from the Gray Waste of Hades to serve you.
The pack fiends magically understand your spoken commands (regardless of your language).
The pack fiends are treated as trained hunting beasts, so you can command them to track if you so choose.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
