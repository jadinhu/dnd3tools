<b>Storm of Needles</b>(Complete Mage)

Conjuration (Creation)
 <b>Level:</b>  Wu Jen 4 (metal),    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 30 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> Cone-shaped burst
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Reflex half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
You flick a needle out in front of you.
Instantly, the area is filled with a burst of thousands of tiny needles, which vanish almost as swiftly as they appear.
Creatures in the cone take 1d4 points of damage per caster level (maximum 15d4).
Material Component: A 1-inch-long metal needle.
