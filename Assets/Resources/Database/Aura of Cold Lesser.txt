<b>Aura of Cold, Lesser</b>(Frostburn)

Transmutation [Cold]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 3,  Druid 3,  Disciple of Thrym 3,  Paladin 4,  Ranger 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 5 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> 5-ft.-radius spherical emanation, centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You are covered in a thin layer of white frost and frigid cold emanates from your body, dealing 1d6 points of cold damage at the start of your round to each creature within 5 feet.
