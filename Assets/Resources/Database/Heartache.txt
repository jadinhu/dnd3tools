<b>Heartache</b>(Book of Vile Darkness)

Enchantment [Evil, Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 1,  Mortal Hunter 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
The caster fills the subject with heart-wrenching sorrow that renders it incapacitated for 1 round.
The subject cannot move or take actions and is helpless for that round.
