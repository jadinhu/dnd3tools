<b>Fireburst, Greater</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 94)

Evocation [Fire]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 5,  Wizard 5,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 10 ft.
 <b>Effect:</b> Burst of fire extending 15 ft. from you
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Reflex half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
This spell functions like _fireburst, except that it affects creatures within 15 feet of you and deals 1d10 points of fire damage per caster level (maximum 15d10).
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Miniatures Handbook  Complete Arcane
