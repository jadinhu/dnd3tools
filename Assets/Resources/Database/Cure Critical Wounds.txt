<b>Cure Critical Wounds</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 215)

Conjuration (Healing)
 <b>Level:</b>  Runescarred Berserker 3,  Ardent 4,  Cleric 4,  Healer 4,  Adept 4,  Hunter of the Dead 4,  Druid 5,  Knight of the Weave 5,  Urban Druid 5,   Healing 4,   
<b>Components:</b>

 	
This spell functions like cure light wounds, except that it cures 4d8 points of damage +1 point per caster level (maximum +20).
