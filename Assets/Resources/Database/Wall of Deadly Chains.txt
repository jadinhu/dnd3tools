<b>Wall of Deadly Chains</b>(Book of Vile Darkness)

Conjuration (Creation)
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,    
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Reflex half

 	
As wall of chains, except as noted above, and the wall sports loose lengths of spiked chain that strike anyone within 5 feet of the wall.
Those within that area take 3d6 points of damage each round.
