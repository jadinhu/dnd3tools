<b>Camouflage</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 43)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 1,  Ranger 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 Standard Action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 Minutes/level

 	
<b>Upon finishing the spell, your skin and clothing change color, warping tint and hue to match your surroundings.</b>
	
Throughout the duration of the spell, your coloration changes instantly to match the background of any new environment you enter, with no effort on your part. This effect grants you a +10 circumstance bonus on Hide checks.
