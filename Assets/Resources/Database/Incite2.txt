<b>Incite</b>(Spell Compendium)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 1,  Cleric 1,  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> Creatures in a 10-ft. burst
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>You cast this spell and your opponent is swallowed in a spiral of sparkling motes that urge your targets to act.</b>
	
Affected creatures are not allowed to delay or to ready an action. If a subject is currently delaying, it acts as soon as the spell is cast. If the subject currently has an action readied, it loses that action.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Miniatures Handbook
