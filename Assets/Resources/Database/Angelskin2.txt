<b>Angelskin</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 11)

Abjuration [Good]
 <b>Level:</b>  Paladin 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Lawful good creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
<b>You touch your ally with the holy symbol and invoke the blessed words. An opalescent glow spreads across her skin, imbuing it with a pearl-like sheen.</b>
