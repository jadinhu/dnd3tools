<b>Overwhelm</b>(Player's Handbook II)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Beguiler 6,  Sorcerer 6,  Wizard 6,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> One creature
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You grip the creature's head, and a surge of magical energy overwhelms its senses.
With a touch, you deal nonlethal damage to the subject equal to the creature's current hit point total.
