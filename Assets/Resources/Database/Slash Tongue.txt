<b>Slash Tongue</b>(Book of Vile Darkness)

Transmutation [Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 0,  Sorcerer 0,  Wizard 0,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One living creature with a tongue
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
The subject's tongue gets a thin cut.
The subject takes 1 point of damage and takes a -1 penalty on attack rolls, saving throws, skill checks, and ability checks on the following round due to the annoying pain.
