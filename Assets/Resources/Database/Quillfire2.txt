<b>Quillfire</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 164)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level

 	
<b>You evoke the power of nature, and the back of your hand bristles with a spikelike growth of quills.</b>
	
Your hand sprouts poisonous quills that you can use in melee or as a ranged attack.
	
The quills deal 1d8 points of damage. They can be thrown (range increment 10 feet). The quills are poisonous (injury DC equal DC of quillfire, 1d6 Str/1d6 Str).
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Magic of Faerun
