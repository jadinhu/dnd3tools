<b>Scare</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 274)

Necromancy [Fear, Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 2,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,  Vigilante 2,  Sha'ir 2,  Death Master 2,  Jester 2,  Dread Necromancer 2,   Hatred 2,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> One living creature per three levels, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level or 1 round; see text for cause fear
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will partial
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
This spell functions like cause fear, except that it causes all targeted creatures of less than 6 HD to become frightened.
Material Component: A bit of bone from an undead skeleton, zombie, ghoul, ghast, or mummy.
