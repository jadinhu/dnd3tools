<b>Law Bearer</b>(Tome of Battle: The Book of Nine Swords, p. 59)

Devoted Spirit (Strike) [Law]
 <b>Level:</b>  Crusader 5 (One Devoted Spirit maneuver),    
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 full-round action
 <b>Range:</b> Melee attack
 <b>Target:</b> One creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round

 	
<b>Prerequisite</b>: lawful alignment
	
<b>The air around you hums with cosmic energy as the power of pure law surges through you. For a moment, you take on the aspect of a perfect being as you charge forward to smite your foes.</b>
	
You must make a charge attack as part of this maneuver. If the target is chaotic-aligned, you gain a +8 bonus on
your attack (in addition to the charge bonus) and your attack deals an extra 4d6 points of damage. In addition, if your charge attack hits and the target is chaotic-aligned, you become wreathed in axiomatic energy. You gain a +5 bonus on saves and to AC until the beginning of your next turn.
