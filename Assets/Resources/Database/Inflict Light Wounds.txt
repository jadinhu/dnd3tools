<b>Inflict Light Wounds</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 244)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 1, Blackguard 1,  Dread Necromancer 1,  Blighter 1,  Death Master 1,   Destruction 1,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
When laying your hand upon a creature, you channel negative energy that deals 1d8 points of damage +1 point per caster level (maximum +5).
Since undead are powered by negative energy, this spell cures such a creature of a like amount of damage, rather than harming it.
