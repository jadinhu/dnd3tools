<b>Daze</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 217)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 0,  Sorcerer 0,  Wizard 0,  Beguiler 0,  Wu Jen 0,  Sha'ir 0,  Jester 0,  Death Master 0,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One humanoid creature of 4 HD or less
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
This enchantment clouds the mind of a humanoid creature with 4 or fewer Hit Dice so that it takes no actions.
Humanoids of 5 or more HD are not affected.
A dazed subject is not stunned, so attackers get no special advantage against it.
Material Component: A pinch of wool or similar substance.
