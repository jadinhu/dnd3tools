<b>Imprison Possessor</b>(Book of Vile Darkness)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Demonologist 4,  Sorcerer 5,  Wizard 5,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One possessing creature and its host
 <b>Duration:</b> Permanent (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
The caster traps one creature that can possess or inhabit other creatures, or one that can move its mind out of its body, within the creature it currently inhabits.
Potential targets include possessing demons, malevolent ghosts, and a caster currently using a magic jar spell.
The possessing creature cannot leave the body it is currently in, whether or not it is its own, by any means.
