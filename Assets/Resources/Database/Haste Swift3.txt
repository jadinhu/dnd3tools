<b>Haste, Swift</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 110)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Ranger 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1d4 rounds

 	
<b>Two quick syllables release the power of the spell, and the world moves a bit slower about you.</b>
	
This spell functions like <b>haste</b> (PH 239), except as noted above. This spell counters and dispels any slow effect on you.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Complete Adventurer  Miniatures Handbook
