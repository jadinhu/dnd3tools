<b>Wyrm's Flame</b>(Tome of Battle: The Book of Nine Swords, p. 56)

Desert Wind (Strike) [Fire]
 <b>Level:</b>  Swordsage 8 (Three Desert Wind maneuvers),    
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 30 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> Cone
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Reflex half

 	
<b>You spin your blade in a whirling arc. With each revolution, seething flames build upon its length. With a flourish, you bring your blade to a halt, point it at your foe, and unleash a roaring wall of flame.</b>
	
Masters of the Desert Wind can twirl and spin their blades with such power that they can evoke a great burst of fire. When you execute this maneuver, you blast forth a cone of fire that deals 10d6 points of fire damage to all in its area. Creatures in the area can attempt a Reflex save (DC 18 + your Wis modifier) for half damage. 
	
This maneuver is a supernatural ability.
