<b>Crown of Flame</b>(Book of Exalted Deeds)

Evocation [Good]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 5,  Exalted Arcanist 5,   Glory 5,   
<b>Components:</b>V, Archon
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 10 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> 10-ft.-radius emanation, centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You become a blazingly hot beacon of the powers of good, searing all evil outsiders, evil undead, and evil fey within 10 feet of you.
Affected creatures take 2d6 points of damage per round.
