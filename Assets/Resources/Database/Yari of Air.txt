<b>Yari of Air</b>(Oriental Adventures)

Evocation [Air]
 <b>Level:</b>  Shugenja 2 (Air),    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Effect:</b> Yarilike beam
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
A 6-foot-long shaft formed of air springs forth from your hands.
You wield this shaft as if it were a yari (shortspear).
Attacks with the yari of air are regular melee attacks.
The yari deals 1d8 points of damage +1 point per two caster levels (maximum +10).
The yari is considered a +1 weapon for purposes of overcoming damage reduction.
