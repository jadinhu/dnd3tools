<b>Ethereal Alarm</b>(Ghostwalk)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 2,  Ranger 2,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>

 	
As alarm, but detecting only ethereal creatures that enter the warded area.
The spell does not give you the ability to see the ethereal creatures once the alarm is triggered.
