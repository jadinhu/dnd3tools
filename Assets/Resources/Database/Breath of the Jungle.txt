<b>Breath of the Jungle</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 39)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./ level)
 <b>Effect:</b> Mist spreads in a 40-ft. radius, 20 ft. high
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
<b>With a word of power and flick of your wrist, a foul-smelling, fine mist appears.</b>
	
The DC of any saving throw made within the mist against poison or disease increases by 2.
	
The mist does not provide concealment.
