<b>Electric Jolt</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 78)

Evocation [Electricity]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 0,  Wizard 0,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> Ray
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
A white-hot electric spark dances on your fingertip and then bolts toward your target.
	
You release a small stroke of electrical energy. You must succeed on a ranged touch attack with the ray to strike a target. The spell deals 1d3 points of electricity damage.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Magic of Faerun
