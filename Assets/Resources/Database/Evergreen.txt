<b>Evergreen</b>(Frostburn)

Transmutation [Fire]
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Area:</b> 10-ft. radius
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level and instantaneous; see text
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
You imbue a 10-foot-radius area of plant life with magical heat, instantly healing 1d8 points of damage +1 point per caster level (maximum +10), and granting immunity to cold for the duration of the spell.
Evergreen affects natural plants as well as creatures with the plant subtype.
