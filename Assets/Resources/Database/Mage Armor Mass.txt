<b>Mage Armor, Mass</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 136)

Conjuration (Creation) [Force]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, AF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature/level, no two of which are more than 30 ft. apart
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
<b>You feel arcane energy encompass you, granting you a sense of serenity. As you choose your targets for the spell, you see each one glow briefly with a silver aura.</b> 
	
This spell functions like <b>mage armor</b> (PH 249), except that it affects multiple creatures.
