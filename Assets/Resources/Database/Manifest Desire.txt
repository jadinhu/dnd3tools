<b>Manifest Desire</b>(Heroes of Horror)

Illusion [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>   Oneiromancy 4,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One living creature
 <b>Duration:</b> Concentration + 3 rounds
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will disbelief (if interacted with)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
The target's greatest desire appears before her in illusory form for all to see.
Interaction with the image allows a Will save to disbelieve.
Many casters use this spell simply to learn the subject's greatest desire, not caring whether the illusion deceives the subject.
