<b>Dragonmarked Weapon, Greater</b>(Dragonmarked, p. 151)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Paladin 4,  Ranger 4,  Artificer 5,  Cleric 5,  Sorcerer 5,  Wizard 5,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, Dragonmark
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Weapon touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless, object)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
As <b>dragonmarked weapon</b>, except that the weapon deals an extra 2d6 points of damage to any dragonmarked creature whose mark does not correspond to the caster's dragonmarked house. In addition, the weapon's hardness is doubled for the spell's duration.
	
<b>Dragonmark:</b> Any greater true or aberrant dragonmark, or any Siberys dragonmark.
