<b>Beget Bogun</b>(Monster Manual II)

Conjuration (Creation)
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, <abbr title="Experience">XP, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Effect:</b> Tiny construct
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
Beget bogun allows you to infuse living magic into a small mannequin that you have created from vegetable matter.
This is the final spell in the process of creating a bogun.
See the bogun's description, above, for further details.
Material Component: The mannequin from which the bogun is created.
XP Cost: 25 XP.
