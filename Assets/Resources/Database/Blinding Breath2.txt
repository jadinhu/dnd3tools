<b>Blinding Breath</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 31)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,    
<b>Components:</b>S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 Swift Action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> Your Breath Weapon
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round

 	
<b>You expel your breath weapon, which crackles with eye-splitting intensity.</b>
	
This spell functions like breath flare (page 38), except that targets that fail their saving throws against your breath weapon are permanently blinded, rather than dazzled.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Draconomicon
