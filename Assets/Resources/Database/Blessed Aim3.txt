<b>Blessed Aim</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 31)

Divination
 <b>Level:</b>  Blackguard 1,  Cleric 1,  Paladin 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 50 ft.
 <b>Effect:</b> 50-ft.-radius spread centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
<b>With the blessing of your deity, you bolster your allies' aim with an exhortation.</b>
	
This spell grants your allies within the spread a +2 morale bonus on ranged attack rolls.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Complete Divine  Defenders of the Faith: A Guidebook to Clerics and Paladins
