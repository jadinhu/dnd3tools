<b>Air Breathing</b>(Stormwrack)

Transmutation [Air]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 3,  Druid 3,  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>S, M, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Living creatures touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 2 hours/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
The transmuted creature can breathe air freely.
Divide the duration evenly among all the creatures you touch.
The spell does not make creatures unable to breathe water.
Air breathing counters and dispels water breathing.
Arcane Material Component: A tiny vial of water.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Champions of Ruin  Savage Species  Spell Compendium
