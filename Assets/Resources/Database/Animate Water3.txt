<b>Animate Water</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 13)

Transmutation [Water]
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 1,    
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels
 <b>Target:</b> Cube of water up to 5 ft. on a side

 	
_You indicate a patch of water with a hand shaking from pent-up divine spell power, and the liquid rises into a vaguely humanoid shape.
	
This spell functions like animate fire, but you can instead animate a quantity of water of at least 4 cubic feet into a Small water elemental (MM 100).
	
<b>Material Component</b>: A vial of pure spring water mixed with cinnabar oil.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Complete Arcane  Oriental Adventures
