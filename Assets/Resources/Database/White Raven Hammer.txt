<b>White Raven Hammer</b>(Tome of Battle: The Book of Nine Swords, p. 94)

White Raven (Strike)
 <b>Level:</b>  Crusader 8 (Three White Raven maneuvers),  Warblade 8 (Three White Raven maneuvers),    
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Melee attack
 <b>Target:</b> One creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round

 	
<b>You slam your opponent with a mighty attack to disrupt his senses and leave him unable to defend himself while your allies close to finish him off.</b>
	
As part of this maneuver, you make a single, devastating strike against an opponent. The raw force of this blow knocks him senseless. Your attack deals an extra 6d6 points of damage and stuns your opponent for 1 round.
