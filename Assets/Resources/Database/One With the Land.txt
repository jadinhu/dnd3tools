<b>One With the Land</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 2,  Hathran 2,  Ranger 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level

 	
You forge a strong link with nature that gives you greater insight into your environment.
You gain a +2 competence bonus on Animal Empathy, Move Silently, Search, Hide, Intuit Direction, Wilderness Lore, and Handle Animal checks.
