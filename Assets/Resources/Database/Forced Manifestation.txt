<b>Forced Manifestation</b>(Ghostwalk)

Transmutation [Ectomancy]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 3,  Bone Collector 3,  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> One ghost or naturally incorporeal undead
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
The target creature is enveloped in a violet haze that forces it to manifest fully for the duration of the spell.
Forced manifestation counters and dispels forced incorporeality.
