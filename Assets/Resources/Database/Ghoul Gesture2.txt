<b>Ghoul Gesture</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 104)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>   Hunger 3,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft. / level)
 <b>Effect:</b> Ray
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round / level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude partial
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>A green ray stabs from your pointing finger to strike the belly of your enemy.</b>
	
You must succeed on a ranged touch attack with the ray to strike a target. A subject that is successfully targeted must make a Fortitude save or be paralyzed for the duration of the spell.
	
A subject of the ray that succeeds on its Fortitude save is instead sickened for the duration of the spell. <b>Neutralize poison</b> removes the sickened condition. 
	
<b>Material Component</b>: A small scrap of cloth taken from clothing worn by a ghoul, or a pinch of earth from a ghoul's lair.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Libris Mortis: The Book of the Dead
