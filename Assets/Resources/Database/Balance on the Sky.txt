<b>Balance on the Sky</b>(Tome of Battle: The Book of Nine Swords, p. 75)

Shadow Hand (Stance)
 <b>Level:</b>  Swordsage 8 (Three Shadow Hand maneuvers),    
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> Stance

 	
<b>With arms spread wide, you step onto the air.</b>
	
You gain the ability to use <b>air walk</b> (PH 196) on yourself only. You must keep at least one hand empty while using this stance. Moving upward requires you to spend 10 feet of movement for each 5 feet of elevation you gain.
	
You gain the benefit of <b>air walk</b> only while you maintain this stance. If for some reason your stance ends while you are in midair, you fall to the ground.
	
This stance is a supernatural ability.
