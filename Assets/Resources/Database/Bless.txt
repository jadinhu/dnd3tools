<b>Bless</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 205)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 1,  Paladin 1,  Adept 1,  Vassal of Bahamut 1,  Knight of the Weave 1,  Death Delver 1,  Savant 1 (Divine),   Community 1,  Family 1,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 50 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> The caster and all allies within a 50-ft. burst, centered on the caster
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 min./level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
Bless fills your allies with courage.
Each ally gains a +1 morale bonus on attack rolls and on saving throws against fear effects.
Bless counters and dispels bane.
