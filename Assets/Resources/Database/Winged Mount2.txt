<b>Winged Mount</b>(Complete Divine)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Blackguard 4,  Paladin 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Your touched mount
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 min./level

 	
This spell causes your mount to sprout giant, feathery white wings.
This grants the mount a fly speed of 60 ft., with a maneuverability of good.
The mount is slowed as normal for weight carried, barding worn, and environmental factors.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Book of Exalted Deeds  Spell Compendium
