<b>Rejection</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 172)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 6,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 60 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> Cone-shaped burst
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>With a stern word of disallowance, you cast the spell. A barely perceptible hum sounds in the direction you indicate.</b>
	
A creature in the area must succeed on a Fortitude saving throw or be pushed away from you to a distance of 5 feet per caster level. If the creature is pushed into a wall or similarly solid surface, it takes 1d6 points of damage for every 10 feet it was moved.
	
Movement forced by this spell can take the creature beyond the spell's range.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Savage Species
