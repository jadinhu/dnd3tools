<b>Eyes of the Avoral</b>(Book of Exalted Deeds)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Apostle of Peace 1,  Beloved of Valarian 1,  Cleric 1,  Druid 1,  Ranger 1,  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> One creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
The subject gains an avoral's sharp eyesight, receiving a +8 racial bonus on Spot checks for the duration of the spell.
