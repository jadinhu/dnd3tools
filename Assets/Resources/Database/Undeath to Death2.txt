<b>Undeath to Death</b>(Faiths &amp; Pantheons)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 6,  Sorcerer 6,  Wizard 6,   Repose 6,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Area:</b> Several undead creatures within a 50-ft.-radius burst
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
Undeath to death snuffs out the life forces of undead creatures, killing them instantly.
The spell slays 1d4 HD worth of undead creatures per caster level (maximum 20d4).
Creatures with the fewest HD are affected first; among creatures with equal HD, those who are closest to the point of origin of the burst are affected first.
Material Component: The powder of a crushed diamond worth at least 500 gp.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Tome and Blood: A Guidebook to Wizards and Sorcerers  Ghostwalk  Magic of Faerun  Player's Handbook v.3.5  Deities and Demigods
