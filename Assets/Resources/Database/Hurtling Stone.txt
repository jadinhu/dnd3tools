<b>Hurtling Stone</b>(Heroes of Battle)

Conjuration [Creation]
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 60 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> 60-ft. line
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Reflex negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
This spell creates a 50-pound stone that speeds away from the caster in a straight line, dealing 6d6 points of damage to each creature in its path.
In addition, any Medium or smaller creatures in its path are knocked prone.
