<b>Ectoplasmic Armor</b>(Libris Mortis: The Book of the Dead)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level (D)

 	
You gain a +5 armor bonus to AC, with an additional +1 to the bonus for every four caster levels you have (maximum +9 bonus at 16th level).
This armor bonus applies only against incorporeal touch attacks.
All other attacks ignore the armor bonus from ectoplasmic armor.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
