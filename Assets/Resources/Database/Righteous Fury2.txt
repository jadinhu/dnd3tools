<b>Righteous Fury</b>(Miniatures Handbook)

Evocation [Good]
 <b>Level:</b>  Paladin 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
The subject's charge attacks deal double damage.
(The subject does not have to be mounted or wielding a lance).
If the subject makes more than one attack on a charge, the double damage applies only to the first attack.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium  Magic of Faerun
