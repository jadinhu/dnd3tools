<b>Scarecrow</b>(Masters of the Wild: A Guidebook to Barbarians, Druids, and Rangers)

Necromancy [Fear, Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 0,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One animal
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
The subject becomes shaken, suffering a -2 morale penalty on attack rolls, saves, and checks for the duration of the spell.
