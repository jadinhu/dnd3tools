<b>True Creation</b>(Eberron Campaign Setting, p. 117)

Conjuration (Creation)
 <b>Level:</b>   Artifice 8,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, <abbr title="Experience">XP, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 10 minutes
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25ft. + 5ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> Unattended, nonmagical object of nonliving plant matter, up to 1 cu. ft./level
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
As <b>major creation</b> ,except items created are permanent and cannot be negated by dispelling magic or negating powers. For all intents and purposes, these items are completely real.
	
XP Cost: The item's gold piece value, or a minimum of 1 XP.
