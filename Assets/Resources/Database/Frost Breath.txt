<b>Frost Breath</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 100)

Evocation
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 2,  Druid 2,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 30 feet
 <b>Effect:</b> Cone-shaped burst
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Reflex half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>Giving a forceful exhale across your cupped hand, your breath shoots forth tinged with frost and crystals of ice.</b>
	
You breathe a cone of intense cold at your foes. The spell deals 1d4 points of cold damage per two caster levels (maximum 5d4). In addition, all creatures damaged by the frost breath that fail their Reflex save are dazed for 1 round by the sudden shock of cold.
	
<b>Material Component:</b> Three drops of water or fragments of ice (which are held in a cupped palm and blown toward the target).
