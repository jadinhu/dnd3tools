<b>Summon Clockwork Mender Swarm</b>(Monster Manual IV)

Conjuration (Summoning) [Lawful]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 4,  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,    
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Effect:</b> One swarm of clockwork menders

 	
This spell functions like summon monster I, except that you summon a single clockwork mender swarm.
The swarm can be directed to attack opponents, to use its swarm repair ability, and even to use its swarm sacrifice ability.
Arcane Focus: A broken metal gear.
