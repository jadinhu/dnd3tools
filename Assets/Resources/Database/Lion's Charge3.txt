<b>Lion's Charge</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 133)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Ranger 2,  Druid 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round

 	
<b>You cast the spell and a nimbus of yellow energy in the form of a lion surrounds you.</b>
	
This spell grants you the pounce special ability (MM 313), allowing you to make a full attack at the end of a charge.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Savage Species  Miniatures Handbook
