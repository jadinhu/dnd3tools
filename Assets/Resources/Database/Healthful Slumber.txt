<b>Healthful Slumber</b>(Song and Silence: A Guidebook to Bards and Rogues)

Conjuration (Healing)
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, AF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 10 minutes
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> Living creatures within range
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 day
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
Healthful slumber doubles the subjects' natural healing rate.
Each affected creature regains twice the hit points it otherwise would have during that day, depending on activity level.
Focus: The caster's instrument.
