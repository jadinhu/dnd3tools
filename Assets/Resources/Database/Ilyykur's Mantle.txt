<b>Ilyykur's Mantle</b>(Unapproachable East)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level (D)

 	
You cloak yourself in a luminous aura that protects you against hostile magic.
The aura has two effects: You gain a luck bonus of +1 per 3 caster levels (maximum +5) on saving throws against spells and spell-like abilities, and you gain electricity resistance 10.
Material Component: An uncut diamond worth at least 50 gp.
