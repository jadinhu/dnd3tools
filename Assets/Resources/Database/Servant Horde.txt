<b>Servant Horde</b>(Complete Arcane)

Conjuration (Creation)
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 5,  Wizard 5,  Wu Jen 5,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> Invisible, mindless, shapeless servants
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
This spell creates a number of unseen servants (see page 297 of the Player's Handbook), up to a maximum of 2d6 +1 servant per level (maximum +15).
Material Component: A small stick crossbar to which many lengths of knotted thread are attached.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium  Oriental Adventures
