<b>Remove Fear</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 271)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 1,  Cleric 1,  Healer 1,  Hunter of the Dead 1,  Knight of the Chalice 1,  Vigilante 1,  Death Delver 1,   Competition 1,  Courage 1,  Pleasure 1,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature plus one additional creature per four levels, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes; see text
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
You instill courage in the subject, granting it a +4 morale bonus against fear effects for 10 minutes. If the subject is under the influence of a fear effect when receiving the spell, that effect is suppressed for the duration of the spell.
	
Remove fear counters and dispels cause fear.
