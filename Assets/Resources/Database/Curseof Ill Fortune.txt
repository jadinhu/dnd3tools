<b>Curseof Ill Fortune</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 2 (Beshaba),    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> One living creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You place a temporary curse upon the target, giving her a &#8211; 3 enhancement penalty on attack rolls, saving throws, ability checks, and skill checks.
Curse of ill fortune is removed by any spell that removes a bestow curse spell.
