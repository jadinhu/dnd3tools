<b>Touch of Madness</b>(Eberron Campaign Setting, p. 116)

Enchantment
 <b>Level:</b>   Madness 2,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 Standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 2 rounds/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
By touching one living creature, you cloud his mind so that he takes no actions. The subject is dazed, not stunned, so attackers get no special advantage against him.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
