<b>Enhance Familiar</b>(Tome and Blood: A Guidebook to Wizards and Sorcerers)

Universal
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Your familiar
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You infuse your familiar with vigor.
While the spell lasts, your familiar receives a +2 competence modifier on attack rolls, saves, and weapon damage.
The familiar also receives a +2 dodge modifier to Armor Class.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Complete Arcane  Spell Compendium
