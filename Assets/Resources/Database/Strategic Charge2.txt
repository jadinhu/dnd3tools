<b>Strategic Charge</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 210)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Blackguard 1,  Paladin 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level

 	
<b>A red nimbus surrounds you as you move smoothly across the crowded battlefield.</b>
	
You gain the benefit of the Mobility feat (PH 98), even if you not meet the prerequisites. You do not have to be charging to gain this benefit.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Magic of Faerun
