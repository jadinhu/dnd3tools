<b>Khelben's Suspended Silence</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Illusion (Glamer)
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> One object
 <b>Duration:</b> Permanent or until discharged; 6 rounds (see text)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None (object)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No (object)

 	
You imbue the touched object with a magical effect.
When you touch the object and give the command word you designate, the object becomes the target of a silence spell.
The effect of the silence spell moves with the object and lasts exactly 6 rounds.
Material Component: A feather and a handful of gem dust worth 50 gp.
