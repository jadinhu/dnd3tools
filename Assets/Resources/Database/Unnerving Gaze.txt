<b>Unnerving Gaze</b>(Book of Vile Darkness)

Illusion (Phantasm)
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 0,  Wizard 0,  Demonologist 1,  Mortal Hunter 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One humanoid creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
The caster makes his face resemble one of the opponent's departed loved ones or bitter enemies.
The subject takes a -1 morale penalty on attack rolls for the duration of the spell.
