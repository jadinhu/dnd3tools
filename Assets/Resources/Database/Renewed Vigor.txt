<b>Renewed Vigor</b>(Player's Handbook II)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 4,  Druid 4,  Bard 5,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 30 ft.
 <b>Effect:</b> 30-ft.-radius burst centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous; see text
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
With a word and a gesture, you suffuse an area with a soft golden light, bestowing a second wind to creatures within.
When you cast this spell, you remove the fatigued condition from any creature in the area and cause exhausted creatures to become fatigued.
In addition, affected creatures gain a +2 bonus to Constitution for 1 round per caster level.
