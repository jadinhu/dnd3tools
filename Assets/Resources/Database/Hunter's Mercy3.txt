<b>Hunter's Mercy</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 117)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Ranger 1,    
<b>Components:</b>S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round

 	
<b>Your eyes glow red, but you see the world as normal except when you look at your target. A small glowing dot reveals the creature's weakest point.</b>
	
This transmutation makes a bow strike true. Your first hit with a bow (not including crossbows) in the next round is automatically a critical hit. If you don't hit in the round following the casting of this spell, the effect is wasted.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Ghostwalk  Magic of Faerun
