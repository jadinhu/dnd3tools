<b>Brilliant Emanation</b>(Book of Exalted Deeds)

Evocation [Good]
 <b>Level:</b>   Sanctified 3,   
<b>Components:</b>Sacrifice
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 100 ft. + 10 ft./level
 <b>Area:</b> 100-ft.-radius emanation + 10-ft. radius per level
 <b>Duration:</b> 1d4 rounds
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude partial
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
This spell causes a divine glow to radiate from any reflective objects worn or carried by the caster, including metal armor.
Evil creatures within the spell's area are blinded unless they succeed on a Fortitude saving throw.
Nonevil characters perceive the brilliant light emanating from the caster, but are not blinded by it and do not suffer any negative effects from it.
Evil characters that make their saving throw are not blinded, but are distracted, taking a -1 penalty on any attacks made within the spell's area for the duration of the spell.
Creatures must be able to see visible light to be affected by this spell.
Sacrifice: 1d3 points of Strength damage.
