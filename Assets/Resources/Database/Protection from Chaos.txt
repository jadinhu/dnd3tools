<b>Protection from Chaos</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 266)

Abjuration [Lawful]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 1,  Paladin 1,  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,  Adept 1,  Runescarred Berserker 1,  Sha'ir 1,  Savant 1 (Divine),   Law 1,  Mechanus 1,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 Standard Action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature Touched
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No; see text

 	
This spell functions like <b>protection from evil</b>, except that the deflection and resistance bonuses apply to attacks from chaotic creatures, and chaotic summoned creatures cannot touch the subject.
