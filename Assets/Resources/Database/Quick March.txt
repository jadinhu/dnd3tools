<b>Quick March</b>(Miniatures Handbook)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 2,  Paladin 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> Allies in a 20-ft.-radius burst
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
Quick march increases your allies' base land speed by 30 feet.
(This adjustment is considered an enhancement bonus).
There is no effect on other modes of movement, such as burrow, climb, fly, or swim.
As with any effect that increases a creature's speed, this spell affects maximum jumping distance.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
