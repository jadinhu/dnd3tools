<b>Shadow Stride</b>(Tome of Battle: The Book of Nine Swords, p. 80)

Shadow Hand [Teleportation]
 <b>Level:</b>  Swordsage 5,    
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 move action
 <b>Range:</b> 50 ft.
 <b>Target:</b> You

 	
<b>You dissipate into a puff of black shadow. A moment later, the shadow coalesces, and you appear fifty feet away.</b>
	
This maneuver functions as the shadow jaunt maneuver, except that it can be initiated as a move action.
