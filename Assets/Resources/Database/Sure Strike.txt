<b>Sure Strike</b>(Player's Handbook II)

Divination
 <b>Level:</b>  Duskblade 2,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round or until discharged

 	
You gain a fleeting glimpse into the future, enough to guide your impending attack.
You cast this spell immediately before you make an attack roll.
You can see into the future for that attack, granting you a +1 insight bonus per three caster levels on your next attack roll.
