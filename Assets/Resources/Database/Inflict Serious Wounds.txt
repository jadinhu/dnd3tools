<b>Inflict Serious Wounds</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 244)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 3, Blackguard 3,  Blighter 3,  Death Master 3,  Dread Necromancer 3,    
<b>Components:</b>

 	
This spell functions like inflict light wounds, except that you deal 3d8 points of damage +1 point per caster level (maximum +15).
