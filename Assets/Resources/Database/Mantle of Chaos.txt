<b>Mantle of Chaos</b>(Planar Handbook)

Abjuration [Chaos]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 min./level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
Flickering yellow chaotic energy surrounds you.
This power grants you spell resistance 12 + caster level against spells with the lawful descriptor.
