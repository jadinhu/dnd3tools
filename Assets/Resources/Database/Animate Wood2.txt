<b>Animate Wood</b>(Oriental Adventures)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Wu Jen 1 (Wood),   Wood 1,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 round
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> One Small or smaller wooden object
 <b>Duration:</b> Concentration, up to 1 round/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
You imbue a Small or smaller object made of wood with mobility and a semblance of life.
The animated wood then attacks whomever or whatever you initially designate.
Statistics for animated objects are found in the Monster Manual.
The spell cannot animate objects carried or worn by a creature.
Material Component: A mixture of powdered cinnabar and ground peach pit.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Complete Arcane  Spell Compendium
