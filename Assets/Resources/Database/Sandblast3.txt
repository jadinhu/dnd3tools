<b>Sandblast</b>(Masters of the Wild: A Guidebook to Barbarians, Druids, and Rangers)

Evocation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> 10 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> Semicircular burst of sand 10 ft. long, centered on your hands
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Reflex half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You fire a hail of hot sand from your fingers, dealing 1d6 points of subdual damage to creatures in the arc.
(See the illustration in the burning hands spell description in the Player's Handbook for details of the arc).
Any creature that fails its Reflex save is also stunned for 1 round.
