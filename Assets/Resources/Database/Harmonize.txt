<b>Harmonize</b>(Races of Stone)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 min./level (D)

 	
You blend magic and music seamlessly.
While this spell is in effect, starting a bardic music effect requires only a move action.
Concentrating on a bardic music effect that requires concentration still requires a standard action.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Song and Silence: A Guidebook to Bards and Rogues
