<b>Construct Energy Ward, Greater</b>(Eberron Campaign Setting, p. 109)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Artificer 4,   Warforged 5,   
<b>Components:</b>S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 round
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Construct touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes/level or until discharged
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
As <b>protection from energy</b>, except that the target must be a construct.
