<b>Wind Stride</b>(Tome of Battle: The Book of Nine Swords, p. 56)

Desert Wind (Boost)
 <b>Level:</b>  Swordsage 1,    
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> End of turn

 	
<b>A warm breeze swirls about you as you move speedily away.</b>
	
The desert wind envelops you and carries you across the battlefield, giving you a burst of speed to move around and through your enemies. Until the end of your turn, you gain a +10-foot enhancement bonus to your land speed.
