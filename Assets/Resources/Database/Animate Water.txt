<b>Animate Water</b>(Complete Arcane)

Transmutation [Water]
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 1,  Wu Jen 1 (Water),    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> Up to a 5-ft. cube of water

 	
As animate wood, but you can animate a quantity of water up to the maximum volume.
Water animated by this spell has hardness 0, but has double the normal hit points that an animated object of the same size would have.
Material Component: A vial of pure spring water mixed with cinnabar oil.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Oriental Adventures  Spell Compendium
