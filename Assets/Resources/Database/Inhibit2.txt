<b>Inhibit</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 123)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 1,  Cleric 1,  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>You cast this spell and a spiral of dark motes surrounds your foe, slowing and distracting him from the task at hand.</b>
	
You inhibit your foe from acting. The subject is forced to delay until the following round, acting immediately before you on your initiative count.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Miniatures Handbook
