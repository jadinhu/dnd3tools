<b>Mage Armor, Greater</b>(Complete Arcane)

Conjuration (Creation) [Force]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
This spell functions like mage armor, except that its tangible field of force provides a +6 armor bonus to Armor Class.
Material Component: A tiny platinum shield worth 25 gp.
