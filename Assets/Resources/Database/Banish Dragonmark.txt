<b>Banish Dragonmark</b>(Dragonmarked, p. 148)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 5,  Wizard 5,  Sorcerer 5,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, Dragonmark
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 Standard Action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25ft. + 5ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One dragonmarked creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 day/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will Negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
If the subject fails its save, its dragonmark i stripped away for the spell's duration. The affected subject cannot use any of its dragonmarked spell-like abilities (or any other abilities that rely on dragonmarks) during this time.
If the target creature's dragonmark is one step more powerful than yours, that creature it gains a +2 bonus to its Will save. If the target creature's dragonmark is two steps more powerful than yours, that creature it gains a +4 bonus to its Will save. 
	
<b>Material Component.</b> A pinch of Eberron dragonshard dust.
	
<b>Dragonmark:</b> Any true or aberrant dragonmark.
