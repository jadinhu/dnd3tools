<b>Earthfast</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Area:</b> One stone structure or rock formation, up to 25 cu. ft./level
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates (harmless, object)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (object)

 	
You reinforce a rock formation or stone structure.
The earthfast spell doubles the structure's hit points and hardness.
The spell collapses if you move beyond the range of the spell.
This does not function on constructs.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
