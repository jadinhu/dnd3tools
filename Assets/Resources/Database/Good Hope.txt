<b>Good Hope</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 237)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 3,  Wu Jen 4,   Charm 4,  Joy 4,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> One living creature/level, no two of which may be more than 30 ft. apart
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 min./level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
This spell instills powerful hope in the subjects.
Each affected creature gains a +2 morale bonus on saving throws, attack rolls, ability checks, skill checks, and weapon damage rolls.
Good hope counters and dispels crushing despair.
