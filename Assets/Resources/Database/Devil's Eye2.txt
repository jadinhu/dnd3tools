<b>Devil's Eye</b>(Fiendish Codex II: Tyrants of the Nine Hells)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Blackguard 2,  Cleric 3,  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,   Diabolic 2,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level (D)

 	
With a few words and a gesture, the darkness melts away, and you can see with perfect clarity.
You gain the visual acuity of a devil.
You can see in darkness and magical darkness out to 30 feet.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Book of Vile Darkness
