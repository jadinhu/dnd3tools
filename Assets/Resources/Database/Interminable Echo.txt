<b>Interminable Echo</b>(Complete Mage)

Illusion (Phantasm) [Mind-Affecting, Sonic]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 5 rounds
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will partial
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
The deep tolling of a bell echoes within the mind of your foe.
You fill the target's mind with a barrage of illusory sound.
The subject takes a -10 penalty on Listen checks and 2d6 points of sonic damage each round.
A successful Will save reduces the duration to 1 round.
