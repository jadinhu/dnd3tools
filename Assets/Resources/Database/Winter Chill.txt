<b>Winter Chill</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 241)

Transmutation [Cold]
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>You makes a sound like whistling wind and inscribe a snowflake shape in the air, willing a chill to overcome your foe.</b>
	
The target creature must succeed on a Fortitude save or take 1d6 points of cold damage and become fatigued. A creature with immunity to cold is not affected by this spell.
