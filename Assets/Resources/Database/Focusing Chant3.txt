<b>Focusing Chant</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 96)

Enchantment (Compulsion)
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute (D)

 	
<b>You chant softly under your breath and concentrate on the sound of your voice. Distractions fade from your consciousness, allowing you to focus on the task at hand.</b>
	
You gain a +1 circumstance bonus on attack rolls, skill checks, and ability checks for the duration of the spell.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Complete Adventurer  Song and Silence: A Guidebook to Bards and Rogues
