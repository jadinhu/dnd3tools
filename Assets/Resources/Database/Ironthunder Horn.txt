<b>Ironthunder Horn</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 126)

Transmutation [Sonic]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 1,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 30 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> Cone-shaped burst
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Reflex negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>The last words of your spell boom out with a thundering roar, knocking the foes in your path off their feet.</b>
	
You create a deep resonant vibration that can shake creatures off their feet. Creatures in the area must succeed on a Reflex saving throw or fall prone.
