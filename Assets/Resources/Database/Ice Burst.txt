<b>Ice Burst</b>(Tome and Blood: A Guidebook to Wizards and Sorcerers)

Evocation [Cold]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Area:</b> 30-ft.-radius burst
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Reflex half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
A hail of magical ice chunks erupts from a point in space.
The chunks fly in all directions, hitting everything within the burst.
The pelting ice deals 1d4 points of cold damage +1 point of blunt damage per caster level, to a maximum of 10d4+10.
Material Component: A chunk of ice or a single pearl.
