<b>Leonal's Roar</b>(Book of Exalted Deeds)

Evocation [Good, Sonic]
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 8,    
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 40 ft.
 <b>Target:</b> Nongood creatures in a 40-ft.-radius spread centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude partial
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
This spell has the effect of a holy word, and it additionally deals 2d6 points of sonic damage to nongood creatures in the area.
A successful Fortitude saving throw negates the sonic damage, but not the other effects of the spell.
