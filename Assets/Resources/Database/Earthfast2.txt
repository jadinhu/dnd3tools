<b>Earthfast</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 76)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Area:</b> One stone structure or rock formation, up to 25 cubic ft./level
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (object)

 	
<b>Gray bands extend from your fingertips, wrapping themselves around the wall like a brace strengthening it for the coming assault.</b>
	
You reinforce a rock formation or stone structure. The <b>earthfast</b> spell doubles the structure's hit points and increases its hardness to 10. This spell does not function on constructs.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Magic of Faerun
