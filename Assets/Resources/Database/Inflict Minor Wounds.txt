<b>Inflict Minor Wounds</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 244)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 0,  Blighter 0,  Death Master 0,    
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates

 	
This spell functions like inflict light wounds, except that you deal 1 point of damage and a Will save negates the damage instead of halving it.
