<b>Aerial Alarm</b>(Heroes of Battle)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Ranger 2,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, AF, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Area:</b> 100-ft.-radius cylinder, 500 ft. high
 <b>Duration:</b> 2 hours/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
As alarm (see Player's Handbook page 197), except the area is a cylinder that extends 500 feet into the air and the alarm only sounds when a flying creature of Medium or larger size enters or touches the warded area.
Arcane Focus: A silver bell and an eagle's feather.
