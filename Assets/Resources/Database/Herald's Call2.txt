<b>Herald's Call</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting, Sonic]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 1,  Harper Scout 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> 30 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> 30-ft.-radius burst centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You produce a crowd-stopping shout that holds an air of authority others find difficult to ignore.
The spell affects only those creatures that have 5 HD or less.
Anyone affected is dazed for 1 round.
Creatures beyond the radius of the burst may hear the shout, but don't become dazed.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium  Complete Adventurer
