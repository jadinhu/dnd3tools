<b>Divine Protection</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 70)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 2,  Paladin 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> Allies in a 20-ft. radius burst
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
<b>You call upon your holy powers to aid your companions and a golden light appears from above, bathing them with sparkling radiance.</b>
	
Allies gain a +1 morale bonus to their Armor Class and on saving throws.
