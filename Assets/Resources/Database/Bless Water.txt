<b>Bless Water</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 205)

Transmutation [Good]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 1,  Paladin 1,  Healer 1,  Knight of the Chalice 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 minute
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Flask of water touched
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (object)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (object)

 	
This transmutation imbues a flask (1 pint) of water with positive energy, turning it into holy water (page 128).
	
Material Component: 5 pounds of powdered silver (worth 25 gp).
