<b>Cure Moderate Wounds, Mass</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 216)

Conjuration (Healing)
 <b>Level:</b>  Healer 5,  Bard 6,  Cleric 6,  Druid 7,  Urban Druid 7,    
<b>Components:</b>

 	
This spell functions like <b>mass cure light wounds</b>, except that it cures 2d8 points of damage +1 point per caster level (maximum +30).
