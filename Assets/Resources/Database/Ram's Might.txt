<b>Ram's Might</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 0,  Ranger 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level

 	
You add extra power to your unarmed attacks.
Your hands harden until they are similar to a ram's horn in texture and hardness.
Your unarmed attacks inflict normal (not subdual) damage.
You are considered armed.
The hardness has no other effect; you can cast spells and manipulate objects normally.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
