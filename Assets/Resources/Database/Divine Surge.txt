<b>Divine Surge</b>(Tome of Battle: The Book of Nine Swords, p. 58)

Devoted Spirit (Strike)
 <b>Level:</b>  Crusader 4 (One Devoted Spirit maneuver),    
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Melee attack
 <b>Target:</b> One creature

 	
<b>Your body shakes and spasms as unfettered divine energy courses through it. This power sparks off your weapon and courses into your foe, devastating your enemy but leaving you drained.</b>
	
As part of this maneuver, you make a single melee attack that deals an extra 8d8 points of damage.
