<b>Smell of Fear</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Ranger 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You bestow your target with an aroma that attracts predators.
The smell triples the chance of encountering wandering monsters in the area.
Attracted creatures won't necessarily attack the target to the exclusion of others; they're just attracted to the area.
