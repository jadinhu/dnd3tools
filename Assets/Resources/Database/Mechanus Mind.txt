<b>Mechanus Mind</b>(Planar Handbook)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Lawful, Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 min./level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
Like entities that inhabit the plane of Mechanus, the subject of Mechanus mind temporarily becomes a being of focused order.
The subject gains a +4 bonus on Will saving throws to resist mind-affecting spells.
While in the grip of Mechanus mind, the subject also becomes more analytical and less emotional.
The subject gains a +2 bonus on all Intelligence-based checks, but takes a -2 penalty on Charisma-based checks.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
