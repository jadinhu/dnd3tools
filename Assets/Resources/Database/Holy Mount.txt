<b>Holy Mount</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 115)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Paladin 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Your special mount
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
<b>Touching your special mount, you feel the divine power seep into your trusted friend and watch as a golden nimbus surrounds it.</b>
	
Your special mount gains the celestial template (MM 31) for the duration of the spell.
