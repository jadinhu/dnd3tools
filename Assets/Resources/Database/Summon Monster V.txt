<b>Summon Monster V</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 286)

Conjuration (Summoning) [see text for summon monster I]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 5,  Cleric 5,  Sorcerer 5,  Wizard 5,  Nar Demonbinder 5,  Wu Jen 5,  Sha'ir 5,   Celestia 5,  Abyss 5,  Arborea 5,  Baator 5,  Summoner 5,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, AF, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 round
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> One or more summoned creatures, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
This spell functions like <b>summon monster I</b>, except that you can summon one creature from the 5th-level list, 1d3 creatures of the same kind from the 4th-level list, or 1d4+1 creatures of the same kind from a lower-level list.
	
Arcane Focus: A tiny bag and a small (not necessarily lit) candle.
	<b>Special</b>	
When casting <b>Summon Monster V</b> as a Abyss Domain spell, only Chaotic Evil creatures may be summoned.
When casting <b>Summon Monster V</b> as a Arborea Domain spell, only Chaotic Good creatures may be summoned.
When casting <b>Summon Monster V</b> as a Baator Domain spell, only Lawful Evil creatures may be summoned.
When casting <b>Summon Monster V</b> as a Celestia Domain spell, only Lawful Good creatures may be summoned.
