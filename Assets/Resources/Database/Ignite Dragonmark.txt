<b>Ignite Dragonmark</b>(Dragonmarked, p. 152)

Transmutation [Fire]
 <b>Level:</b>  Artificer 2,  Cleric 2,  Druid 2,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, Dragonmark
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> One dragonmarked creature
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will half; see text
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>With a soft utterance and a sharp thrust of your hand, you cause the target's dragonmark to burst into flame.</b>
	
The target's dragonmark ignites momentarily, dealing it 1d6 points of fire damage per caster level (maximum 5d6). In addition, the target cannot use any of its dragonmark powers for 1 round per 5 points of damage taken (rounded down).
	
<b>Dragonmark:</b> Any true or aberrant dragonmark.
