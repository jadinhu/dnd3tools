<b>Sandblast</b>(Complete Divine)

Evocation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 10 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> Semicircular burst of sand 10 ft. long, centered on your hands
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Reflex half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You fire a hail of hot sand from your fingers, dealing 1d6 points of nonlethal damage to creatures in the arc.
Any creature that fails its Reflex save is also stunned for 1 round.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Lost Empires of Faerun  Spell Compendium  Masters of the Wild: A Guidebook to Barbarians, Druids, and Rangers
