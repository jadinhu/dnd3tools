<b>Disrupting Weapon</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 223)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Hunter of the Dead 4,  Cleric 5,  Knight of the Weave 5,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> One melee weapon
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless, object); see text
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless, object)

 	
This spell makes a melee weapon deadly to undead. Any undead creature with HD equal to or less than your caster level must succeed on a Will save or be destroyed utterly if struck in combat with this weapon. Spell resistance does not apply against the destruction effect.
