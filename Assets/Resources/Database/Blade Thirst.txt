<b>Blade Thirst</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Ranger 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> One slashing weapon
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None (object)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (object)

 	
You grant a slashing weapon a +3 enhancement bonus.
The weapon takes on a blue, fiery glow, shedding illumination as if it were a torch.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
