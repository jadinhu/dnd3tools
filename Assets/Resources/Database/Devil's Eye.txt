<b>Devil's Eye</b>(Book of Vile Darkness)

Divination [Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Blackguard 2,  Cleric 3,  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,   Diabolic 2,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> Caster
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level

 	
The caster gains the visual acuity of a devil.
He can see not only in darkness, but also in magical darkness, with a range of 30 feet.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Fiendish Codex II: Tyrants of the Nine Hells
