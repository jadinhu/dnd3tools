<b>Ectoplasmic Enhancement</b>(Book of Vile Darkness)

Necromancy [Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 6,  Wizard 6,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 full round
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One incorporeal undead/level
 <b>Duration:</b> 24 hours
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
The undead affected by this spell gain a +1 deflection bonus to Armor Class, +1d8 temporary hit points, a +1 enhancement bonus on attack rolls, and a +2 bonus to turn resistance.
Each of these enhancements increases by +1 for every three caster levels.
So a 12th-level caster grants a +5 deflection bonus to AC, an extra 1d8+4 temporary hit points, a +5 enhancement bonus on attack rolls, and a +6 bonus to turn resistance.
