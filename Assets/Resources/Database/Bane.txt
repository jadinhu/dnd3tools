<b>Bane</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 203)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Fear, Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 1,  Dread Necromancer 1,  Blighter 1,  Death Master 1,  Jester 1,  Savant 1 (Divine),  Demonologist 1,   Baator 1,  Suffering 1,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 50 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> All enemies within 50 ft.
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 min./level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
Bane fills your enemies with fear and doubt. Each affected creature takes a -1 penalty on attack rolls and a -1 penalty on saving throws against fear effects. 
	
Bane counters and dispels bless.
