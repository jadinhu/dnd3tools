<b>Aspect of the Icy Hunter</b>(Complete Mage)

Transmutation (Polymorph)
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level (D)

 	
Thick ivory fur grows from your skin and your back hunches over
until you stand on four paws.
Cold, blue eyes stare out of your now-canine face.
You take the form of a winter wolf (MM 256).
You gain 20 temporary hit points, which disappear at the end of the spell's duration.
See the Polymorph Subschool sidebar on page 91 for more details.
