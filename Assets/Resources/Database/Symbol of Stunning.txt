<b>Symbol of Stunning</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 291)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 7,  Sorcerer 7,  Wizard 7,  Wu Jen 7,  Sha'ir 7,  Death Master 7,    
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates

 	
This spell functions like symbol of death, except that all creatures within 60 feet of a symbol of stunning instead become stunned for 1d6 rounds.
Note:Magic traps such as symbol of stunning are hard to detect and disable.
A rogue (only) can use the Search skill to find a symbol of stunning and Disable Device to thwart it.
The DC in each case is 25 + spell level, or 32 for symbol of stunning.
Material Component:Mercury and phosphorus, plus powdered diamond and opal with a total value of at least 5,000 gp.
