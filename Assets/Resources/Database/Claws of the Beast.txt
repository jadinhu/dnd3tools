<b>Claws of the Beast</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 1,  Ranger 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level

 	
Your fingers sprout claws.
Your hands become weapons that do 1d6 points of damage.
If your normal unarmed damage is greater than 1d6, the damage does not change.
You are considered armed while this spell is in effect.
