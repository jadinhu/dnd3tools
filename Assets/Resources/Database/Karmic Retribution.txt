<b>Karmic Retribution</b>(Complete Mage)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Hexblade 4,  Sorcerer 6,  Wizard 6,    
<b>Components:</b>

 	
By manipulating fate itself, you ensure that any who attack you suffer greatly for their insolence.
	
This spell functions like <b>karmic aura</b>, except that any creature that damages you and fails its Will save becomes stunned for 1 round.
