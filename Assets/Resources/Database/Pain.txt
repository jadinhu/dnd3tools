<b>Pain</b>(Complete Arcane)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Wu Jen 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> Up to 1 HD/level of creatures, no two of which can be more than 20 ft. apart
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude partial
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
The target creatures are struck by wracking pains and agony, giving them a -4 penalty on attack rolls, skill checks, and ability checks.
A successful Fortitude save lessens this penalty to -2.
Material Component: A live leech.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Oriental Adventures
