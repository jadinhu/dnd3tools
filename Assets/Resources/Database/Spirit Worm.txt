<b>Spirit Worm</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Living creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level (see text)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude partial
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You create a lingering decay in the spirit and body of the target.
If the target fails its saving throw, it takes 1 point of temporary Constitution damage each round while the spell lasts (maximum 5 points of Constitution).
If it makes its save, it does not lose any Constitution but takes 1d2 points of damage each round while the spell lasts (maximum 5d2).
The damage remains after the spell ends.
Material Component: A piece of fire-blackened ivory or bone carved in the shape of a segmented worm.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
