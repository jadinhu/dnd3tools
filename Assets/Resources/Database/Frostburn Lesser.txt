<b>Frostburn, Lesser</b>(Frostburn)

Necromancy [Cold]
 <b>Level:</b>  Disciple of Thrym 1,  Cleric 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
When laying your hand upon a creature, you channel cold energy that deals 1d12 points of frostburn damage +1 point per caster level (maximum +5).
When cast upon a cold subtype creature, this spell heals a like amount of damage, rather than harming it.
