<b>Wave of Pain</b>(Book of Vile Darkness)

Necromancy [Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 6,   Pain 7,   
<b>Components:</b>S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Area:</b> Cone
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/2 levels
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
All living creatures within the cone are overcome with pain and suffering.
They are stunned for the duration of the spell.
A creature with no discernible anatomy is unaffected by this spell.
Material Component: A needle.
