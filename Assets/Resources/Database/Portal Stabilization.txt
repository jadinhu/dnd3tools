<b>Portal Stabilization</b>(Underdark, p. 60)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>   Portal (Alternative) 1,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 round
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels).
 <b>Target:</b> One portal
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
You temporarily stabilize a malfunctioning portal,making it safer for use.
Any time the portal is activated during the duration of this spell (or immediately, if it is continuously active), add +30% to the required roll on Table 2-2: Portal Malfunction in the <span class="caps">FORGOTTEN</span> <span class="caps">REALMS</span> Campaign Setting.
