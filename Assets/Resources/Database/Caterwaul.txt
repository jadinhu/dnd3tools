<b>Caterwaul</b>(Complete Mage)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting, Sonic]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 30 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> Cone-shaped burst
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will partial
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You loose an earsplitting shriek that induces revulsion.
You draw upon your primal emotions to emit a stomach-churning wail.
Creatures in the area become nauseated for 1 round; those that make successful Will saves become sickened for 1 round instead.
