<b>Stone Body</b>(Races of Faerun)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 6 (gnome),  Sorcerer 6,  Wizard 6,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level (D)

 	
As iron body, except as follows.
You gain damage reduction 20/+2.
You are vulnerable to all special attacks that affect stone golems.
You gain a +4 enhancement bonus to Strength and a -4 penalty to Dexterity (to a minimum Dexterity score of 1).
Your speed is reduced to half normal.
Your weight increases by a factor of three.
Arcane Material Component: A small piece of stone that was once part of either a stone golem, a greater earth elemental, or a castle's outer wall.
