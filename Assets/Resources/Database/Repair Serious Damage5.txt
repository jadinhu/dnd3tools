<b>Repair Serious Damage</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 173)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> One construct
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
<b>With a touch, you mold the battered construct's form to be more like it was on its day of creation.</b>
	
This spell functions like <b>repair light damage</b>, except that you repair 3d8 points of damage +1 point per caster level (maximum +15) to a construct.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Eberron Campaign Setting  Complete Arcane  Tome and Blood: A Guidebook to Wizards and Sorcerers  Miniatures Handbook  Dragon Compendium
