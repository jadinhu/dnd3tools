<b>Dance of Ruin</b>(Book of Vile Darkness)

Necromancy [Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 2,  Cleric 2,  Demonologist 2,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 full round
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Area:</b> Spread centered on caster
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Reflex half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
To cast this spell, the caster dances wildly and chants.
After she finishes her dance, a wave of crackling energy flashes outward up to the extent of the range.
All nondemon creatures within the area take 2d20 points of damage.
