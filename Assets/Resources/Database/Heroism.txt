<b>Heroism</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 240)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 2,  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,  Sha'ir 3,   Arborea 3,  Courage 3,  Pride 3,  Revered Ancestor 3,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 min./level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
This spell imbues a single creature with great bravery and morale in battle. The target gains a +2 morale bonus on attack rolls, saves, and skill checks.
