<b>Deeper Darkvision</b>(Underdark)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,  Prime Underdark Guide 4,  Ranger 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
The subject gains the ability to see 60 feet in total darkness and even magical darkness (such as might be created by a blacklight or deeper darkness spell).
Deeper darkvision is black and white only but otherwise similar to normal sight.
Material Component: A pinch of dried carrot or an agate.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
