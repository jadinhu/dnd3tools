<b>Inflict Moderate Wounds, Mass</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 244)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 6,  Death Master 6,  Dread Necromancer 6,    
<b>Components:</b>

 	
This spell functions like <b>mass inflict light wounds</b>, except that it deals 2d8 points of damage +1 point per caster level (maximum +30).
