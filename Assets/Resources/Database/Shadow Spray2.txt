<b>Shadow Spray</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 186)

Illusion (Shadow)
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Area:</b> 5-ft.-radius burst
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>As you finish casting this spell, ribbonlike shadows burst outward from the midst of your foes.</b>
	
You cause a multitude of ribbonlike shadows to instantaneously explode outward from the point of origin. Creatures in the area take 4 points of Strength damage and are dazed for 1 round.
	
Material Component: A handful of black ribbons.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Forgotten Realms Campaign Setting
