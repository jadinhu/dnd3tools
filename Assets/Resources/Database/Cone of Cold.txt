<b>Cone of Cold</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 212)

Evocation [Cold]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 5,  Wizard 5,  Warmage 5,  Wu Jen 5 (Water),  Sha'ir 5,  Death Master 5,   Blightbringer 5,  Water 6,  Cold 6,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 60 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> Cone-shaped burst
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Reflex half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
Cone of cold creates an area of extreme cold, originating at your hand and extending outward in a cone.
It drains heat, dealing 1d6 points of cold damage per caster level (maximum 15d6).
Arcane Material Component: A very small crystal or glass cone.
