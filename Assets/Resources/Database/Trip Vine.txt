<b>Trip Vine</b>(Heroes of Battle)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 2,  Ranger 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Area:</b> One 10-ft. square/level (S)
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Reflex negates; see text
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
Trip vine causes plants within the area to grow together to form a tangle.
Any creature entering an affected square must succeed on a Reflex save or fall prone.
Any creature charging into an affected square takes a -2 penalty on its saving throw.
If this spell is cast on an area of undergrowth (see page 87 of the Dungeon Master's Guide), saves against the spell take a -2 penalty.
