<b>Rapid Burrowing</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 166)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 1,  Ranger 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
<b>Splaying your fingers like a mole's forepaws completes the spell. With your touch, the target's digging appendages increase in size and sharpness.</b>
	
This spell increases the touched creature's burrow speed by 20 feet. It has no effect on other modes of movement, nor does it grant the subject a burrow speed or the ability to burrow through stone if the creature cannot already do so.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Savage Species
