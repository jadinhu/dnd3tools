<b>Consumptive Field, Greater</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 51)

Necromancy [Death, Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 7,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 30 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> 30-ft.-radius emanation centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
This spell functions like <b>consumptive field</b>, except that the field affects all creatures in the area with 9 hit points or fewer. Such creatures that fail their saving throws die, and creatures that fall to 9 hit points or lower in the area after the spell is cast are likewise subject to its effect.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Libris Mortis: The Book of the Dead
