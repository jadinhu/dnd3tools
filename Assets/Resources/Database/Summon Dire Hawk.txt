<b>Summon Dire Hawk</b>(Races of the Wild)

Conjuration (Summoning)
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 2,   Sky 2,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 round
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> One summoned dire hawk
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
This spell summons a dire hawk (see page 189).
It appears where you designate and acts immediately, on your turn.
You may command the dire hawk telepathically as a free action, allowing you to direct its actions as you desire.
