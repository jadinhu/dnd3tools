<b>Righteous Fury</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 177)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Paladin 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level

 	
<b>You pull a holy aura about you that glows a golden red.</b>
	
Summoning the power of your deity, you charge yourself with positive energy. This gives you 5 temporary hit points per caster level (maximum 50) and a +4 sacred bonus to Strength. These temporary hit points last for up to 1 hour.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Miniatures Handbook  Magic of Faerun
