<b>Draconic Might</b>(Draconomicon)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Paladin 4,  Sorcerer 5,  Wizard 5,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Living creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
The subject of the spell gains a +5 enhancement bonus to Strength, Constitution, and Charisma.
It also gains a +4 enhancement bonus to natural armor.
Finally, it has immunity to magic sleep and paralysis effects.
Special: Sorcerers cast this spell at +1 caster level.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
