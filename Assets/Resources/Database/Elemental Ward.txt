<b>Elemental Ward</b>(Complete Arcane)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Wu Jen 4 (All),    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 60 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> 60-ft.-radius emanation, centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
This spell allows you to drive off elementals of a specific type by uttering a fearsome cry.
When you cast the spell, any elemental within the spell's area must leave the area unless it makes a successful Will save.
If you try to force the barrier against an elemental that has failed its saving throw, the spell fails.
