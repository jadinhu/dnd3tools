<b>Elemental Ward</b>(Oriental Adventures)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Wu Jen 4 (All),   Nature 4,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> 60 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> 60-ft. emanation, centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
This spell allows you to drive off elementals of a specific type by uttering a fearsome cry.
When you cast the spell, all elementals within the spell's area flee the area unless they make successful Will saving throws.
The spell ends if you try to force the barrier against an elemental that has failed its saving throw.
