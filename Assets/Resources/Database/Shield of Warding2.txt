<b>Shield of Warding</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 188)

Abjuration [Good]
 <b>Level:</b>  Paladin 2,  Cleric 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> One shield or buckler touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (object, harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
<b>You press your palm against the shield and speak the words of power. A silver radiance, in the shape of your holy symbol spreads over the shield.</b>
	
The touched shield or buckler grants its wielder a +1 sacred bonus to Armor Class and on Reflex saves, +1 per five caster levels (maximum +5 at 20th level). The bonus applies only when the shield is worn or carried normally (but not, for instance, if it is slung over the shoulder).
