<b>Evil Eye</b>(Book of Vile Darkness)

Enchantment [Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Mortal Hunter 2,  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous (see text)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
The caster focuses malevolent wishes through her gaze and curses someone with bad luck.
The subject takes a -4 luck penalty on all attack rolls, saves, and checks.
The spell ends at the next sunrise, when dismissed, when a remove curse is cast on the subject, or when the caster takes at least 1 point of damage from the subject.
