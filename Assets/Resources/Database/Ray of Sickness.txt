<b>Ray of Sickness</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 167)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> Ray
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>Holding your outstretched hand palm down, you fire a sickly green ray. Your hand feels cold and clammy for a few seconds after the ray flies forth.</b>
	
You must succeed on a ranged touch attack with the ray to strike a target. If your ranged touch attack hits, the subject becomes sickened.
