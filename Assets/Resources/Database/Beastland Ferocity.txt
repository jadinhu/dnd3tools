<b>Beastland Ferocity</b>(Planar Handbook)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 1,  Druid 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 min./level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
You unleash the savage will to survive inherent in every creature.
The subject becomes such a tenacious combatant that it continues to fight without penalty even while disabled or dying.
If the creature is reduced to -10 hit points, it dies normally.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
