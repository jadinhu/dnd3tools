<b>Sonic Orb, Lesser</b>(Miniatures Handbook, p. 39)

Conjuration (Creation) [Sonic]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,  Warmage 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> One orb of sonic energy
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
This spell functions like lesser acid orb, except that it deals from 1d6 to 5d6 points of sonic damage instead of 1d8 to 5d8 points of acid damage.
