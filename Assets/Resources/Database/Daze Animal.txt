<b>Daze Animal</b>(Masters of the Wild: A Guidebook to Barbarians, Druids, and Rangers)

Enchantment [Compulsion, Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 0,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One Medium-size or smaller animal with less than 5 HD
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
This enchantment clouds the target animal's mind.
The subject is not stunned, so attackers get no special advantage against it, but it cannot move or attack.
