<b>Wall of Greater Dispel Magic</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 233)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 8,  Sorcerer 8,  Wizard 8,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> A straight wall whose area is up to one 10-ft. square/level
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
<b>You cast the spell and a translucent wall springs up, its shimmering field pulsing with strong, malignant shades than spin and twist into each other.</b>
	
This spell functions like <b>wall of dispel magic</b>, except that the effect is that of <b>greater dispel magic</b> (PH 223).
