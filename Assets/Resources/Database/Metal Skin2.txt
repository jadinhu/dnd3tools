<b>Metal Skin</b>(Oriental Adventures)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Wu Jen 5 (Metal),    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
This spell transforms the skin of the recipient into a gleaming metallic surface.
The recipient's natural armor bonus becomes +8 (unless it was already better than +8), but the creature also becomes somewhat slow and stiff, suffering a -2 penalty to effective Dexterity for the duration of the spell.
Material Component: A small piece of rhinoceros hide.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Complete Arcane
