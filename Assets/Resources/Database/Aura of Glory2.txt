<b>Aura of Glory</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 18)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Paladin 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Area:</b> 10-ft.-radius spread
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
<b>You invoke the holy words and a soft golden light radiates from you. You feel surer of yourself, bolstered by the power of your beliefs.</b>
	
You channel divine power into yourself, spreading glory to your comrades. This spell removes any fear effect from all allies within your aura of courage.
