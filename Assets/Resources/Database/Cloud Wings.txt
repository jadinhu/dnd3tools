<b>Cloud Wings</b>(Savage Species)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action)
 <b>Range:</b> Tough
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes(harmless)

 	
This spell increases the subject's fly speed by 30 feet.
It has no effect on other modes of movement, nor does it allow the subject to fly if it cannot already do so.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
