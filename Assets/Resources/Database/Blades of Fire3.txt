<b>Blades of Fire</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 31)

Conjuration (Creation) [Fire]
 <b>Level:</b>  Ranger 1,  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Up to two melee weapons you are wielding
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
<b>With a word your weapons burst into flame. You feel no heat and the flames merely tickle your skin.</b>
	
Your melee weapons each deal an extra 1d8 points of fire damage. This damage stacks with any energy damage your weapons already deal.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Miniatures Handbook  Complete Arcane
