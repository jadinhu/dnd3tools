<b>Invisibility Purge</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 245)

Evocation
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 3,  Knight of the Chalice 3,  Savant 3 (Divine),    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 min./level (D)

 	
You surround yourself with a sphere of power with a radius of 5 feet per caster level that negates all forms of invisibility. Anything invisible becomes visible while in the area.
