<b>Mummify</b>(Sandstorm)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 6,  Druid 6,  Sorcerer 6,  Wizard 6,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> One living creature
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude partial; see text
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You flash-dry a creature, killing it and preserving it in an instantaneous mummification process.
A creature that makes a successful Fortitude saving throw instead takes 6d6 points of dessication damage and is dehydrated.
If the damage kills the target, it is still mummified.
Arcane Material Component: A strip of salted cloth.
