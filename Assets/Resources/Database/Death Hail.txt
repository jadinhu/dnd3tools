<b>Death Hail</b>(Frostburn)

Conjuration (Creation) [Cold, Death]
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 6,   Winter 6,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 round
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Area:</b> Cylinder (40-ft radius, 20 ft. high)
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
You call into being an intense storm of death hail in the area you designate.
Creatures in the area must succeed on a Fortitude save or take 1d2 points of Strength and Constitution damage.
