<b>Inhibit</b>(Miniatures Handbook)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 1,  Cleric 1,  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
The caster inhibits his foe from acting.
The subject is forced to delay until the following round, acting immediately before the caster on the caster's initiative count.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
