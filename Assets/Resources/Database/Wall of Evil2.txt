<b>Wall of Evil</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 233)

Abjuration [Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 4,  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> A straight wall whose area is up to one 10-ft. square/level or a sphere or hemisphere with a radius of up to 5 ft./2 levels
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> See text
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>You invoke the abjuration and a wall of red and black energy springs up, only to fade away to invisibility.</b>
	
This spell functions like <b>wall of good</b>, except that good creatures are blocked.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Magic of Faerun
