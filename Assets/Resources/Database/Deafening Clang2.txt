<b>Deafening Clang</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 59)

Transmutation [Sonic]
 <b>Level:</b>  Paladin 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Your weapon
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude partial; see text
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
<b>At your touch, the weapon rings like a struck tuning fork. The sound diminishes until it's imperceptible, but when you touch the weapon you can feel the vibration.</b>
	
You empower the touched weapon with magic that causes it to emit a loud clang when it is struck against a hard surface, such as a floor, wall, or creature. The weapon deals 1d6 points of sonic damage with each successful hit, and any creature struck by the weapon must succeed on a Fortitude saving throw or be deafened for 1 minute.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Magic of Faerun
