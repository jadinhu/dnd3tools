<b>Orb of Force</b>(Complete Arcane)

Conjuration (Creation) [Force]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,  Warmage 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Effect:</b> One orb of force
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
You create a globe of force 3 inches across, which streaks from your palm toward your target.
You must succeed on a ranged touch attack to hit the target.
The orb deals a total of 1d6 points of damage per caster level (maximum 10d6).
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
