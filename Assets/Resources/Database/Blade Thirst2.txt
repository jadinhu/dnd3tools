<b>Blade Thirst</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 31)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Ranger 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 Swift Action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> 1 Slashing Weapon
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None (object)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (object)

 	
<b>You touch a blade, and it catches fire. Unearthly blue flames crackle along its length while the weapon beneath remains cold and unharmed.</b>
	
You grant a slashing weapon a 3 enhancement bonus. The weapon sheds illumination as if it were a torch.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Magic of Faerun
