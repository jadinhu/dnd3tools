<b>Accelerated Movement</b>(Complete Adventurer)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 1,  Ranger 1,  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,  Vigilante 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level (D)

 	
While this spell is in effect, you can move at your normal speed when using Balance, Climb, or Move Silently without taking any penalty on your check.
	
This spell does not affect the penalty for using these skills while running or charging.
	
Material Component: A dead cockroach.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
