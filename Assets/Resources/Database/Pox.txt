<b>Pox</b>(Book of Vile Darkness)

Necromancy [Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 6,   Corruption 6,  Pain 6,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> Up to one living creature/level, no two of which can be more than 10 ft. apart
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
Subjects take 1d4 points of Constitution drain.
Their skin breaks out in lesions and takes on a pale yellow pallor.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Lords of Madness  Fiendish Codex I: Hordes of the Abyss
