<b>Blindness-Deafness</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 206)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Telflammar Shadowlord 1,  Bard 2,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,  Vigilante 2,  Sha'ir 2,  Death Master 2,  Dread Necromancer 2,  Cleric 3,   Darkness 2,   
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> One living creature
 <b>Duration:</b> Permanent (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You call upon the powers of unlife to render the subject blinded or deafened, as you choose.
