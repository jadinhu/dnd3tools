<b>Wave of Grief</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 236)

Enchantment [Evil, Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 2,  Blackguard 2,  Cleric 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 30 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> Cone-shaped burst
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>Emitting a mournful wail, you send out a pulse of magic imbued with sorrow and sadness.</b>
	
All within the cone when the spell is cast take a -3 penalty on attack rolls, saving throws, ability checks, and skill checks.
