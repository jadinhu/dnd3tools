<b>Longstrider, Mass</b>(Player's Handbook II)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Ranger 4,  Druid 5,    
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> 60 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> 60-ft.-radius emanation centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
Reality bends at your behest, speeding up your allies.
All allies in the area gain a +10-foot enhancement bonus to their speed scores.
