<b>Brilliant Aura</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 39)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer/Wizard Variant 7,  Cleric 8,  Druid 8,    
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Target:</b> Weapons carried by one creature/2 levels, no two of which are more than 30 ft. apart
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
<b>Taking fresh insight from contemplation of light, you cast the spell and set your allies aglow with white brilliance.</b>
	
This spell functions like <b>brilliant blade</b> (see below), except as noted here, and that all weapons of the subject creatures gain the brilliant energy special ability.
