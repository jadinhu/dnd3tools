<b>Halt Deathless</b>(Eberron Campaign Setting, p. 111)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>   Deathless 3,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> Up to three deathless, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
This spell renders up to three deathless creatures immobile. If the spell is successful, it renders the deathless paralyzed for the duration of the spell (similar to the effect of <b>hold person</b> on a living creature). The effect is broken if the halted creatures are attacked or take damage.
