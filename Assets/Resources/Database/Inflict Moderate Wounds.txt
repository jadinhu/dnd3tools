<b>Inflict Moderate Wounds</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 244)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 2, Blackguard 2,  Blighter 2,  Death Master 2,  Dread Necromancer 2,    
<b>Components:</b>

 	
This spell functions like inflict light wounds, except that you deal 2d8 points of damage +1 point per caster level (maximum +10).
