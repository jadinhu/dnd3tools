<b>Razorscales</b>(Serpent Kingdoms)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 2,  Druid 2,  Ranger 2,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, M, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level (D)

 	
Your scales become razor-sharp.
While this spell is in effect, you deal lethal instead of nonlethal damage when grappling.
If are not a Scaled One, this spell automatically fails.
Material Component: A thin-bladed razor.
