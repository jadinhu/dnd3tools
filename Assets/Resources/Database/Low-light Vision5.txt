<b>Low-light Vision</b>(Unapproachable East)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Assassin 1,  Harper Scout 1,  Hathran 1,  Ranger 1,  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,  Runescarred Berserker 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
You give the target creature low-light vision, the ability to see twice as far as a human in starlight, moonlight, torchlight, and similar conditions of poor illumination.
The target retains the ability to distinguish color and detail under these conditions.
Arcane Material Component: A small candle.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Complete Arcane  Savage Species  Spell Compendium  Magic of Faerun
