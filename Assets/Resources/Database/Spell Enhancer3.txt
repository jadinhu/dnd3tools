<b>Spell Enhancer</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 198)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round

 	
<b>You utter an ancient word tied to the fundamental principles of magic and immediately begin casting another spell.</b>
	
This spell enhances the next spell you cast, making it more difficult for targets to resist. The next spell you cast this round is cast at +2 caster level, and its save DC increases by 1.
