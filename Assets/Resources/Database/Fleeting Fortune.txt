<b>Fleeting Fortune</b>(Champions of Valor)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>   Initiate of tymora 1,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 immediate action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round or until discharged
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Yes (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
You imbue the subject with a bit of Tymora's luck, granting her a +5 luck bonus on a single skill check of her choice.
Activating the luck bonus requires no action but must be declared before the skill check is rolled.
