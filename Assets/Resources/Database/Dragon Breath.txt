<b>Dragon Breath</b>(Complete Divine)

Evocation [Good or Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 5,  Sorcerer 5,  Wizard 5,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level

 	
You gain the ability to breathe a gout of energy that mimics a dragon's breath as a standard action.
Once you've used the breath attack, you must wait 1d4 rounds before doing so again.
When you cast dragon breath, you choose one true dragon whose breath you're emulating.
If you choose a chromatic dragon, then dragon breath gains the evil descriptor.
If you choose a metallic dragon, then dragon breath gains the good descriptor.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Draconomicon  Spell Compendium
