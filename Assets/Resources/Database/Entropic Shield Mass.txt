<b>Entropic Shield, Mass</b>(Shining South)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature/level, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 min./level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
This spell functions like entropic shield, except that it affects multiple creatures.
