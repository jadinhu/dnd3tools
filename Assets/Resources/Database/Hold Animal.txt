<b>Hold Animal</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 241)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 2,  Ranger 2,   Blightbringer 1,  Animal 2,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Target:</b> One animal

 	
This spell functions like hold person, except that it affects an animal instead of a humanoid.
