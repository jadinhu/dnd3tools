<b>Cloak of Bravery, Greater</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 48)

Abjuration [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>   Courage 9,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 1 mile; see text
 <b>Area:</b> 1-mile-radius emanation centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
This spell functions like cloak of bravery, except all allies within the emanation (including you) are immune to fear effects and gain a +2 morale bonus on attack rolls. Allies who don't have line of sight to you are unaffected.
