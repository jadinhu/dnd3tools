<b>Blessing of Bahamut</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 31)

Abjuration [Good]
 <b>Level:</b>  Paladin 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level

 	
<b>You hear a distant dragon's roar that no one els detects, and your skin takes on a platinum sheen.</b>
	
You gain damage reduction 10/magic for the spell's duration.
<b>Material Component:</b> A canary feather, which is flung into the air.
