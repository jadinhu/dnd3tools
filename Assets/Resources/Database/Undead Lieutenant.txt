<b>Undead Lieutenant</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One undead
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
You empower the subject undead with the authority of command over other undead in your control.
The targeted undead must have an Intelligence of 5 or higher.
Other undead under your control obey the subject undead as if it were you.
You may give orders to the undead normally, superseding the orders of the subject of this spell.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
