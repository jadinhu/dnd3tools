<b>Slide, Greater</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 192)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
This spell functions like <b>slide</b>, except as described above, and you can slide the subject creature 20 feet in a straight line. This movement likewise does not provoke attacks of opportunity.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Miniatures Handbook
