<b>Nature's Favor</b>(Complete Divine)

Evocation
 <b>Level:</b>  Ranger 2,  Druid 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Animal touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 min/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
By calling on the power of nature, you grant the target animal a +1 luck bonus on attack and damage rolls for every three caster levels you possess (maximum +3).
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Masters of the Wild: A Guidebook to Barbarians, Druids, and Rangers  Complete Adventurer  Spell Compendium
