<b>Demonflesh</b>(Book of Vile Darkness)

Transmutation [Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Blackguard 1,  Demonologist 1,   Demonic 1,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> Caster
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level

 	
The caster grows the thick, leathery flesh of a demon, gaining a +1 natural armor bonus to Armor Class for every five caster levels (at least +1, maximum +4).
This spell has no effect if the caster is an evil outsider.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Fiendish Codex I: Hordes of the Abyss
