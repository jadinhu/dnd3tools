<b>Marked Pulse</b>(Dragonmarked, p. 152)

Evocation [Force]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, Dragonmark
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 immediate action
 <b>Range:</b> 20 ft.
 <b>Target:</b> One creature/level in a 20-ft.-radius burst centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude half; see text
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>You unleash a pulse of bluish-green energy that slams into your foes.</b>
	
Creatures you select within the spell's area take force damage based on the strength of your dragonmark.

If you possess a greater or Siberys dragonmark, any creature that fails its Fortitude save is also knocked prone.
	
<b>Dragonmark:</b> Any true dragonmark.
