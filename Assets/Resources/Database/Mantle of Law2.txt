<b>Mantle of Law</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 137)

Abjuration [Law]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 3,  Paladin 3,   Mechanus 3,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>You invoke the powers of law, and a constant blue field of axiomatic energy surrounds you.</b>
	
This spell functions like <b>mantle of chaos</b>, except that mantle of law grants spell resistance against spells with the chaotic descriptor.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Planar Handbook
