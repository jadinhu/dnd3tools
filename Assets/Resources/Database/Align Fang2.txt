<b>Align Fang</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 8)

Transmutation [see text]
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 2,  Ranger 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Living creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
<b>Align fang</b> makes a creature's natural weapons good-, evil-, lawful-, or chaotic-aligned, as you choose. A natural weapon that is aligned can overcome the damage reduction of certain creatures, usually outsiders of the opposite alignment. This spell has no effect on a natural weapon that is already treated as being aligned, such as the claw or bite attack of most demons. 
	
You can't cast this spell on a manufactured weapon, such as a sword.
	
When you cast this spell to make a natural weapon good-, evil-, lawful-, or chaotic-aligned, <b>align fang</b> is a good, evil, lawful, or chaotic spell, respectively.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Miniatures Handbook
