<b>Summon Weapon</b>(Complete Mage)

Conjuration (Summoning)
 <b>Level:</b>  Assassin 2,  Bard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> 0 ft.
 <b>Effect:</b> Nonmagical light weapon
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
A weapon appears in your hand, ready for use.
You conjure a nonmagical light weapon sized for you.
The weapon is normal in every respect.
It can't be made of any unusual metal, nor is it masterwork.
