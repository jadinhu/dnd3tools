<b>Ectoplasmic Feedback</b>(Libris Mortis: The Book of the Dead)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 min./level (D)

 	
A sheath of energy that reacts to incorporeal touch attacks protects you.
Any creature that hits you with an incorporeal touch attack takes 1d6 points of force damage +1 point per caster level (maximum +10).
If the attacker has spell resistance, it applies to this effect.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
