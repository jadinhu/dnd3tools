<b>Lullaby</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 249)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 0,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium(100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Area:</b> Living creatures within a 10-ft.-radius burst
 <b>Duration:</b> Concentration + 1 round/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
Any creature within the area that fails a Will save becomes drowsy and inattentive, taking a -5 penalty on Listen and Spot checks and a -2 penalty on Will saves against sleep effects while the lullaby is in effect.
Lullaby lasts for as long as the caster concentrates, plus up to 1 round per caster level thereafter.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Song and Silence: A Guidebook to Bards and Rogues
