<b>One Mind, Greater</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 149)

Divination
 <b>Level:</b>  Paladin 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level

 	
This spell functions like <b>one mind</b>, except as noted here, and in addition you and your mount gain a +2 bonus on melee damage rolls as long as you are mounted.
