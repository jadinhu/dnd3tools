<b>Insanity</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 244)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Jester 6,  Sorcerer 7,  Wizard 7,  Sha'ir 7,   Limbo 6,  Madness 7,  Charm 7,  Moon 7,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> One living creature
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
The affected creature suffers from a continuous <b>confusion</b> effect, as the spell.
	
Remove curse does not remove insanity. <b>Greater restoration</b>, <b>heal</b>, <b>limited wish</b>, <b>miracle</b>, or <b>wish</b> can restore the creature.
