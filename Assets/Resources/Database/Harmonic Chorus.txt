<b>Harmonic Chorus</b>(Complete Adventurer)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, AF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One living creature
 <b>Duration:</b> Concentration, up to 1 round/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
Harmonic chorus lets you improve the spellcasting ability of another spellcaster.
For the duration of the spell, the subject gains a +2 morale bonus to caster level and a +2 morale bonus on save DCs for all spells she casts.
Focus: A tuning fork.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium  Song and Silence: A Guidebook to Bards and Rogues
