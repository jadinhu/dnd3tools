<b>Fireburst, Greater</b>(Miniatures Handbook)

Evocation [Fire]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 5,  Wizard 5,  Warmage 5,    
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Effect:</b> Burst of fire extending 10 ft. from you

 	
This spell functions like fireburst, except that it affects creatures within 10 feet of you and deals a maximum of 15d8 points of damage to each one.
