<b>Hellfire Storm</b>(Fiendish Codex II: Tyrants of the Nine Hells)

Evocation [Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>   Diabolic 7,   
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Area:</b> 20-ft.-radius spread

 	
This spell functions like hellfire,
except it deals 1d8 points of damage per caster level (maximum 10d8) to creatures in the affected area.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Book of Vile Darkness
