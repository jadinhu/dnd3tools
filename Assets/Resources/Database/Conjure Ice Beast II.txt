<b>Conjure Ice Beast II</b>(Frostburn)

Conjuration (Creation) [Cold]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 2,  Druid 2,  Ranger 2,    
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Effect:</b> One or more conjured ice creatures, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart

 	
This spell functions like conjure ice beast I, except that you can conjure one creature from the 2nd-level list or two creatures of the same kind from the 1st-level list.
