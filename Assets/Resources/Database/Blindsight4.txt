<b>Blindsight</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 32)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 3,  Druid 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 Standard Action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature Touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
You touch your intended subject, which then turns its head rapidly about as if looking for the source of some sound.
	
This spell grants the subject the blindsight ability (MM 306) effective out to 30 feet.
