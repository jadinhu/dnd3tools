<b>Iron Bones</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 125)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 4,  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, AF, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Corporeal undead creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
<b>A brief flash engulfs your undead ally, and through its flesh you can see its skeleton. The skeleton glows a dusky red for a moment.</b>
	
This spell functions like stone bones (Spell Compendium page 208), except that the subject creature's skeleton changes to iron. The creature gains a +6 natural armor bonus to AC.
	
<b>Arcane Focus</b>: A miniature skull made out of iron or steel.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Ghostwalk  Tome of Battle: The Book of Nine Swords  Magic of Faerun
