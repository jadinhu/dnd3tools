<b>Fool's Gold</b>(Power of Faerun)

Illusion [Glamer]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 2,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,  Merchant Prince 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 round
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Metal objects of up to 10 cu. in. in volume/level
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will disbelief (if interacted with)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No
