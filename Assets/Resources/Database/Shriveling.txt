<b>Shriveling</b>(Book of Vile Darkness)

Necromancy [Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,  Cleric 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, Disease
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One living creature
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Reflex half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
The caster channels dark energy that blasts and blackens the subject's flesh.
The subject takes 1d4 points of damage per caster level (maximum 10d4).
Disease Component: Soul rot.
