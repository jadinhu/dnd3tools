<b>Ayailla's Radiant Burst</b>(Book of Exalted Deeds)

Evocation [Good]
 <b>Level:</b>   Sanctified 2,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, Sacrifice
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 60 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> Cone-shaped burst
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates (blindness) and Reflex half (shards)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
Shards of heavenly light spray from your fingertips, blinding evil creatures in their path for 1 round.
A successful Fortitude save negates the blindness.
The luminous shards also sear the flesh of evil creatures, dealing 1d6 points of damage per two caster levels (maximum 5d6).
A successful Reflex save halves the damage, which is of divine origin.
Sacrifice: 1d2 points of Strength damage.
