<b>Blood Frenzy</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 33)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 Standard Action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Any creature with the rage ability
 <b>Duration:</b> see text
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will Negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>A hot rush of anger flows through your target's veins.</b>
	
The target creature enters a rage, as its rage special ability, but this rage does not count toward the creature's number of rage attempts per day.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Magic of Faerun
