<b>Blessing of the Righteous</b>(Player's Handbook II)

Evocation [Good]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 4,  Paladin 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 40 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> All allies in a 40-ft.-radius burst centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
A sudden burst of warm, radiant light engulfs you and your allies.
The light fades quickly but lingers on the weapons of those affected.
You bless yourself and your allies.
You and your allies'melee and ranged attacks deal an extra 1d6 points of holy damage and are considered good-aligned for the purpose of overcoming damage reduction.
