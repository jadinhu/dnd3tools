<b>Insignia of Warding</b>(Races of Destiny)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 3,  Paladin 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, AF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 min./level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
This spell works just like insignia of alarm except the insignia wearers also gain a +1 divine bonus to AC and on Fortitude saves.
