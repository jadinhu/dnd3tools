<b>Sound Lance</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 196)

Evocation [Sonic]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,  Cleric 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature or object
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>You unleash a shrill, piercing cry at your target, which takes the barely visible form of a translucent lance hurtling through the air.</b>
	
This spell causes a projectile of intense sonic energy to leap from you to a target within range. The sound deals 1d8 points of sonic damage per caster level (maximum 10d8). A sound lance cannot penetrate the area of a silence spell.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Savage Species
