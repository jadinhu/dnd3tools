<b>Cloak of Bravery, Greater</b>(Complete Warrior)

Abjuration [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>   Courage 9,   
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Range:</b> 1 mile; see text
 <b>Area:</b> 1-mile-radius emanation centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level

 	
As cloak of bravery, except all allies within the emanation (including you) are immune to fear effects and gain a +2 morale bonus on attack rolls.
Allies who don't have line of sight to you are unaffected.
