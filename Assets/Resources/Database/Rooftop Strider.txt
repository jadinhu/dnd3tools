<b>Rooftop Strider</b>(Races of Destiny)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Assassin 1,   City 1,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 min./level

 	
You can run across rooftops or similar uneven surfaces much more easily than normal.
You gain a +5 competence bonus on Balance and Jump checks.
You automatically succeed on all Jump checks to clear a horizontal distance of 5 feet or less.
You do not need to make Balance checks for running or charging across a sloped surface.
Material Component: A chip of roof tile.
