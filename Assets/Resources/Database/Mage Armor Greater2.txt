<b>Mage Armor, Greater</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 136)

Conjuration (Creation) [Force]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
<b>An invisible sheen of armor-shaped force surrounds you.</b>
	
This spell functions like <b>mage armor</b> (PH 249), except that it requires no material component and its tangible field of force provides a +6 armor bonus to Armor Class.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Complete Arcane
