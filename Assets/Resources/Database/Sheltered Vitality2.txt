<b>Sheltered Vitality</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 188)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 4,  Druid 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Living creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
<b>Pressing your hand to the creature's chest, you utter a low chant, and a warm glow passes from your hand into the subject.</b>
	
The subject gains immunity to fatigue, exhaustion, and ability damage or ability drain (regardless of the source).
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Libris Mortis: The Book of the Dead
