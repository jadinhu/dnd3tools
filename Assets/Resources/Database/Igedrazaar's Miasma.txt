<b>Igedrazaar's Miasma</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Conjuration (Creation)
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, AF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Area:</b> 15-ft.-radius burst
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You conjure a cloud of foul-looking gray fog.
The toxic fog deals 1d4 points of subdual damage/level (maximum 5d4).
Holding one's breath doesn't help.
Focus: Three polished gray stones.
