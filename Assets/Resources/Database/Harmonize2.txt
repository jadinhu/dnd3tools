<b>Harmonize</b>(Song and Silence: A Guidebook to Bards and Rogues)

Evocation
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, AF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 3 rounds
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Up to 4 bards (including the caster), none of whom can be more than 10 feet from the nearest other target
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
This spell enables two to four bards to pool their talents by performing in a group.
The caster designates one bard to be the lead performer, and the rest provide backup.
Harmonize grants the lead performer a circumstance bonus on Perform checks equal to +1 per three bard levels of the backup performers for the duration of the spell.
Focus: The caster's instrument.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Races of Stone
