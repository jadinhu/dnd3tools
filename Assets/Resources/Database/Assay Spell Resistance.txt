<b>Assay Spell Resistance</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 17)

Divination
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 4,  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level

 	
<b>Finishing the spell, your eyes glow with a pale blue radiance, and you understand how to overcome your foe's resistance to your magic.</b>
	
This spell gives you a +10 bonus on caster level checks to overcome the spell resistance of a specific creature. Assay resistance is effective against only one specific creature per casting, and you must be able to see the creature when you cast the spell.
