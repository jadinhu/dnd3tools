<b>Stalking Spell</b>(Savage Species)

Illusion (Glamer)
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 8,  Wizard 8,    
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)

 	
As improved invisibility, except as noted above and that the creature is hidden from hearing and scent as well.
The subject radiates silence to a radius of 5 feet and neither leaves a scent nor can be detected by the scent ability.
