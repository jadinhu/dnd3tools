<b>Unearthly Beauty</b>(Book of Exalted Deeds)

Transmutation [Good]
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 8,   Fey 8,   
<b>Components:</b>

 	
As blinding beauty, but at any time during the duration of the spell, you can evoke an additional effect as a free action.
When you choose, any creature within 30 feet of you that is looking directly at you must succeed on a Will save or die.
Abstinence Component: You must abstain from sexual intercourse for one month before casting this spell.
