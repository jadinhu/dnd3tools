<b>Instant Locksmith</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 124)

Divination
 <b>Level:</b>  Assassin 1,  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round

 	
<b>You point at a mechanism, twist your hand, and make a clicking sound with your tongue. You now have a better idea of how the mechanism works.</b>
	
You can make one Disable Device check or one Open Lock check in this round as a free action. You gain a +2 insight bonus on the check.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Complete Adventurer
