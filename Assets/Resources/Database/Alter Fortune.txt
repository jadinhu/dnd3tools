<b>Alter Fortune</b>(Player's Handbook II)

Divination
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 3,  Cleric 3,  Druid 3,  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, <abbr title="Experience">XP, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 immediate action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
With a single utterance, you create a momentary distortion that engulfs and confounds your foe.
You change the flow of chance, causing the subject to immediately reroll any die roll it just made.
It must abide by the second roll.
XP Cost: 200 XP.
