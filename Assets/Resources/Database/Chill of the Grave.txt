<b>Chill of the Grave</b>(Libris Mortis: The Book of the Dead)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>   Deathbound 1,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> Ray
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
A pale ray erupts from your pointing finger.
You must succeed on a ranged touch attack with the ray to affect a target.
The target takes 1d10 points of cold damage.
This damage increases to 2d10 at caster level 4, 3d10 at caster level 7th, and 4d10 at caster level 10th.
