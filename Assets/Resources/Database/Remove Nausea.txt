<b>Remove Nausea</b>(Book of Exalted Deeds)

Conjuration (Healing)
 <b>Level:</b>  Apostle of Peace 3,  Cleric 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
Remove nausea restores to health a creature who is nauseated or sickened.
Remove nausea cures only the nausea or sickening effect, not disease, poison, or any other effects.
