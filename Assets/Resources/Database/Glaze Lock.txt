<b>Glaze Lock</b>(Frostburn)

Conjuration (Creation) [Cold]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Lock touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 min./level; see text
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
You jam a locking mechanism with ice, raising its Open Lock DC by 10 and the lock's hardness by 5.
Fire attacks against the lock deal double damage.
In cold or lower temperature bands, this spell lasts 1 hour per level.
