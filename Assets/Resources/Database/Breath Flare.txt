<b>Breath Flare</b>(Draconomicon)

Transmutation [Light]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>S, <abbr title="Meta Breath">MB, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> Your breath weapon
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous

 	
If your breath weapon uses fire or electricity, it is suffused with bright light.
In addition to taking the normal fire or electricity damage, creatures that fail their saving throws against the breath weapon are dazzled for 1 minute per caster level.
Sightless creatures are not affected by breath flare.
The modification applies only to the breath produced as part of casting.
