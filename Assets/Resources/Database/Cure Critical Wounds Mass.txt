<b>Cure Critical Wounds, Mass</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 215)

Conjuration (Healing)
 <b>Level:</b>  Healer 7,  Cleric 8,  Druid 9,  Urban Druid 9,   Healing 8,   
<b>Components:</b>

 	
This spell functions like mass cure light wounds, except that it cures 4d8 points of damage +1 point per caster level (maximum +40).
