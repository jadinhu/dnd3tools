<b>Master's Touch</b>(Player's Handbook II)

Divination
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 2,  Cleric 2,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 immediate action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
With a mere utterance, you grant the creature special insight into how best to complete a task at hand.
You cast this spell immediately before the target makes a skill check.
The subject envisions how a master might accomplish the same task, gaining a +4 insight bonus on its skill check.
Master's touch has no effect on skill checks that represent effort over more than 1 round of time (Craft checks, for example).
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Complete Adventurer  Spell Compendium
