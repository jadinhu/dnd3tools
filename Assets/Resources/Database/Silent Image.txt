<b>Silent Image</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 279)

Illusion (Figment)
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 1,  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,  Vigilante 1,  Beguiler 1,  Wu Jen 1,  Sha'ir 1,  Jester 1,  Court Herald 1,   Gnome 1,  Illusion 1,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, AF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level)
 <b>Effect:</b> Visual figment that cannot extend beyond four 10-ft. cubes + one 10-ft. cube/level (S)
 <b>Duration:</b> Concentration
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will disbelief (if interacted with)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
This spell creates the visual illusion of an object, creature, or force, as visualized by you.
The illusion does not create sound, smell, texture, or temperature.
You can move the image within the limits of the size of the effect.
Focus: A bit of fleece.
