<b>Camouflage</b>(Masters of the Wild: A Guidebook to Barbarians, Druids, and Rangers)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 1,  Ranger 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes/level

 	
You change your coloring to match your environment, gaining a +10 competence bonus on Hide checks.
Material Component:Mud painted on your face.
