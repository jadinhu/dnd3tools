<b>Tremorsense</b>(Underdark)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Harper Scout 3,  Ranger 3,  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, AF, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
You can automatically pinpoint the location of any object or creature within 30 feet that is in contact with the ground.
Arcane Focus: A fleck of skin from a creature that has
tremorsense.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
