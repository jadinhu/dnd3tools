<b>Fireburst</b>(Miniatures Handbook)

Evocation [Fire]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,  Warmage 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 5 ft.
 <b>Effect:</b> Burst of fire extending 5 ft. from you
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Reflex half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
Fireburst causes a powerful explosion of flame to burst from you, damaging anyone within 5 feet of you.
All creatures within that area, except for you, take 1d8 points of fire damage per caster level (maximum 5d8).
The burst does not affect you or any creatures or objects in your space.
Material Component: A bit of sulfur.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium  Complete Arcane
