<b>Wind Tunnel</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Evocation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 5,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Area:</b> One target/level
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
You call on the wind to assist your allies' accuracy.
This wind tunnel improves the accuracy of ranged weapons, granting each target a +10 competence bonus on ranged attacks.
Further, it doubles the range increment of these weapons.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
