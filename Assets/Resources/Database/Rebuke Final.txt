<b>Rebuke, Final</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 170)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Fear, Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 7,  Wizard 7,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, AF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One living creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will partial
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>You jab the stick into your side and twist it, causing yourself some discomfort but creating a killing pain in the target.</b>
	
This spell functions like <b>rebuke</b>, except that the target dies instantly if it fails its saving throw. A creature that survives this effect is dazed for 1 round.
