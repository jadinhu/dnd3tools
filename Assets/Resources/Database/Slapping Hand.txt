<b>Slapping Hand</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 191)

Evocation [Force]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, AF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Effect:</b> One Tiny hand
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None; see text
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>You complete the spell and blow into the leather glove. Immediately a blue-white, translucent hand appears next to the enemy and smacks it across the side of the head ï¿½ ;not enough to hurt it, but hard enough to give others nearby a chance to attack.</b>
	
This spell distracts the subject, causing it to immediately provoke attacks of opportunity from creatures threatening its space. The spell allows no saving throw, but a slapped creature can negate the effect with a DC 20 Concentration check.
	
Focus: A leather glove.
