<b>Bottomless Hate</b>(Ghostwalk)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Ranger 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level

 	
You fill yourself with a terrible hatred against your favored enemies.
Against them you gain a +1 morale bonus on attack and damage rolls for every three caster levels.
This spell is suppressed by spells such as calm emotions.
