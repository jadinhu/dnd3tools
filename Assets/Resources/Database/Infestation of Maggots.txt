<b>Infestation of Maggots</b>(Complete Divine)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> One round/2 levels
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
With a successful melee touch attack, you infest a creature with maggotlike creatures.
They deal 1d4 points of temporary Constitution damage each round.
The subject makes a new Fortitude save each round to negate the damage of that round and end the effect.
The infestation can be removed with a cure disease or heal spell.
Material Component: A handful of dead, dried flies.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium  Magic of Faerun
