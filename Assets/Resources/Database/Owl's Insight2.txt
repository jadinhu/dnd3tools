<b>Owl's Insight</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 152)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 5,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
<b>As you cast this spell, your face is overlaid with the image of a snow owl. The image fades as you bestow your ally with comprehension.</b>
	
The subject gains an insight bonus to Wisdom equal to 1/2 your caster level.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Magic of Faerun
