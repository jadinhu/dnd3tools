<b>Ray of Weakness</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 168)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> Ray
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>A dark ray flies forth from your hand. The air fills with the smell of blood.</b>
	
You must succeed on a ranged touch attack with the ray to strike a target. The target of this ray feels weaker and takes a -2 penalty on attack rolls. Its speed is reduced by 10 feet.
