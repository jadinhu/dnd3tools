<b>Spell Immunity, Greater</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 282)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 8,   Pride 8,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
This spell functions like <b>spell immunity</b>, except the immunity applies to spells of 8th level or lower.
	
A creature can have only one <b>spell immunity</b> or <b>greater spell immunity</b> spell in effect on it at a time.
