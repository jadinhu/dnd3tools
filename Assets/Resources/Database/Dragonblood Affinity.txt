<b>Dragonblood Affinity</b>(Dragons of Faerun, p. 114)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 3,  Sorcerer 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> One living dragon or dragonblood creature/level, no two of which are more than 30 ft. apart
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 min./level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
You feel the draconic blood stirring within you, strengthening your nearby kin.
	
This spell summons the strength of your dragon heritage to empower other draconic creatures, instilling them with a powerful sense of pride. Each affected dragon or dragonblood creature gains a +2 bonus on saving throws, caster level checks, attack rolls, ability checks, skill checks, and weapon damage rolls.
	
<b>Special:</b> Only dragons and dragonblood creatures can cast this spell.
