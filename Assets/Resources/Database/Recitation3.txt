<b>Recitation</b>(Spell Compendium)

Conjuration (Creation)
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 4,   Purification 3,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 60 ft.
 <b>Target:</b> All allies within a 60-ft.-radius burst centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>By reciting a sacred passage or declaration, you invoke your deity's blessing upon yourself and your allies.</b> 
	
The spell affects all allies within the spell's area at the moment you cast it. Your allies gain a +2 luck bonus to AC, on attack rolls, and on saving throws, or a +3 luck bonus if they worship the same deity as you. 
	
Divine Focus: In addition to your holy symbol, this spell requires a sacred text as a divine focus.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Unapproachable East  Complete Divine  Defenders of the Faith: A Guidebook to Clerics and Paladins
