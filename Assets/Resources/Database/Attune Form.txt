<b>Attune Form</b>(Manual of the Planes)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 3,  Druid 3,  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> One or more living creatures touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 2 hours/level (see text)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
This spell allows you to attune the affected creatures to the plane you are currently on, negating harmful and negative natural effects of that plane.
Affected creatures gain the protections listed in the avoid planar effects spell.
Arcane Material Component: A bit of stone or earth from your home plane.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium  Planar Handbook
