<b>Hawkeye</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 110)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 1,  Ranger 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes/level (D)

 	
<b>By crying out like a hawk, you improve your eyesight. Distant objects and creatures seem closer and more distinct.</b>
	
Your range increment for ranged weapons increases by 50%, and you gain a +5 competence bonus on Spot checks.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Masters of the Wild: A Guidebook to Barbarians, Druids, and Rangers  Complete Adventurer  Complete Divine
