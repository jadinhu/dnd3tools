<b>Forestfold</b>(Complete Divine)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Ranger 3,  Druid 4,    
<b>Components:</b>

 	
This spell grants you a +20 competence bonus on Hide and Move Silently checks.
The spell ends if you move more than 10 feet from where you stood when you cast the spell.
It is otherwise the same as camouflage.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Masters of the Wild: A Guidebook to Barbarians, Druids, and Rangers  Savage Species  Complete Adventurer  Spell Compendium
