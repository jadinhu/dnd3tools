<b>Haunting Tune</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 110)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Fear, Mind-Affecting, Sonic]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 round
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature/level, no two of which are more than 30 ft. apart
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 minutes/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>The spell wrapped within your music makes even the most hard-hearted quail.</b>
	
Targets that fail their saves become shaken.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Magic of Faerun
