<b>Sticky Fingers</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 206)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Assassin 1,  Bard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round

 	
<b>Your fingers exude a slightly viscous secretion and become more nimble. When touched together, your fingertips stick slightly.</b>
	
This spell grants you a +10 bonus on Sleight of Hand checks.
