<b>Hold Person</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 241)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 2,  Urban Druid 2,  Death Master 2,  Wu Jen 2,  Thayan Slaver 2,  Cleric 2,  Beguiler 3,  Wizard 3,  Sorcerer 3,  Sha'ir 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, AF, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> One humanoid creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level (D); see text
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates; see text
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
The subject becomes paralyzed and freezes in place.
It is aware and breathes normally but cannot take any actions, even speech.
Each round on its turn, the subject may attempt a new saving throw to end the effect.
(This is a full-round action that does not provoke attacks of opportunity).
A winged creature who is paralyzed cannot flap its wings and falls.
A swimmer can't swim and may drown.
Arcane Focus: A small, straight piece of iron.
