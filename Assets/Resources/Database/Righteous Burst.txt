<b>Righteous Burst</b>(Player's Handbook II)

Evocation [Good]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 7,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 30 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> 30-ft.-radius burst centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None or Will half; see text
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
Holy light blasts from your upraised fist.
This spell heals every ally in the area of 1d8 points of damage +1 per caster level (maximum +35).
Every enemy in the area takes 1d8 points of damage +1 per caster level (maximum +35).
Enemies are allowed Will saves to halve the damage.
Material Component: A handful of silver dust (worth 15 gp).
