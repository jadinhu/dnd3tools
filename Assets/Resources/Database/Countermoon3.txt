<b>Countermoon</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 53)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One lycanthrope
 <b>Duration:</b> 12 hours
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (D)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>Translucent red chains fly from your outstretched hand to wrap around the target before fading away.</b>
	
This spell forces a lycanthrope back to its natural form and keeps it from changing form, preventing both voluntary shapechanging through the alternate form ability and involuntary shapechanging because of lycanthropy.
	
<b>Material Component:</b> A hair, scale, or other cast-off item from the type of creature.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Masters of the Wild: A Guidebook to Barbarians, Druids, and Rangers  Savage Species
