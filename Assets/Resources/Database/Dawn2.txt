<b>Dawn</b>(Savage Species)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 0,  Ranger 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> All creatures within a 15-ft.-radius burst centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
All sleeping creatures in the affected area awaken.
Those who are unconscious because of subdual damage wake up and become staggered (see Subdual Damage in Chapter 8 of the Player's Handbook).
This spell does not affect dying creatures.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Masters of the Wild: A Guidebook to Barbarians, Druids, and Rangers  Spell Compendium
