<b>Aerial Summoning Dance</b>(Races of Faerun)

Conjuration (Summoning)
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 3 rounds
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> One summoned Large air elemental
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
As summon monster VI, except as noted above and that casting the spell requires a flying dance by the caster and at least four aarakocra, all of whom are treated as participating in the spellcasting for the purpose of provoking attacks of opportunity and maintaining concentration.
