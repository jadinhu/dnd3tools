<b>Catsfeet</b>(Complete Mage)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Assassin 1,  Bard 1,  Hexblade 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 immediate action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous

 	
As you complete this spell, you feel as lithe and quick as a cat.
When you cast this spell, you can immediately reroll a Balance, Climb, Jump, or Move Silently check that you have just rolled.
This second roll gains a +5 insight bonus.
You must abide by the second result, even if it is worse than the first.
