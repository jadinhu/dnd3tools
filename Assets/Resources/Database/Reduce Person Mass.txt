<b>Reduce Person, Mass</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 269)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,  Sha'ir 4,  Jester 4,   Pride 5,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 round
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One humanoid creature/level, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
This spell functions like <b>reduce person</b>, except that it affects multiple creatures.
