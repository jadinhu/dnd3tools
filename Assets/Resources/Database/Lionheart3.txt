<b>Lionheart</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 132)

Abjuration [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Paladin 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
<b>You touch your ally while intoning the spell. Where your finger touches, a warm glow appears, then fades.</b>
	
The subject gains immunity to fear effects.
	
Material Component: A hair from a lion's mane.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Champions of Valor  Miniatures Handbook
