<b>Heroism, Greater</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 240)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 5,  Sorcerer 6,  Wizard 6,  Sha'ir 6,   Joy 6,  Revered Ancestor 6,  Courage 7,  Pride 7,  Arborea 8,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 min./level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
This spell functions like <b>heroism</b>, except the creature gains a +4 morale bonus on attack rolls, saves, and skill checks, immunity to fear effects, and temporary hit points equal to your caster level (maximum 20).
