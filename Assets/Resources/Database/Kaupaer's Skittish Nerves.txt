<b>Kaupaer's Skittish Nerves</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You enhance the target's reactions in combat, granting him a +5 bonus on initiative checks.
