<b>Stony Grasp</b>(Complete Arcane)

Transmutation [Earth]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Effect:</b> Animated stone arm
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level

 	
As earthen grasp (see page 104), except the stony arm can appear from any natural surface, including unworked rock, earth, mud, grass, or sand.
The stone arm has AC 18, hardness 8, and 4 hit points per caster level.
Material Component: A miniature hand sculpted from stone.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
