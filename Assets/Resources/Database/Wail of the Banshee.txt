<b>Wail of the Banshee</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 298)

Necromancy [Death, Sonic]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 9,  Wizard 9,  Warmage 9,  Sha'ir 9,  Death Master 9,  Dread Necromancer 9,   Death 9,  Deathbound 9,  Hatred 9,   
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Area:</b> One living creature/level within a 40-ft.-radius spread
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You emit a terrible scream that kills creatures that hear it (except for yourself).
Creatures closest to the point of origin are affected first.
