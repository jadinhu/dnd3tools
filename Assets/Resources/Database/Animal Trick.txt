<b>Animal Trick</b>(Masters of the Wild: A Guidebook to Barbarians, Druids, and Rangers)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 0,  Ranger 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One animal companion bonded to you by an animal friendship effect
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
Your animal companion performs a trick of your choosing that it does not already know.
This trick can be any of those listed in the Animal Companions sidebar in Chapter 2 of the <span class="caps">DUNGEON</span> MASTER'S Guide or in Chapter 2 of this book.
The animal retains no knowledge of the trick after performing it.
