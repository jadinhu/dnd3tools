<b>Junglerazer</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 127)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 3,  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 120 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> 120-ft. line
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Reflex half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>Invisible destructive energy springs silently forth from you, instantly destroying all natural plant life in the area and leaving a path of ash in its wake.</b>
	
Fey, vermin, plants and plant creatures, and animals caught in the area take 1d10 points of negative energy damage per caster level (maximum 10d10).
	
Material Component: A pinch of ash from a burnt plant.
