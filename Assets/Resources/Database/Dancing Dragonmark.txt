<b>Dancing Dragonmark</b>(Dragonmarked, p. 149)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, Dragonmark
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 Standard Action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25ft. + 5ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> Up to one dragonmarked creature per level, all within 30 ft of each other
 <b>Duration:</b> Concentration
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
A creature affected by <b>dancing dragonmark</b> is nauseated.
If the target creature's dragonmark is one step more powerful than yours, that creature it gains a +2 bonus to its Will save. If the target creature's dragonmark is two steps more powerful than yours, that creature it gains a +4 bonus to its Will save.
	
<b>Dragonmark:</b> Any true or aberrant dragonmark.
