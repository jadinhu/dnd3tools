<b>Undetectable Alignment</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 297)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Assassin 2, Bard 1,  Beguiler 1,  Dread Necromancer 1,  Vigilante 1,  Death Master 2,  Knight of the Chalice 2,  Vassal of Bahamut 2,  Ebonmar Infiltrator 2,  Paladin 2,  Cleric 2,  Jester 2,   Liberation 2,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature or object
 <b>Duration:</b> 24 hours
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (object)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (object)

 	
An undetectable alignment spell conceals the alignment of an object or a creature from all forms of divination.
