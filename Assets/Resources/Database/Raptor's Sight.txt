<b>Raptor's Sight</b>(Races of the Wild)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 1,  Ranger 1,   Sky 1,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level (D)

 	
Your visual acuity improves dramatically.
You gain a +5 competence bonus on Spot checks.
If you have 5 or more ranks in Spot, you take only half the normal range increment penalty for ranged attacks (-1 per range increment instead of -2).
