<b>Mindfrost</b>(Frostburn)

Necromancy [Cold]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
This spell freezes the mental pathways of living creatures, dealing 5d6 points of cold damage and 1d4 points of Intelligence damage.
Material Component: A small stone covered in frost.
