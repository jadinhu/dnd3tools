<b>Dragon Ally</b>(Draconomicon)

Conjuration (Summoning)
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 7,  Wizard 7,   Dragon 7,   
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Effect:</b> Up to two summoned dragons, totaling no more than 18 HD, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart when they appear

 	
As lesser dragon ally, except you may summon a single dragon of up to 18 HD or two dragons of the same kind whose HD total no more than 18.
The dragons agree to help you and request your return payment together.
XP Cost: 250 XP.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
