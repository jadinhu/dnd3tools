<b>Quickshift</b>(Book of Exalted Deeds)

Transmutation [Good]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 6,  Sorcerer 6,  Wizard 6,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, Celestial
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 round
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level

 	
For the duration of this spell, the caster's teleport or greater teleport spell-like ability is quickened (as if enhanced with the Quicken Spell feat).
This spell has no effect on other spells or spell-like abilities.
