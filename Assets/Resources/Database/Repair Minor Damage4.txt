<b>Repair Minor Damage</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 173)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 0,  Wizard 0,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> One construct
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
<b>As if with the eye of an expert craftsman, your touch draws out a minor dent in the construct's surface.</b>
	
This spell functions like <b>repair light damage</b>, except that you repair 1 point of damage to a construct.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Complete Arcane  Tome and Blood: A Guidebook to Wizards and Sorcerers  Miniatures Handbook  Dragon Compendium
