<b>Song of the Calling</b>(Ghostwalk)

Abjuration [Ectomancy]
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> One ghost
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates; see text
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You force a ghost to succumb to the Calling and depart immediately for the True Afterlife.
Each level in the eidolon class the target ghost has gives it a -1 penalty on its saving throw against this spell.
