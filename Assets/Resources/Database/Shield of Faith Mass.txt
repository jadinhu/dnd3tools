<b>Shield of Faith, Mass</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 188)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature/level, no two of which are more than 30 ft. apart
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
<b>You hold your arms aloft and implore the power you venerate. Your comrades are bathed in a silver light, and over their hearts appears the symbol of your god, showing the deity's protection.</b>
	
This spell functions like <b>shield of faith</b> (PH 278), except that it affects multiple allies at a distance.
