<b>Alarm, Greater</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 8)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 2,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, AF, 
 <b>Duration:</b> 4 hours/level (D)

 	
<b>Uttering a low chant, you ring a crystal bell with the drumming of your fingernails, bringing into being an invisible warded area.</b>
	
This spell functions like alarm (PH 197), and in addition the spell works on creatures traveling through the area on coterminous or coexistent planes (<span class="caps">DMG</span> 150), such the Ethereal Plane and the Plane of Shadow.
	
<b>Focus</b>: A bell made of carved crystal, worth at least 100 gp.
