<b>Resistance, Greater</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 174)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 4,  Cleric 4,  Druid 4,  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 24 hours
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
<b>Just as you touch the spell's subject, a feeling of peace and watchful guardianship fills your being.</b>
	
This spell functions like <b>resistance</b> (PH 272), except as noted here. You grant the subject a +3 resistance bonus on saves.
