<b>Inflict Critical Wounds</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 244)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 4, Blackguard 4,  Blighter 4,  Death Master 4,  Dread Necromancer 4,   Destruction 4,   
<b>Components:</b>

 	
This spell functions like inflict light wounds, except that you deal 4d8 points of damage +1 point per caster level (maximum +20).
