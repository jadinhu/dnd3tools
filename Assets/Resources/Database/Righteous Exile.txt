<b>Righteous Exile</b>(Fiendish Codex II: Tyrants of the Nine Hells)

Abjuration [Good]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 9,    
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 full-round action
 <b>Range:</b> 20 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> 20-ft.-radius burst, centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will partial
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
With a word, your body gives off an explosion of divine energy, scorching the flesh of those creatures despised by good beings.
All evil creatures in the area take 20d6 points of damage.
In addition, evil outsiders that fail a Will saving throw (DC equal to spell's save DC &#8211; creature's HD + your caster level) are instantly transported back to their home planes.
