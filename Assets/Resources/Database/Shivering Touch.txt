<b>Shivering Touch</b>(Frostburn)

Necromancy [Cold]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 3,  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,  Disciple of Thrym 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
On a successful melee touch attack, you instantly suck the heat from the target's body, rendering it numb. The target takes 3d6 points of Dexterity damage.
	
Creatures with the cold subtype are immune to the effects of <b>shivering touch</b>.
