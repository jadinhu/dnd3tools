<b>Earth Reaver</b>(Savage Species)

Transmutation [Fire]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 4,  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Area:</b> 20-ft.-radius spread, +10 ft./level
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You point at a spot on the ground, and the earth at that point erupts in a shower of rock, dirt, and fire.
Creatures and objects within the area take 7d6 points of damage; the damage is 4d6 impact and 3d6 fire.
Those that fail the saving throw are knocked prone.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
