<b>Doom</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 225)

Necromancy [Fear, Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 1, Blackguard 1,  Dread Necromancer 1,  Disciple of Thrym 1,  Knight of the Chalice 1,  Blighter 1,  Death Master 1,  Jester 1,  Demonologist 1,   Hades 1,  Hatred 1,  Pestilence 1,  Wrath 1,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> One living creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 min./level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
This spell fills a single subject with a feeling of horrible dread that causes it to become shaken.
