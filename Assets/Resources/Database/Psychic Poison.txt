<b>Psychic Poison</b>(Book of Vile Darkness)

Abjuration [Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 4,  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 10 minutes
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Area:</b> 50-ft.-radius spread, or one single object or creature that can fit in that area
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level

 	
The caster taints an area, creature, or object so that anyone casting a mindaffecting or divination spell at the creature or object or within the area is subject to a psychic poison (see Table 3-6).
The caster may choose any psychic poison for which he meets the minimum level requirement.
Arcane Material Component: A bit of humanoid brain tissue.
