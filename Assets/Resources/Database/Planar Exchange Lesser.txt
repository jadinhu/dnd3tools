<b>Planar Exchange, Lesser</b>(Planar Handbook)

Conjuration (Calling)
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 4,    
<b>Components:</b>

 	
As planar exchange, except that you trade places with a celestial brown bear, celestial griffon, fiendish dire ape, or fiendish tiger, at your option.
If the called creature is slain, you take 2d6 points of damage and the spell ends.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
