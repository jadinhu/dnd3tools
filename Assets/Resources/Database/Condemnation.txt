<b>Condemnation</b>(Player's Handbook II)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,  Cleric 5,    
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One outsider
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You speak words of power, forcing the entity to shrink with horror.
The outsider targeted by this spell must succeed on a Will save or be stunned for 1 round and have its spell resistance reduced by 10.
