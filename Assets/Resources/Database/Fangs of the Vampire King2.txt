<b>Fangs of the Vampire King</b>(Libris Mortis: The Book of the Dead)

Transmutation [Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Assassin 3,  Blackguard 3,   Deathbound 3,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 min./level

 	
You grow vampirelike fangs that allow you to make bite attacks with an attack bonus of +10 plus your Strength modifier.
Your bite attack deals 1d6 points of damage and 1 point of Constitution damage.
If you make a full attack with other weapons, you can also make a bite attack as a secondary attack (-5 to hit).
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Book of Vile Darkness
