<b>Invest Moderate Protection</b>(Player's Handbook II)

Conjuration (Healing)
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 3,    
<b>Components:</b>

 	
This spell functions like invest light protection, except that it cures 3d4 points of damage +1 per two caster levels (maximum +6) and grants damage reduction 3/evil.
Undead creatures take an extra 3 points of damage from any weapon or physical attack capable of overcoming good damage reduction.
