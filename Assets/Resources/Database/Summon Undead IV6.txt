<b>Summon Undead IV</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 215)

Conjuration (Summoning) [Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Blackguard 4,  Cleric 4,  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, AF, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 round
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> One or more summoned creatures, no two of which are more than 30 ft. apart
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
This spell functions like <b>summon undead I</b>, except that you can summon one undead from the 4th-level list, two undead of the same kind from the 3rd-level list, or four undead of the same kind from a lower-level list.
