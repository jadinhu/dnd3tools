<b>Karmic Backlash</b>(Complete Mage)

Abjuration
 <b>Level:</b>  Hexblade 2,  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>

 	
Your magic influences the flow of fate, doing harm to all who would dare attack you.
	
This spell functions like <b>karmic aura</b>, except that any creature that damages you and fails its Will save becomes exhausted for 2 rounds.
