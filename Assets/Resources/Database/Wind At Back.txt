<b>Wind At Back</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Evocation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> Two creatures/level, no two of which can be more than 50 ft. apart
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 day
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
This spell doubles the overland speed of all targets, assuming they are all traveling together in the same direction, including pack animals and mounts.
This spell does not affect nonliving material.
The spell does not affect tactical speed.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
