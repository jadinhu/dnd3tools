<b>Heal Animal Companion</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 110)

Conjuration (Healing)
 <b>Level:</b>  Ranger 3,  Druid 5,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Your animal companion touched
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
<b>You lay your hand on your animal companion, and as you do, its eyes become clear and focused, and it breathes easier.</b>
	
This spell functions like <b>heal</b> (PH 239), except that it affects only your animal companion.
