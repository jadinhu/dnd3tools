<b>Accuracy</b>(Complete Arcane)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Warmage 1,  Wu Jen 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> One thrown weapon/level touched or one projectile weapon touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 10 min./level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless, object)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless, object)

 	
When you cast this spell, you enhance one or more thrown weapons or one projectile weapon to improve its chance of hitting distant targets.
For the duration of the spell, the range increment for the affected weapon or weapons is doubled.
Material Component: Ink used to scribe a mystical character on each weapon affected by the spell.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Oriental Adventures
