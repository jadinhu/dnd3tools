<b>Pulse of Hate</b>(Player's Handbook II)

Necromancy [Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 7,  Sorcerer 7,  Wizard 7,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 20 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> 20-ft.-radius emanation centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
A red light pulses at your feet, sending malign waves of energy through the area that damage your enemies.
Starting in the round you cast it, pulse of hate deals 2d6 points of unholy damage per round, on your turn, to all enemies in the area.
Arcane Material Component: A heart-shaped locket and a pin.
