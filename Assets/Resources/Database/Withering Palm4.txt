<b>Withering Palm</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 241)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 7,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Living creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>A black shroud of magical energy surrounds your hands. Beneath the shroud your hands look withered and aged, like uncared-for ancient leather on the verge of crumbling to dust.</b>
	
Your successful melee touch attack deals 1 point of Strength damage and 1 point of Constitution damage per two caster levels to the target (maximum of 10 points each). If you score a critical hit, the subject takes ability drain instead, but the effect is not doubled.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Complete Arcane  Eberron Campaign Setting  Oriental Adventures
