<b>Stunning Breath, Greater</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 211)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 8,  Wizard 8,    
<b>Components:</b>S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> Your breath weapon
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous

 	
This spell functions like <b>stunning breath</b>, except that creatures that fail their Fortitude saves are stunned for 2d4 rounds.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Draconomicon
