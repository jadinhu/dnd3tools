<b>Prayer</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 264)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 3,  Paladin 3,  Knight of the Chalice 3,  Divine Bard 3,   Community 3,  Competition 3,  Orc 3,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 40 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> All allies and foes within a 40-ft.-radius burst centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You bring special favor upon yourself and your allies while bringing disfavor to your enemies. You and your each of your allies gain a +1 luck bonus on attack rolls, weapon damage rolls, saves, and skill checks, while each of your foes takes a -1 penalty on such rolls.
