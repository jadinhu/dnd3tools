<b>Thunderstroke</b>(Champions of Valor)

Evocation [Electricity]
 <b>Level:</b>   Initiate of anhur 2,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature or object
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Reflex half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
You call down a bolt of electricity, which unerringly strikes the target and deals 1d6 points of electricity damage per caster level (maximum 5d6).
A small rumble of thunder accompanies the bolt, which is as loud as a battle (Listen DC -10 to hear it).
