<b>Launch Bolt</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 0,  Wizard 0,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> One crossbow bolt in your possession
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
You cast this spell on a crossbow bolt, causing it to fly at a target of your choice as if you had fired it from a light crossbow.
Any properties of the bolt (such as magical abilities, masterwork quality, and so on) or feats you possess (such as Point Blank Shot, Weapon Focus [light crossbow], and so on) apply.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
