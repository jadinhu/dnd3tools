<b>Rhino's Rush</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 176)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Paladin 1,  Ranger 1,   Wrath (SpC) 1,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round

 	
<b>A violent fury consumes you. You seek nothing more than to charge at your enemies and bash in their heads.</b>
	
This spell allows you to propel yourself in a single deadly charge. The first charge attack you make before the end of the round deals double damage on a successful hit.
