<b>Stony Grasp</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 209)

Transmutation [Earth]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 3,  Wizard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> Animated stone arm
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>By thrusting your arm downward, you complete the casting of the spell. Nearby, a roughly formed arm about the size of a human erupts from the ground and grasps at the air.</b>
	
This spell functions like <b>earthen grasp</b> (page 76), except as noted here. The stony arm can appear from any natural surface, including unworked rock, earth, mud, grass, or sand. The stone arm has AC 18, hardness 8, and 4 hit points per caster level.
	
Material Component: A miniature hand sculpted from stone.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Complete Arcane
