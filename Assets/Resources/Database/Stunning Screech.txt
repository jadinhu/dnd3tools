<b>Stunning Screech</b>(Book of Vile Darkness)

Evocation [Evil, Sonic]
 <b>Level:</b>  Demonologist 2,  Bard 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, Drug
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> 30 ft.
 <b>Target:</b> All creatures within range
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
The caster emits a piercing screech like that of a vrock demon.
Every creature within the area is stunned for 1 round.
Material Component: Feather of a large bird or a vrock.
Drug Component:Mushroom powder.
