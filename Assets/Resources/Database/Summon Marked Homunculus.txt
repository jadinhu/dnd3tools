<b>Summon Marked Homunculus</b>(Dragonmarked, p. 154)

Conjuration (Summoning)
 <b>Level:</b>  Artificer 1,  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, Dragonmark
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 round
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25ft. + 5ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> One summoned homunculus
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
This spell allows a caster with the Mark of Making to summon a special homunculus. If you possess a least Mark of Making, you can summon a dedicated wright, expeditions messenger, or furtive filcher homunculus (<b>ECS</b> 284-286), or an arbalester homunculus (<b>Magic of Eberron</b> 152). If you possess a more powerful Mark of Making, you can instead summon a true homunculus (<b>MM</b> 154), an iron defender (<b>ECS</b> 287), a packmate (<b>MoE</b> 153), or a persistent harrier (<b>MoE</b> 153).
A small glyph resembling your dragonmark appears somewhere on the homunculus's body. If the homunculus is reduced to 0 hit points, it explodes in a burst of blue, green, and purple light that deals 1d6 points of damage to all adjacent creatures except you.
	
<b>Material Component:</b> A pinch of dragonshard dust.
	
<b>Dragonmark:</b> Any Mark of Making
