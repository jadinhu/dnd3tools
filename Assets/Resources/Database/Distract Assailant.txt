<b>Distract Assailant</b>(Complete Adventurer)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Assassin 1,  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,  Hexblade 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
The subject of this spell is distracted, flinching at blows that seem to come from the shadows.
A creature affected by this spell is flat-footed until the beginning of its next turn.
Material Component: The dried wing of a fly.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
