<b>Orb of Electricity, Lesser</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 151)

Conjuration (Creation) [Electricity]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> One orb of electricity
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
<b>The air crackles around your outstretched hand and the smell of ozone assaults your nostrils as a ball of electrical energy, its surface crackling with bolts of lightning, streaks from your open palm.</b>
	
This spell functions like <b>lesser orb of acid</b>, except that it deals electricity damage.
