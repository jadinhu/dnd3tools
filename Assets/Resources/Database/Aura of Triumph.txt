<b>Aura of Triumph</b>(Tome of Battle: The Book of Nine Swords, p. 57)

Devoted Spirit (Stance) [Good]
 <b>Level:</b>  Crusader 6 (Two Devoted Spirit maneuvers),    
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You and one ally within 10 ft.
 <b>Duration:</b> Stance

 	
<b>You channel the power of good through your body and soul, infusing the area around you with a soft, golden radiance. With each blow you strike against evil, you feel invigorated and driven onward.</b>
	
While you are in this stance, you and any ally within 10 feet of you both heal 4 points of damage with each successful melee attack either of you makes against an evil target.
