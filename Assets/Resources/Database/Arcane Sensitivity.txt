<b>Arcane Sensitivity</b>(Shining South)

Divination
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> One creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
With a touch, you instantly determine if a creature can cast arcane spells and the maximum spell level it can cast.
