<b>Divine Agility</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 69)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 5,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Living creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
<b>Calling aloud on the divine power of your deity, you imbue a living creature with agility and skill in combat.</b>
	
You grant the subject a +10 enhancement bonus to Dexterity.
