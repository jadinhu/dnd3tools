<b>Instant of Power</b>(The Forge of War, p. 114)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 1,  Druid 1,  Ranger 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 immediate action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
You bestow a brief moment of power on your ally, granting a +4 enhancement bonus on his next attack roll, saving throw, or damage roll.
