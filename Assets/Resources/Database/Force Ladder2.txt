<b>Force Ladder</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 97)

Evocation [Force]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, AF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> One ladder of force up to 60 ft. long
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
<b>You concentrate on the small silver model of the ladder in your outstretched hand. A transparent ladder, visible only by its edges, appears.</b>
	
You create an immobile transparent ladder made of force. The ladder is 2 feet wide and anywhere from 10 to 60 feet long, with rungs spaced 1 foot apart. The ladder functions as a normal ladder and can support any weight. It can be destroyed by anything that destroys a wall of force.
	
<b>Focus:</b> A miniature silver ladder (50 gp).
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Magic of Faerun
