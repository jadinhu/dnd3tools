<b>Possess</b>(Oriental Adventures)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Shaman 5,    
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 round
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature

 	
As possess animal, but you can take over the body of any creature.
The possessed creature retains no memory of actions performed during the possession or knowledge of who possessed it.
Material Component: A bowl of incense.
