<b>Anyspell, Greater</b>(Forgotten Realms Campaign Setting)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>   Spell 6,   
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 15 minutes
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> You
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
