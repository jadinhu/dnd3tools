<b>Lucky Streak</b>(Complete Scoundrel)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 1,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 minute/level (D)
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
A tinkling laugh and a momentary shine surround your ally, leaving him with an aura of confidence and a sly smile.
You improve the luck of the subject creature.
For the duration of the spell, any reroll the subject makes using a luck feat gains a +2 luck bonus.
