<b>Laeral's Cutting Hand</b>(Magic of Faerun)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 1,  Wizard 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> Your hand
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level (D)

 	
You alter the structure of your hand so that it becomes as hard and sharp as a blade.
Your hand gains a +2 enhancement bonus on attack and damage when making unarmed attacks, and you inflict normal damage instead of subdual damage.
You are considered armed with this hand.
You cannot use that hand to cast spells, but can otherwise use the hand normally.
Your hand's enhancement bonus does not apply to melee touch attacks.
