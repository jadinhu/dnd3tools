<b>Exalted Fury</b>(Book of Exalted Deeds)

Evocation [Good]
 <b>Level:</b>   Sanctified 9,   
<b>Components:</b>V, Sacrifice
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 40 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> 40-ft. radius burst, centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
Uttering a single, awesomely powerful syllable of the Words of Creation, your body erupts in the same holy power that shaped the universe at its birth.
All evil creatures within the area take damage equal to your current hit points +50.
Sacrifice: You die.
You can be raised or resurrected normally.
