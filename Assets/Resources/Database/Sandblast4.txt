<b>Sandblast</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 180)

Evocation
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 10 ft.
 <b>Area:</b> 10-ft.-radius burst centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Reflex half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>Whipping your hand in a wide arc, you spray sand from your fingers in a powerful blast.</b>
	
Creatures in the area take 1d6 points of nonlethal damage. Any creature that fails its Reflex save is also stunned for 1 round. You are not harmed by the spell.
