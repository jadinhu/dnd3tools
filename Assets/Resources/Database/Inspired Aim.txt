<b>Inspired Aim</b>(Book of Exalted Deeds)

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Language-Dependent, Mind-Affecting]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 3,  Ranger 3,  Bard 4,   Fey 3,   
<b>Components:</b>V, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> 40 ft.
 <b>Target:</b> Allies with 40-ft.-radius emanation centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> Concentration
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
You inspire allies within the spell's area to focus their minds on hitting their intended targets.
All affected allies gain a +2 insight bonus on all ranged attacks.
