<b>Shadow Blink</b>(Tome of Battle: The Book of Nine Swords, p. 78)

Shadow Hand [Teleportation]
 <b>Level:</b>  Swordsage 7,    
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 swift action
 <b>Range:</b> 50 ft.
 <b>Target:</b> You

 	
<b>In the blink of an eye, you disappear and emerge from a mote of shadow energy across the battlefield.</b>
	
This maneuver functions as the shadow jaunt maneuver, except that it can be initiated as a swift action.
