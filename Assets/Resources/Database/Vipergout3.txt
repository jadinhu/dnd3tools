<b>Vipergout</b>(Savage Species)

Conjuration (Summoning)
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 7,  Wizard 7,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Effect:</b> 1d4+3 summoned creatures
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
This spell summons 1d4+3 fiendish (CE) or celestial (CG) Medium-size vipers (snakes), which leap forth from your location to attack your enemies.
If you can communicate with the vipers, you can direct them not to attack, to attack particular enemies, or to perform other actions.
When you use a summoning spell to summon an evil or good creature, it is a spell of that type.
Material Component: A snakeskin.
