<b>Spirit Ally, Greater</b>(Oriental Adventures)

Conjuration (Calling) [see text]
 <b>Level:</b>  Shaman 8,   Celestial 8,   
<b>Components:</b>
 <b>Target:</b> Up to 24 HD worth of spirits, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart when they appear

 	
As lesser spirit ally, except you may call a single spirit of up to 24 HD or a number of spirits of the same type whose HD total no more than 24.
The spirits agree to help you and request your return favor together.
