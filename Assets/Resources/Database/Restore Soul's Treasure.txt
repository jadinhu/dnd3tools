<b>Restore Soul's Treasure</b>(Book of Exalted Deeds)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>   Sanctified 8,   
<b>Components:</b>Sacrifice
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 round
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> Disintegrated remains of one item
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
If the target of this spell has lost a valuable item as the result of the spell soul's treasure lost (described in the Book of Vile Darkness), this spell restores that item.
This spell must be cast within 4 hours of the destruction of the item.
Upon completion of the casting, the disintegrated item
returns to the possession of the original owner in the condition it was in before being disintegrated.
Sacrifice: 1d4 points of Constitution drain.
