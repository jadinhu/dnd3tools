<b>Stick</b>(Underdark)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Bard 0,  Sorcerer 0,  Wizard 0,  Prime Underdark Guide 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Nonmagical, unattended object weighing up to 5 lb.
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (object)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (object)

 	
Stick affixes one item weighing up to 5 pounds to another, heavier item.
The two items can be separated with even a small amount of force, such as a wind stronger than 10 mph, a mage hand or unseen servant spell, or a move action by any corporeal creature (which provokes attacks of opportunity).
Material Component: A bit of dried glue.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
