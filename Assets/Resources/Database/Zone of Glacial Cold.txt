<b>Zone of Glacial Cold</b>(Frostburn)

Conjuration [Cold]
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 2,  Ranger 2,  Sorcerer 2,  Wizard 2,  Disciple of Thrym 2,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Effect:</b> 20-ft. radius
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 round/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude half
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
You create a zone of icy cold within the spell's area, dealing 1d6 points of cold damage per round.
Arcane Material Component: A snowball.
