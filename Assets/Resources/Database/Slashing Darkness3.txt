<b>Slashing Darkness</b>(Spell Compendium, p. 191)

Evocation
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
 <b>Effect:</b> Ray
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
<b>You complete this spell and a hissing, hurtling ribbon of pure darkness flies from your hand.</b>
	
You must succeed on a ranged touch attack with the ray to strike a target. A creature struck by this ray of negative energy takes 1d8 points of damage per two caster levels (maximum 5d8). An undead creature instead heals 1d8 points of damage per two caster levels (maximum 5d8).
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Champions of Ruin  Miniatures Handbook
