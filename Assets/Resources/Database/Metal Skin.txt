<b>Metal Skin</b>(Complete Arcane)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Wu Jen 5 (Metal),    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 min./level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
When you cast this spell, the recipient's skin toughens and gleams as if metallic, and it gains a natural armor bonus of +8.
The target becomes somewhat slow and stiff, taking a -2 penalty to Dexterity.
Material Component: A small piece of rhinoceros hide.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Oriental Adventures
