<b>Attraction</b>(Oriental Adventures)

Transmutation
 <b>Level:</b>  Shaman 1,  Sohei 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Target:</b> One creature
 <b>Duration:</b> 2 rounds/level
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes

 	
When you cast this spell, a mystical aura surrounds the subject creature, drawing attacks to the creature and making successful attacks more effective.
The subject takes an additional +1 point of damage per caster level (max +5) from each melee or ranged attack that deals damage to it.
This spell does not increase damage from spells.
