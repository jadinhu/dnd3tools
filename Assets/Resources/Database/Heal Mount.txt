<b>Heal Mount</b>(Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 239)

Conjuration (Healing)
 <b>Level:</b>  Paladin 3,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> Your mount touched
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Will negates (harmless)
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> Yes (harmless)

 	
This spell functions like <b>heal</b>, but it affects only the paladin's special mount (typically a warhorse).
