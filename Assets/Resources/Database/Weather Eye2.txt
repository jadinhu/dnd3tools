<b>Weather Eye</b>(Defenders of the Faith: A Guidebook to Clerics and Paladins)

Divination
 <b>Level:</b>  Druid 3,  Cleric 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 hour
 <b>Range:</b> 1 mile radius + 1 mile/level
 <b>Area:</b> 1 mile radius + 1 mile/level, centered on you
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
You may accurately predict the natural weather up to one week into the future.
If unnatural forces currently affect the weather, then weather eye reveals as much information as a detect magic spell.
Material Component: Incense.
Divine Focus: A scrying device of some kind (bowl, mirror, crystal ball, and so forth).
