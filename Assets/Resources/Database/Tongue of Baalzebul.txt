<b>Tongue of Baalzebul</b>(Book of Vile Darkness)

Transmutation [Evil]
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, M, Drug
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 full round
 <b>Range:</b> Personal
 <b>Target:</b> Caster
 <b>Duration:</b> 1 hour/level

 	
The caster gains the ability to lie, seduce, and beguile with a devil's skill.
He gains a +2 competence bonus on Bluff, Diplomacy, and Gather Information checks.
Material Component: A tongue from any creature capable of speech.
Drug Component:Mushroom powder.
