<b>Orb of Sound</b>(Complete Arcane)

Conjuration (Creation) [Sonic]
 <b>Level:</b>  Sorcerer 4,  Wizard 4,  Warmage 4,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 standard action
 <b>Range:</b> Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
 <b>Effect:</b> One orb of sonic energy
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> Fortitude partial
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
This spell functions like orb of acid, except that it deals 1d4 points of sonic damage per level (maximum 15d4).
In addition, a creature struck by an orb of sound must make a Fortitude save or be deafened for 1 round instead of being sickened.
 <b>Also appears in</b>   Spell Compendium
