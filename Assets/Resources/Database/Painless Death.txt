<b>Painless Death</b>(Ghostwalk, p. 56)

Necromancy
 <b>Level:</b>  Cleric 1,    
<b>Components:</b>V, S, DF, 
 <b>Casting Time:</b> 1 action
 <b>Range:</b> Touch
 <b>Target:</b> One willing living creature touched
 <b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
 <b>Saving Throw:</b> None
 <b>Spell Resistance:</b> No

 	
The touched creature dies painlessly and instantly.
